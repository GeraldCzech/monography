# /review

## Purpose

Simulate peer review for a chapter, paper, theory object or model.

## Usage

`/review <file-or-folder>`

## Instructions

1. Read `.claude/agents/reviewer-simulator.md`.
2. Review as:
   - Reviewer 1: theory and contribution
   - Reviewer 2: methods and evidence
   - Associate Editor: positioning and publishability
3. Provide actionable criticism.

## Output Sections

1. Overall assessment
2. Contribution
3. Theory
4. Method / evidence
5. Literature positioning
6. Boundary conditions
7. Writing and structure
8. Major revisions
9. Minor revisions
10. Recommendation

## Rule

Be critical and precise. Do not invent missing literature.

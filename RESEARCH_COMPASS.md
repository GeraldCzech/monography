# RESEARCH_COMPASS

BEBA explains how nonprofit brands become behaviourally effective under conditions of uncertainty.

Central chain:

Decision under uncertainty
-> Information asymmetry
-> Credence goods
-> Signalling
-> Brands
-> Brand Equity
-> Belief formation
-> Behavioural enactment
-> Resource mobilisation

The dissertation is not primarily about brands. It is about human decision-making under epistemic uncertainty and the role of nonprofit brands as institutionalised signal systems.

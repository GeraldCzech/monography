# Brand Equity

## Definition

In this monograph, brand equity is treated as the accumulated cognitive, evaluative and relational value of a nonprofit brand in the donor's mind.

## Role in BEBA

Brand equity is the bridge between observable organisational signals and behavioural decision processes. It is not the final outcome, but a mediating signal architecture.

## Key Dimensions

- Awareness / familiarity
- Image
- Personality
- Trust
- Commitment
- Authenticity
- Reputation

## Related Models

- Aaker
- Keller
- Faircloth
- Boenigk and Becker
- Rios Romero et al.

## Boundary Conditions

- Awareness alone is insufficient.
- Brand equity may be sector-specific.
- Strong brand equity can become fragile under scandal or reputational crisis.

# Epistemic Uncertainty

## Definition

Epistemic uncertainty refers to the donor's limited ability to know whether a nonprofit organisation is competent, trustworthy, effective and likely to create the intended impact.

## Role in BEBA

This is the foundational problem that makes signals, trust and brands theoretically relevant.

## Related Constructs

- Information asymmetry
- Credence goods
- Trust
- Reputation
- Brand credibility

## Boundary Conditions

- Some donors do not seek to reduce uncertainty cognitively.
- Emotional or habitual giving may bypass detailed evaluation.
- Institutional trust may reduce perceived uncertainty.

## Measurement Links

Currently conceptual. Later potentially linked to perceived transparency, trust, perceived effectiveness and donor confidence.

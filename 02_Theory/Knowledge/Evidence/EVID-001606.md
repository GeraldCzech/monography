**Classifying nonprofit organisations based on the 'primary economic activity' of the 'establishment' provides a more homogenous and rigorous basis for comparative economic analysis than classifying by the entire 'organisation' or by legal status.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Classification, Nonprofit Sector, Unit of Analysis*

**Limitations:** This method is less effective for multi-purpose organisations with no clear primary activity and does not capture an entity's secondary or tertiary functions.

**Notes:** This principle was adopted to align nonprofit sector analysis with established national income accounting methodologies.

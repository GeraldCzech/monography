**To accurately represent the global nonprofit sector, a classification system must include a distinct category for development, housing, and related community-building organisations, which are prevalent in developing countries.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Classification, Nonprofit Sector, NGOs, Developing Countries*

**Limitations:** The precise boundaries of this category can be difficult to apply consistently across different national contexts.

**Notes:** The ICNPO addresses this by creating a new major group (Group 6: "Development and Housing") not found in traditional industrial classifications.

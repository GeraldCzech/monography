**Behavioural inertia, where non-donation results from a lack of conscious thought rather than opposition, is a mechanism for non-support.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Behavioural Inertia, Cognitive Availability, Salience, Non-support*

**Limitations:** The study relies on self-reported (un)consciousness, which can be difficult for respondents to assess accurately.

**Notes:** This suggests a BEBA must not only build positive perceptions but also ensure the brand and its calls to action are sufficiently salient to overcome attentional neglect.

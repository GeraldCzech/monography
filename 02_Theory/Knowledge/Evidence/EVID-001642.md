**The explanatory power of brand image for giving intentions (R² = .31) appears substantially higher than that found for brand personality on actual donations (R² = .04-.06) in a prior study.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Brand Image, Brand Personality, Giving Intentions, Donation Behaviour*

**Limitations:** This is an indirect comparison across different studies using different independent constructs (image vs. personality) and different dependent variables (intentions vs. actual behaviour).

**Notes:** Suggests brand image may be a more powerful predictor than brand personality, but the comparison is not direct.

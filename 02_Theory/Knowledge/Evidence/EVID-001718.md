**Existing brand measurement scales are predominantly static, cross-sectional tools, with a lack of instruments designed to measure the evolution of consumer-brand relationships over time.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Brand Dynamics, Longitudinal Measurement*

**Limitations:** Cross-sectional data cannot adequately capture the causal dynamics of brand relationship development.

**Notes:** BEBA aims to model a behavioural architecture, which implies a dynamic process. This gap is therefore highly relevant.

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Psychology, Brand Outcomes, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** The model treats psychological factors as antecedents of outcomes but does not directly model the outcomes themselves.

**Notes:** Important boundary condition for applying the model to BEBA behavioural outcomes.

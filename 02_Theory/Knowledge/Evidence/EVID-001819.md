**In nonprofit service contexts with a strong member community, organizational identification is positively associated with donations, while peer identification is negatively associated with donations.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Organizational Identification, Peer Identification, Charitable Giving*

**Limitations:** This finding was demonstrated in a service-membership context and may not generalize to other nonprofit models where donor peer groups are less cohesive.

**Notes:** Challenges a monolithic view of "identification" and provides a key competing explanation for BEBA to model.

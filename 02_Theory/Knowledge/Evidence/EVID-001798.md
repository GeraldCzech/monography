**The conceptual dimensions of Employee Brand Equity (EBE) are applicable to formal volunteers in the nonprofit sector, forming a valid basis for a Volunteer Brand Equity (VBE) model.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Volunteer Brand Equity, Employee Brand Equity, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** The study is exploratory, limited to formal volunteers, and conducted only in the Portuguese cultural context.

**Notes:** This provides a foundational validation for applying internal branding models, originally from the for-profit sector, to NPO volunteers.

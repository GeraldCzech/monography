**The 4-dimension nonprofit brand image scale developed by Michel and Rieunier lacked psychometric stability when tested in a UK context.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Nonprofit Brand Image, Measurement Validity, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** The replication test was conducted in the United Kingdom and does not establish whether the original scale is unstable in all contexts.

**Notes:** This finding supports the need for context-sensitive measurement of nonprofit brand image.

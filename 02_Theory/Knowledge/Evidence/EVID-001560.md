**A negative direct effect from Brand Identity to Brand Resonance (β = -0.143) was observed when modeling all paths simultaneously in the SEM.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Identity, Brand Resonance, Mediation, Suppression Effect*

**Limitations:** The interpretation as a statistical suppression effect is a post-hoc explanation of a correlational finding.

**Notes:** The authors interpret this not as a contradiction of the theory, but as evidence for the critical importance of the mediating paths (Brand Meaning and Brand Responses) for building a positive brand relationship.

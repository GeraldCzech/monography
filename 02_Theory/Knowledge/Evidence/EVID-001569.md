**Brand credibility does not significantly moderate the relationship between brand awareness and brand equity.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Credibility, Brand Awareness, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** Unidimensional measurement of brand credibility; specific cultural context.

**Notes:** This finding contributes to a more nuanced view where credibility is not a universal amplifier but interacts specifically with certain brand dimensions like performance and commitment.

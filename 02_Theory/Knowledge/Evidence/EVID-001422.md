*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Long-Term Orientation, Brand Asset, Strategic Brand Management, Organizational Commitment*

**Limitations:** The reviewed material does not test whether organizations actually adopt or sustain this orientation.

**Notes:** This is a boundary condition for applying the model as a behavioural architecture.

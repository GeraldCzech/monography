**The relative influence of satisfaction versus identification constructs on donor loyalty is contingent on the donation context.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Donor Satisfaction, Organizational Identification, Identity Salience, Donor Loyalty*

**Limitations:** The finding is based on a comparison of only two contexts: a money-collecting charity and a blood donation service.

**Notes:** In the money donation (more abstract) context, identity constructs had a stronger total effect. In the blood donation (more personal/service-like) context, satisfaction had the strongest effect. This is a key boundary condition for BEBA.

**Externally activating a relevant identity through primes (e.g., reminding someone of their prior donor status) can increase giving.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Priming, Identity Salience, Charitable Giving*

**Limitations:** The effect depends on situational activation, not just an individual's chronic level of identity.

**Notes:** This suggests a specific mechanism for bridging the intention-behavior gap by making an identity salient at the point of decision.

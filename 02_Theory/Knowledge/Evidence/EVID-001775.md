**The relationship between a member's perception of a nonprofit's brand orientation and their perceived benefits is fully mediated by their perception of the organization's market orientation.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Perceived Brand Orientation, Perceived Market Orientation, Perceived Benefits, Mediation*

**Limitations:** Based on cross-sectional SEM data, which cannot definitively prove causality. The model is context-specific to Australian churches.

**Notes:** This is the central mechanism of the study, providing a causal pathway that aligns with the BEBA framework's "behaviour before brands" principle.

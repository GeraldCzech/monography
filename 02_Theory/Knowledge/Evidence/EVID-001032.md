**Organizational identification and identity salience are empirically distinct constructs in donor behavior.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Organizational Identification, Identity Salience, Donor Behavior*

**Limitations:** The finding is based on two nonprofit donation contexts and may not generalize to other forms of giving or stakeholder roles.

**Notes:** The distinction separates a group-level social identity construct from an individual-level role-identity construct.

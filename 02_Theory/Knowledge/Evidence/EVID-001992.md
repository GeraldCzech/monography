*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Descriptive Norms, Donation Intention*

**Limitations:** No simultaneous injunctive norm control; intention-only outcome.

Source: Lindersson, Guntell, Carlsson, & Agerström (2019). Descriptive norms increase charitable giving. International Journal of Nonprofit and Voluntary Sector Marketing.

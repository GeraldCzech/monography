**Brand commitment has a significant positive effect on donor-based brand equity in the tested NGO model.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Commitment, Donor-Based Brand Equity, Attitudinal Commitment, Emotional Commitment*

**Limitations:** The evidence comes from PLS-SEM using a convenience sample of 131 Spanish respondents focused on childhood-related NGOs.

**Notes:** Commitment is described as attitudinal and emotional rather than simple repeat behaviour.

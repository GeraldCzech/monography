**The constructs of brand awareness and brand associations, as conceptualized by Aaker (1991), lack discriminant validity in multiple national contexts.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Awareness, Brand Associations, Discriminant Validity, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** The finding applies to measurement using generic, abstract survey items; brand-specific measures might perform differently.

**Notes:** This is a core finding suggesting a fundamental measurement flaw in the widely used CBBE model.

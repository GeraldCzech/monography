**Brands with higher historical advertising budgets exhibited substantially higher levels of consumer-based brand equity, operationalized as brand awareness, brand associations, and perceived quality.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Consumer-Based Brand Equity, Advertising Investment, Brand Awareness, Brand Associations, Perceived Quality*

**Limitations:** The study demonstrates correlation but cannot definitively establish causality between advertising spending and brand equity; other factors such as distribution, sales promotions, and historical advantages were not fully controlled.

**Notes:** The study used advertising expenditure as a proxy for communication effort.

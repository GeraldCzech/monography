**Faircloth defines nonprofit brand equity as a favourable bias toward support, driven by awareness, image, and personality.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Nonprofit Brand Equity, Brand Awareness, Brand Image, Brand Personality, Support Intention*

**Limitations:** The model conceptually conflates brand equity with behavioural intentions.

**Notes:** This finding is relevant to BEBA because it represents an early transfer of commercial brand equity logic into nonprofit contexts.

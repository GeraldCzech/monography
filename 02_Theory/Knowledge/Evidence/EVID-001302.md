**For customers with low brand familiarity, satisfaction formation is strongly shaped by expectancy-disconfirmation.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Familiarity, Customer Satisfaction, Expectancy-Disconfirmation, Epistemic Uncertainty*

**Limitations:** The evidence comes from a restaurant context with Chinese consumers in Hong Kong, and brand familiarity was measured with a single self-reported item.

**Notes:** Low familiarity is relevant to BEBA as a condition of higher uncertainty in which consumers rely on cognitive evaluation and learning.

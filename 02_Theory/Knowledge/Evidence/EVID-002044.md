**Methodologies from natural sciences or medicine must be explicitly justified and adapted for use in social science contexts.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Methodology, Interdisciplinarity*

**Limitations:** Direct transfer of methods is not automatic and requires contextual adaptation.

**Notes:** This is a boundary condition for applying evidence-based medicine principles or philosophy of science models to nonprofit branding.

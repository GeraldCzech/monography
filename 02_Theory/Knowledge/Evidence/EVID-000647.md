*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Nonprofit Brand Equity, Brand Awareness, Brand Trust, Brand Commitment, Formative Measurement, Reflective Measurement*

**Limitations:** The awareness measure was dichotomous and did not allow assessment of awareness weight relative to trust and commitment.

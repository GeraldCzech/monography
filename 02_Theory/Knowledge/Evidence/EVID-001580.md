**Behavioral intentions for giving blood, time, and money are significantly predicted by both past behavior and a specific role-identity.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Behavioral Intention, Past Behavior, Role Identity, Prosocial Behavior*

**Limitations:** The study uses cross-sectional data. The dependent variable was behavioral intention, not observed behavior. The role-identity scale had low reliability (alphas .39 to .48), which may underestimate the relationship strength.

**Notes:** This confirms the core predictive relationships of the proposed model.

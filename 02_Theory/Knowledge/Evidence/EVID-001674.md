**Potential volunteers can be categorised into distinct typologies based on their dominant brand decision-making process.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Volunteer Typology, Decision-making, Brand Discovery*

**Limitations:** The proposed typologies require quantitative validation and were derived from a sample of formal, service-delivery volunteers in the UK.

**Notes:** The six typologies are Leader-lover, Personally connected, Novice, Role-seeker, Maximiser, and Satisficer.

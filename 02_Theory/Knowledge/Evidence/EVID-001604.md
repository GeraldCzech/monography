**Highly detailed, nationally-specific taxonomies, such as the US NTEE, are not suitable for international comparison due to their complexity, cultural specificity, and lack of economy and organising power.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Classification, Nonprofit Sector, Comparative Research*

**Limitations:** Such systems may be superior for purely national studies where a high level of detail is necessary and culturally relevant.

**Notes:** This finding illustrates the analytical trade-off between detail (combinatorial richness) and cross-national applicability.

**Strategic communication that provides a plausible rationale for a low-fit sponsorship ("created fit") can mitigate the negative effects on brand equity and produce positive outcomes comparable to a high-fit partnership.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Communication, Sponsor Fit, Brand Equity, Created Fit*

**Limitations:** The efficacy of "created fit" depends on the nonprofit's ability to control a plausible narrative. The long-term persistence of this effect was not examined.

**Notes:** This demonstrates that communication is a key architectural tool for managing and repairing the coherence of the BEBA, making it more resilient.

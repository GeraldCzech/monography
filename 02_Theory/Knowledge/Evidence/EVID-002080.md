**Monetary non-donors are not a homogeneous group and can be empirically segmented into distinct clusters.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Stakeholder Segmentation, Non-support, Behavioural Heterogeneity*

**Limitations:** Findings are based on a German sample and may not generalize to other philanthropic cultures; data is self-reported and cross-sectional.

**Notes:** This finding challenges one-size-fits-all fundraising strategies and supports BEBA's premise that different behavioural architectures are needed for different stakeholder segments.

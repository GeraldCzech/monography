*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Brand Awareness, Brand Associations, Discriminant Validity*

**Limitations:** This finding may be specific to the measurement instrument used or the consumer goods context.

**Notes:** The authors found a very high correlation (0.80 to 0.90) between the factors. This empirical collapse is a critical insight for BEBA's ontology, suggesting that these two concepts may function as a single cognitive step for stakeholders.

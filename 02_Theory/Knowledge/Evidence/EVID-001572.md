**"Professional" NPOs are more likely to leverage public funding sources, whereas "nonprofessional" NPOs tend to focus more on individual and corporate donors.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: NPO Professionalism, Fundraising, Resource Acquisition*

**Limitations:** The classification of NPOs as "professional" or "nonprofessional" is based on self-identification, not objective criteria like budget or staff size.

**Notes:** Highlights that internal capacity (professionalization) is associated with the ability to access specific, more complex funding streams.

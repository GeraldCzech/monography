**The "Social Competence and Trust" dimension is the most prominent and encompassing factor of nonprofit brand personality.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Personality, Nonprofit Brand, Social Competence, Trust*

**Limitations:** This prominence is established within the specific German cultural context and for the donor stakeholder group.

**Notes:** This dimension integrates traits from Aaker's Sincerity and Competence dimensions, but also adds new NPO-specific traits. It is a candidate for a core construct in the BEBA ontology.

**An individual's habitus, shaped by social background, is theorized to condition their dispositions toward giving, making certain donation patterns feel "natural" or intuitive.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Habitus, Individual Giving, Charitable Giving*

**Limitations:** The reviewed literature indicates this is a conceptually consistent proposition but lacks substantial direct empirical evidence in donation contexts.

**Notes:** This aligns with the BEBA concept of a "Behaviour Architecture" that resonates with ingrained dispositions.

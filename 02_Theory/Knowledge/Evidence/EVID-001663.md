**Sociodemographic, psychographic, and health-related variables explain only a small portion of the variance in donation behavior.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Prediction, Donation Behavior, Donor Profile*

**Limitations:** The study's dataset (SOEP) lacks direct measures of donation motives (e.g., altruism, warm glow), which likely accounts for a significant portion of the unexplained variance.

**Notes:** This is a critical boundary condition, indicating that BEBA models must incorporate motivational and psychological constructs beyond demographics to achieve explanatory power.

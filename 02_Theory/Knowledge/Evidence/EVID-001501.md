**A four-dimensional model of consumer-based brand equity (CBBE) consisting of Brand Awareness, Brand Associations, Perceived Quality, and Brand Loyalty provides a good fit to consumer data.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Brand Equity, Brand Awareness, Brand Associations, Perceived Quality, Brand Loyalty*

**Limitations:** The study was conducted in a single Australian city with a non-probability mall-intercept sample, and was limited to two high-involvement product categories (cars and televisions).

**Notes:** This finding aligns with the foundational conceptual models of Aaker (1991) and Keller (1993).

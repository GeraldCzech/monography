**Principles from a for-profit brand management framework can be successfully adapted to create a parsimonious and psychometrically sound measurement model for the nonprofit sector.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Management, For-Profit, Nonprofit, Adaptation, Measurement*

**Limitations:** The adaptation involved a significant reduction from Keller's (2000) 37-item tool to a 12-item scale, suggesting that a direct transfer is inappropriate and many for-profit nuances are not captured.

**Notes:** This validates the BEBA project's approach of adapting established branding concepts rather than assuming a direct, one-to-one transfer from the commercial sector.

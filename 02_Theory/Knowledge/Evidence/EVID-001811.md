**The link from donor loyalty intentions to actual donation behavior is weak.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Donor Loyalty, Donation Behavior*

**Limitations:** Behavior was self-reported. The link was non-significant for blood donations, potentially due to a ceiling effect. The model's explained variance for behavior (R²) was very low (.04 and .05).

**Notes:** This highlights a significant loyalty-behavior gap, which is a core problem for the BEBA framework to address.

**A higher perceived level of an NPO's "Social Competence and Trust" is significantly and positively correlated with an individual's willingness to donate.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Social Competence, Trust, Brand Personality, Willingness to Donate, Donor Behaviour*

**Limitations:** The evidence is correlational, not causal. The study measured behavioural intention, not actual donation behaviour.

**Notes:** This directly links a specific dimension of brand perception to behavioural intent, providing a core mechanism for the BEBA model where brand architecture enables behaviour.

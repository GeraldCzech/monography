**The process of internal branding can be explained by Self-Determination Theory as a mechanism of motivation internalization, progressing from external regulation (brand behaviours) to integrated regulation (emotional attachment) to intrinsic motivation (service involvement).**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Self-Determination Theory, Internalization, Brand Orientation, Emotional Attachment, Service Involvement*

**Limitations:** This is a theoretical model tested with correlational data; it does not directly measure the psychological process of internalization over time.

**Notes:** Provides a strong psychological theory to explain *how* a Brand-Enabled Behaviour Architecture functions at the individual level.

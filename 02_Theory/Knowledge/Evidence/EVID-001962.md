**After the 2008 financial crisis, Austria's nonprofit social-services sector experienced persistently slower growth and rising market concentration.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Crisis Effects, Sector Concentration, Economic Growth, Resilience*

**Limitations:** Evidence for concentration is specific to the social services sub-sector and not demonstrated to be a sector-wide phenomenon.

**Notes:** This finding shows that the sector's resilience during the crisis had negative long-term consequences.

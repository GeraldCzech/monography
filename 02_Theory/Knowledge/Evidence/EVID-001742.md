**The relationship between an NPO's brand orientation behaviour and its organizational performance is not direct, but is serially mediated through staff emotional brand attachment and staff service involvement.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Orientation, Emotional Attachment, Service Involvement, Organizational Performance, Mediation*

**Limitations:** The cross-sectional research design establishes correlation but cannot definitively prove causality. The findings are specific to the UK nonprofit sector.

**Notes:** This finding provides an empirically tested internal mechanism for the BEBA framework, detailing how strategic inputs are converted into behavioural outcomes.

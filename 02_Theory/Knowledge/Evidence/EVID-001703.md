**A three-dimensional brand personality structure for nonprofit organisations emerged from an exploratory factor analysis in the German context.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Personality, Nonprofit Brand, Social Competence, Trust*

**Limitations:** The scale was developed and validated only in Germany; the factor structure may be culture-specific. The study focused on large, well-known NPOs.

**Notes:** The three dimensions are (1) Social Competence and Trust, (2) Emotion and Assertiveness, and (3) Sophistication and Exclusivity.

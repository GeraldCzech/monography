**Top-of-mind awareness, unaided awareness, and aided awareness represent a hierarchy of brand-name retrieval difficulty.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Awareness, Mental Availability, Memory Retrieval, Salience*

**Limitations:** The empirical evidence discussed is drawn from whisky brands in the UK, Greece, and Taiwan.

**Notes:** Aided awareness is described as the easiest retrieval task, unaided awareness as more difficult, and top-of-mind awareness as the most difficult.

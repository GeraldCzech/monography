**The direct relationship between donor-perceived service quality and a charity brand's overall brand equity was found to be non-significant.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Perceived Quality, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** Finding is highly context-specific due to the religious and obligatory nature of giving (Zakat) in the studied sample, which may override service quality evaluations. May not generalize to secular or individualistic giving contexts.

**Notes:** This challenges a core tenet of Aaker's (1991) model and suggests for BEBA a need to distinguish `Operational Quality` from `Mission Credibility`.

**The reviewed literature reports a robust and consistent positive relationship between key nonprofit brand equity dimensions and supportive behaviors, particularly intentions to donate money and time.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Nonprofit Brand Equity, Supportive Behavior, Donation Intention, Time Donation Intention*

**Limitations:** The reviewed studies mostly use donation intention rather than verified donation behavior, and the review notes that many studies are cross-sectional and correlational.

**Notes:** This finding is directly relevant to BEBA because it links brand architecture components to supportive behavior.

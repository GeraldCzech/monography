**The Interaction, Orchestration, and Affect dimensions of NBO are all significant, positive predictors of a nonprofit's ability to achieve its short- and long-term goals.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Interaction, Orchestration, Affect, Goal Achievement, Organizational Performance*

**Limitations:** The finding relies on self-reported data from a managerial perspective and from a single cultural context (Australia).

**Notes:** Unlike stakeholder value, goal achievement is associated with all three NBO dimensions, including the understanding of stakeholder affect.

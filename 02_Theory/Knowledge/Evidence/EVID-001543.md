**Brand trust acts as a partial, not full, mediator in the relationship between overall satisfaction and brand loyalty.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Trust, Overall Satisfaction, Brand Loyalty, Mediation*

**Notes:** This finding is a key point of nuance, as a direct effect from satisfaction to loyalty remains significant even when trust is included in the model. It challenges a strict interpretation of commitment-trust theory where trust would be a full mediator.

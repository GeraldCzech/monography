**Low brand recognition is a primary marketing challenge for small NPOs, limiting their ability to engage stakeholders.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Brand Recognition, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** The study is exploratory and geographically limited to Michigan NPOs. "Brand recognition" was a self-reported challenge.

**Notes:** This directly links a key practitioner problem to the concept of brand equity. BEBA can frame brand equity as the capacity of the architecture to efficiently enable stakeholder behaviours, thereby overcoming this challenge.

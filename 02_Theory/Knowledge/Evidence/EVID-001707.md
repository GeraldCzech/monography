**The brand personality profiles of nonprofit organisations differ significantly from those of for-profit organisations.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Personality, Nonprofit Brand*

**Limitations:** The comparison was illustrative (NPOs vs. Coke and BMW) rather than a systematic, representative comparison of the two sectors.

**Notes:** NPOs were perceived as being substantially higher on traits related to social competence and trustworthiness, reinforcing the idea of a fundamentally different brand equity logic.

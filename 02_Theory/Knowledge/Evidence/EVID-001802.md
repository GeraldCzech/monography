**A direct, unaltered application of employee-centric brand models to volunteers is inadequate; concepts require contextual adaptation (e.g., 'job training' becomes 'personal development', 'goals' become 'values').**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Volunteer Management, Employee Management*

**Limitations:** This finding is based on qualitative expert interviews rather than a quantitative comparative study.

**Notes:** This nuances the primary finding, indicating that while the EBE *structure* is portable, the *content* must be adapted to the specific motivations and psychological contract of volunteers.

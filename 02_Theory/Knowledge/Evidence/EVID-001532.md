**Nonprofit Brand Orientation (NBO) is a higher-order construct that can be reliably and validly measured via a 12-item scale (NBOS) with three first-order dimensions: Interaction, Orchestration, and Affect.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Nonprofit Brand Orientation, Measurement, Scale Development, Interaction, Orchestration, Affect*

**Limitations:** The scale was developed and validated exclusively with Australian NPOs, so its cross-cultural generalizability is unknown.

**Notes:** The NBOS provides a potential tool for operationalizing the internal organizational architecture component of BEBA.

**In a largely secular societal context like Germany, an explicit religious mission is negatively associated with trust from the general public.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Trust, Brand Signals, Mission, Contextual Fit*

**Limitations:** Highly context-specific due to the German cultural landscape and contemporary church scandals. The study did not test for interaction effects (e.g., religious individuals' trust in religious NPOs).

**Notes:** This highlights the critical BEBA concept of "contextual fit," where a brand signal's valence depends on the socio-cultural environment.

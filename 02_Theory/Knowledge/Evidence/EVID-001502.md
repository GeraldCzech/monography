**Brand Awareness and Brand Associations are empirically distinct and separable constructs within a CBBE model.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Brand Awareness, Brand Associations, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** The measurement of Brand Awareness used a single parcelled indicator in the CFA due to the use of dichotomous scale items, which is a methodological limitation.

**Notes:** This directly challenges the findings of earlier studies that found the two dimensions to be inseparable.

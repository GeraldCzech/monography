**Fundraising appeals framed around donor-centric benefits like warm glow are more effective at increasing donation likelihood and amount than recipient-centric appeals.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Warm Glow Giving, Fundraising Appeals, Donation Behaviour*

**Limitations:** The finding is about the average effect; recipient-benefit appeals may be effective in specific contexts.

**Notes:** A field experiment found that self-benefit or warm-glow appeals increased giving likelihood by 6.6% and donation amount by 23%.

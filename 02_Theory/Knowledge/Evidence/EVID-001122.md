**A three-factor structure of brand associations comprising perceived value, brand personality, and organisational associations provides a better fit than a uni-dimensional model.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Associations, Perceived Value, Brand Personality, Organisational Associations, Consumer-Based Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** The finding was established for strong, mature consumer brands in four product categories and not for nonprofit brands.

**Notes:** Organisational associations are especially relevant for BEBA because nonprofit brand equity may depend strongly on organisational trust, credibility, and liking.

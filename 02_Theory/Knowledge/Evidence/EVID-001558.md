**Brand Resonance with a nonprofit health brand is a significant positive predictor of stakeholder intentions to adopt the promoted health behaviour (β = 0.315, p < .001).**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Resonance, Behavioural Intentions, Social Marketing*

**Limitations:** The outcome is self-reported behavioural intention, not objectively measured behaviour, which is subject to the intention-behaviour gap.

**Notes:** This finding provides a crucial link between the brand-building process and behavioural outcomes, supporting a core premise of the BEBA project.

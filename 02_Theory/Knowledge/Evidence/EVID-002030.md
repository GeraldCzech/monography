**Strict "reproductionist" interpretations of habitus are challenged by evidence from adjacent fields that cultural capital can be acquired to facilitate social mobility.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Habitus, Cultural Capital, Social Mobility*

**Limitations:** The supporting evidence comes from adjacent fields like education, not directly from philanthropy research.

**Notes:** This suggests that while habitus structures behaviour, it is not entirely deterministic, allowing for agency and change.

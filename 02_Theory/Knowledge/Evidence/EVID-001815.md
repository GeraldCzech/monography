**A meta-analysis found a medium-sized positive association between social identification and charitable giving.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Social Identity, Charitable Giving, Organizational Identification*

**Limitations:** The association is weaker for objectively measured behavior than for self-reported intentions.

**Notes:** The effect is present for identification with donors, beneficiaries, and fundraisers, indicating the target of identification matters.

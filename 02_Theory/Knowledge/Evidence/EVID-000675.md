**The NGO brand equity findings may not generalize to the broader donor population because the sample was non-representative and skewed toward younger and highly educated respondents.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Sampling, Generalizability, Donor-Based Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** The study used a convenience sample of 131 respondents, with 50.4% aged 18–24 and a skew toward highly educated respondents.

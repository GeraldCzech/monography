**The relevance of cognitive-intentional models (like TPB) is highest for novel behaviours, while habitus-driven models are better suited for explaining established, routine behaviours.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Habitus, Theory of Planned Behaviour, Routine, Dual-Process Model*

**Limitations:** There is a lack of empirical studies that test these competing or complementary explanations within a single statistical model.

**Notes:** This suggests a "dual-process" architecture where both theories are valid under different conditions.

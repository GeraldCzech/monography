**Low perceived fit between a nonprofit and a for-profit sponsor weakens or dilutes the nonprofit's brand equity, negatively influencing brand identity, meaning, response, and relationships.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Sponsor Fit, Brand Equity, Brand Dilution, Identity, Meaning, Response, Relationships*

**Limitations:** The study used highly familiar and well-liked brands. The results are based on a single exposure in an experimental setting.

**Notes:** This finding implies that a low-fit partnership introduces structural incoherence into the BEBA, potentially damaging its behavioural effectiveness.

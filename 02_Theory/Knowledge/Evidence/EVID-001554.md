**The hierarchical structure of Keller's brand equity pyramid (Identity → Meaning → Responses → Resonance) is a valid model for nonprofit, health-promotion organizations.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Equity, Brand Identity, Brand Meaning, Brand Responses, Brand Resonance, Nonprofit Branding*

**Limitations:** The finding is based on a cross-sectional study of a single, well-established national nonprofit in Canada, which limits causal claims and generalizability.

**Notes:** This extends the boundary conditions of Keller's (2001, 2003) model from the commercial to the nonprofit social marketing context.

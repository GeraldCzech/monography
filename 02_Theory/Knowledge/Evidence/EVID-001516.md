**The most significant barriers to implementing marketing for small, local NPOs are internal resource constraints, specifically a lack of money, time, and expertise.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Resource Constraints, Marketing Strategy*

**Limitations:** Based on an exploratory study focused on smaller, local NPOs in Michigan. The low survey response rate limits generalizability.

**Notes:** This finding establishes resource scarcity as a critical boundary condition, implying that a viable BEBA model must be resource-efficient and implementable by non-specialists.

**NPO practitioners often define marketing narrowly, frequently conflating it with fundraising.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Marketing Strategy, Fundraising, Stakeholder Management*

**Limitations:** Based on an exploratory study with a non-random interview sample (n=43) and a low-response survey (3.8%) in Michigan during 2006-2007.

**Notes:** This observed confusion validates the need for the BEBA framework to provide a clear ontology that defines and situates distinct functions like marketing and fundraising within a broader architecture.

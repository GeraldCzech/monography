**A donor's perception that a donation will benefit their family has a significant positive effect on giving intention, but not on actual giving behavior.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Familial Benefit, Giving Intention, Giving Behavior, Intention-Behavior Gap*

**Limitations:** Based on a 2008 survey of a young, highly-educated, online sample in China; uses self-reported data.

**Notes:** Similar to brand equity, the effect of perceived familial benefit attenuates at the intention-behavior link.

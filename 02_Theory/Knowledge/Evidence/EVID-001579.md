**An identity theory model, originally developed to explain blood donation, generalizes well to explaining volunteering time and donating money.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Role Identity, Prosocial Behavior, Volunteering, Donation, Blood Donation*

**Limitations:** The study uses cross-sectional data, preventing definitive causal proof for time and money donations. Donations to religious and political organizations were excluded from the scope.

**Notes:** This is the central finding of the study, confirming the model's applicability across different forms of institutionalized helping.

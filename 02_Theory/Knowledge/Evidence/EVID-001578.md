**NPOs in the Czech Republic have a very high dependency on funding from state and public administration (reported at 65%).**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Fundraising, Resource Acquisition*

**Limitations:** The data is from 2013. The finding is specific to the institutional context of the Czech Republic and may not be generalizable.

**Notes:** This is a critical boundary condition that helps explain the observed focus on public funding sources among "professional" NPOs in the primary study.

**The Brand Associations dimension can be effectively measured by including items related to brand personality and organizational associations.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Associations, Brand Personality, Organizational Associations*

**Limitations:** The measurement did not comprehensively include other types of associations like attributes, benefits, and attitudes as suggested by Keller (1993).

**Notes:** This enriches the measurement of Brand Associations beyond simple attribute-based items.

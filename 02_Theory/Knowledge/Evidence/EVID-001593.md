**The public display of a recognized, third-party accountability certification is positively associated with public trust in a nonprofit organization.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Trust, Accountability, Brand Signals*

**Limitations:** The effect is specific to the DZI Seal-of-Approval in Germany and may not generalize to other certifications or cultural contexts.

**Notes:** Empirically validates the BEBA premise that brands can reduce stakeholder uncertainty via credible signals.

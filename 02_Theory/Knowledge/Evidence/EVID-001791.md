**The relationship between public perceptions of nonprofits follows a hierarchy of effects, where cognitive states like awareness are a necessary precursor to affective states like confidence.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Hierarchy of Effects, Nonprofit Awareness, Public Confidence, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** The study empirically tests only the link from the cognitive to the affective stage, not the subsequent link to conative (behavioural) outcomes.

**Notes:** This provides a core theoretical mechanism for how nonprofit brands build equity and influence stakeholders.

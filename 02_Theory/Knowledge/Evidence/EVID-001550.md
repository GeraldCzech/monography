**The non-profit brand image scale developed by Michaelidou, Micevski, and Cadogan (2015) fails to achieve scalar invariance across the UK, India, and Bosnia and Herzegovina.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Non-profit Brand Image, Measurement Invariance, Culture*

**Limitations:** The study does not empirically test the reasons for the lack of scalar invariance.

**Notes:** This implies that mean scores of non-profit brand image cannot be meaningfully compared across these countries using this scale, as item intercepts differ.

**A key emerging critique is that mainstream research often treats intention as a stable trait, whereas it likely fluctuates dynamically within individuals over time.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Dynamic Models, Within-Person Variation, Intention*

**Limitations:** This dynamic view is not yet fully integrated into mainstream empirical research.

**Notes:** This critique calls for more within-person research designs like Ecological Momentary Assessment.

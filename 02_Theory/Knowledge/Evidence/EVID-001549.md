**The factor loadings for the items of the non-profit brand image scale are equivalent across the UK, India, and Bosnia and Herzegovina.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Non-profit Brand Image, Measurement Invariance, Culture*

**Limitations:** The finding is specific to the three countries studied and the specific well-known charities used as stimuli.

**Notes:** This evidence establishes metric invariance, suggesting a shared meaning of the scale items relative to the latent construct.

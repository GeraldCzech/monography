**An organization's level of Nonprofit Brand Orientation is significantly and positively associated with its self-reported overall performance.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Nonprofit Brand Orientation, Organizational Performance*

**Limitations:** The study design is cross-sectional and correlational, so causality cannot be inferred. Performance measures were based on managerial self-reports.

**Notes:** This provides primary empirical support for the core BEBA thesis that a brand architecture enables behavioural effectiveness.

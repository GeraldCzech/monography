**Standard industrial classifications, such as ISIC and NACE, are inadequate for detailed nonprofit sector research because they collapse diverse nonprofit activities into a few broad, residual categories.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Classification, Nonprofit Sector, Heterogeneity*

**Limitations:** The critique is specific to the goal of nonprofit sector analysis; these systems may be fit for their original purpose of broader industrial classification.

**Notes:** This inadequacy is framed as a lack of 'significance' and 'combinatorial richness' for the specific research purpose.

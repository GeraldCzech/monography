**In cause-related marketing, very large donation sizes can cool or reverse the warm-glow effect by triggering consumer skepticism about firm motives.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Warm Glow Giving, Cause-Related Marketing, Consumer Skepticism, Donation Size, Brand Authenticity*

**Limitations:** The reviewed material identifies this boundary condition in commercial cause-related marketing contexts.

**Notes:** This finding indicates that warm glow can depend on perceived authenticity and motive attribution.

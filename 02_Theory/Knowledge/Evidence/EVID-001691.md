**Organizational identification exerts a strong, direct, and positive effect on donor loyalty.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Organizational Identification, Donor Loyalty*

**Limitations:** The study used an adapted, non-standard scale for organizational identification. The finding's generalizability beyond the specific NPO context is not established.

**Notes:** The effect of identification on loyalty was found to be comparable in magnitude to the direct effect of satisfaction, supporting its role as a key parallel mechanism in a BEBA.

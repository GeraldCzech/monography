**The reasons for non-donation can be reduced to four underlying factors: Negative Attitude Toward Donating, Perceived Financial Inability, Past Donation Experience and Unconsciousness, and Solicitation.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Attitude, Perceived Behavioural Control, Behavioural Inertia, Solicitation*

**Limitations:** The "Solicitation" factor was statistically weak, indicating a need for more robust measurement.

**Notes:** These four factors can be interpreted as four primary barriers or failure modes that a Brand-Enabled Behaviour Architecture must overcome.

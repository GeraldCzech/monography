*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Brand Equity, Sponsor Fit, Brand Dilution*

**Limitations:** Damage is measured in terms of brand perceptions and behavioral intentions, not long-term financial impact or donor retention.

**Notes:** Directly addresses the BEBA project's core question by highlighting the trade-off between reducing financial uncertainty and increasing brand uncertainty.

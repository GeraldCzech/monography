**A triadic model suggests that donor attitudes toward beneficiaries should be considered alongside donor attitudes toward nonprofit organisations.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Donor-Beneficiary-Organisation Triad, Beneficiary Perceptions, Brand Equity, Donor Attitudes*

**Limitations:** The reviewed material presents this as a future direction and does not report direct empirical testing in the Austrian study.

**Notes:** Relevant to BEBA because it implies that brand-enabled behaviour may depend on donor, organisation, and beneficiary relationships.

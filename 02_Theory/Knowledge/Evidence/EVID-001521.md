**Many small, service-oriented NPOs in the study sample did not struggle to find clients.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Stakeholder Management, Marketing Strategy*

**Limitations:** This finding may be specific to the sample of service-oriented NPOs in Michigan and contrasts with literature focused on other NPO types (e.g., arts).

**Notes:** This suggests a potential misalignment between academic research focus and practitioner priorities, and implies that for many NPOs, the BEBA's primary utility may be in enabling volunteer and donor behaviours rather than client acquisition.

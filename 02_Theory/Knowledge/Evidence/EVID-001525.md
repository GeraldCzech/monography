**A 10-item Multidimensional Brand Equity (MBE) scale is a reliable, valid, and parsimonious instrument for measuring brand equity.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Brand Equity, Scale, Measurement*

**Limitations:** The scale's validity is not established for non-student populations, services, industrial goods, or cultures beyond the US and South Korea.

**Notes:** The scale consists of 3 loyalty items, 2 perceived quality items, and 5 awareness/associations items.

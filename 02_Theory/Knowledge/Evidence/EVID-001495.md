**Aaker's (1991) four-dimensional model of consumer-based brand equity (CBBE) does not demonstrate measurement invariance when tested on real consumers across the UK, Germany, and Greece.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Equity, Measurement Invariance, Brand Awareness, Brand Associations, Perceived Quality, Brand Loyalty*

**Limitations:** The finding is specific to the three European countries studied and may have been influenced by the socio-economic context of the European debt crisis.

**Notes:** This finding challenges prior research that had found the model to be invariant.

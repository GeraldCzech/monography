**The development of a blood donor role-identity is more dependent on feelings of moral obligation (personal norms) compared to volunteer or financial donor role-identities.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Personal Norms, Role Identity, Blood Donation*

**Limitations:** The finding is based on regression analysis of cross-sectional survey data and is subject to the low reliability of the identity measure.

**Notes:** Highlights that different internal motivations may drive identity formation for different helping behaviors.

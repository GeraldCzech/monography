**In one national study, familiarity was positively associated with the propensity to donate, while trustworthiness was not a significant factor.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Familiarity, Trustworthiness, Donation Propensity, Trust*

**Limitations:** The reviewed material suggests the discrepancy could be due to methodological differences, sample characteristics, or the specific measurement of trustworthiness.

**Notes:** This finding qualifies the claim that trust is always a central predictor of donation intention.

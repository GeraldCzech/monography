**Most brand-related research on giving is from Anglo-American, Turkish, or Chinese contexts, and relies heavily on self-reported intention rather than observed behaviour.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Generalizability, Behavioural Intention, Actual Behaviour*

**Limitations:** The findings may not be generalizable to other cultural contexts (e.g., European welfare states), and the intention-behaviour gap is not addressed.

**Notes:** This is a key boundary condition for BEBA and highlights the need for research on observed behaviour in its target context.

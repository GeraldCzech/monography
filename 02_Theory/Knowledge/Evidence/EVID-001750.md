**The public uses two dominant, contrasting mental models to perceive and judge humanitarian NGOs: "The Good Samaritan" and "The Marketer".**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Archetype, Mental Models, Public Perception*

**Limitations:** The study is qualitative and UK-specific; it does not quantify the prevalence of these models in the population. The focus is on humanitarian and international development NGOs.

**Notes:** These models act as cognitive heuristics, providing a moral and operational shorthand for judgment.

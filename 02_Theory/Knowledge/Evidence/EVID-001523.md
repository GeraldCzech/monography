**Consumer-based brand equity can be operationalized as a three-dimensional construct comprising brand loyalty, perceived quality, and a combined brand awareness/associations factor.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Brand Equity, Brand Loyalty, Perceived Quality, Brand Awareness, Brand Associations*

**Limitations:** The study was conducted on student populations and limited product categories (athletic shoes, camera film, color TVs).

**Notes:** This dimensional structure provides a validated starting point for operationalizing brand equity in a nonprofit context.

**The proposed International Classification of Nonprofit Organizations (ICNPO) provides a robust and workable framework for producing comparable data on the nonprofit sector across diverse national contexts.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Classification, Nonprofit Sector, Comparative Research, ICNPO*

**Limitations:** The paper acknowledges that large-scale empirical testing of the ICNPO's validity, reliability, and rigour across a wide range of countries remained a research gap at the time of publication.

**Notes:** The claim is primarily based on the theoretical design of the ICNPO and its initial application in a limited number of countries.

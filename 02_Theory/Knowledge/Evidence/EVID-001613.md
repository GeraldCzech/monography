**The relationship between a donor's perception of an NPO's operational efficiency and their giving intention or behavior is not significantly positive.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Perceptual Efficiency, Giving Intention, Giving Behavior*

**Limitations:** The study reports a non-significant negative coefficient. The research is based on a 2008 survey of a young, highly-educated, online sample in China; uses self-reported data.

**Notes:** This finding is counter-intuitive and contradicts the common assumption that donors always prefer NPOs with low overheads. It suggests a potential "efficiency paradox".

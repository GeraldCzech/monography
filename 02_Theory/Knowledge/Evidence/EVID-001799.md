**Internal brand management practices are antecedents to volunteer Role Clarity and Brand Commitment, which in turn mediate the relationship with positive behavioral outcomes like Brand Citizenship Behavior and Positive Word-of-Mouth.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Internal Brand Management, Role Clarity, Brand Commitment, Brand Citizenship Behavior, Positive Word-of-Mouth*

**Limitations:** The study used EFA and correlations; the full causal chain has not been tested with confirmatory structural equation modeling (SEM).

**Notes:** This mechanism can be seen as a specific implementation of a BEBA, detailing the link from organizational actions to internal states and then to behavior.

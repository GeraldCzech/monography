**Perceptions of demonstrable (self-oriented) and emotional benefits do not have a significant effect on either giving intention or giving behavior.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Demonstrable Benefit, Emotional Benefit, Giving Intention, Giving Behavior*

**Limitations:** Based on a 2008 survey of a young, highly-educated, online sample in China; uses self-reported data.

**Notes:** This challenges social exchange theories that emphasize direct or emotional returns for donors. The authors suggest this may be a culturally specific finding for Chinese donors.

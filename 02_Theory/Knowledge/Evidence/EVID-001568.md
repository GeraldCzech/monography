**High brand credibility establishes a positive relationship between perceived quality and brand equity, a relationship that is otherwise non-significant.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Credibility, Perceived Quality, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** Brand credibility was measured as a unidimensional construct. The study's context is specific to Pakistani Muslim donors.

**Notes:** This implies that credibility acts as a crucial signal that validates quality perceptions, making them relevant to brand equity only when the brand itself is believable.

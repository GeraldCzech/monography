**The interchangeability of brand awareness measures breaks down over time for brands at the high and low ends of the market-share spectrum.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Awareness, Measurement Interchangeability, Market Share, Longitudinal Measurement*

**Limitations:** The finding is based on longitudinal data from one product category and should be tested in other categories.

**Notes:** This challenges the practical implication attributed to Laurent et al., 1995, that awareness measures are interchangeable after mathematical transformation.

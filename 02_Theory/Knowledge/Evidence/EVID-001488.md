**A statistically workable and similar structure for brand equity, with Brand Awareness and Brand Image as antecedents, can be established for both internal (recruits) and external (LSPs) stakeholders of a nonprofit.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Equity, Brand Awareness, Brand Image, Stakeholders*

**Notes:** This finding is presented as evidence for the co-creation of brand equity.

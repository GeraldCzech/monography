**Public or observable donations increase giving because reputational concerns and social pressure affect donor behaviour.**

*Strength: strong*

*Concepts: Reputation, Social Pressure, Observability, Charitable Giving*

**Limitations:** The effect is amplified when observers are part of the donor’s social network, when the solicitor is liked or high status, and in smaller, less anonymous groups.

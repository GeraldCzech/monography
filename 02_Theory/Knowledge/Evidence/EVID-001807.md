**Donor-nonprofit organizational identification is a causal antecedent to a donor's identity salience.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Organizational Identification, Identity Salience*

**Limitations:** The effect size of the causal direction difference was small to non-existent across the two samples. The study's initial hypothesis was in the opposite direction.

**Notes:** This suggests a causal flow from social group identification to the internalization of that identity into an individual's self-concept.

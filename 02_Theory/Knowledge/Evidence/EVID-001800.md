**A volunteer's Brand Commitment is strongly and positively correlated with all measured VBE benefits, particularly with Positive Volunteer Word-of-Mouth (r = .772) and Brand Citizenship Behavior (r = .714).**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Commitment, Positive Word-of-Mouth, Brand Citizenship Behavior*

**Limitations:** The evidence is correlational and cross-sectional, so causality cannot be definitively inferred.

**Notes:** This highlights the affective component (Brand Commitment) as a critical driver of the behavioral outcomes sought by the brand architecture.

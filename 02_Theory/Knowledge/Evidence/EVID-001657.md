**The population of nonprofit donors can be segmented into distinct profiles based on their past behavior of donating blood, money, time, or combinations thereof.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Behavioral Segmentation, Pro-social Behavior, Donation Behavior*

**Limitations:** The study is specific to the German population. The model's overall explanatory power is low (pseudo-R² = .06), making it suitable for profiling but not strong prediction.

**Notes:** This is the central finding of the study and provides empirical validation for the "Behaviour Architecture" concept in BEBA.

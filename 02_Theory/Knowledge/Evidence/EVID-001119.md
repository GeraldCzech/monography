**Buil, de Chernatony, and Martínez conceptualise consumer-based brand equity as a multi-dimensional construct comprising brand awareness, perceived quality, brand associations, and brand loyalty.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Consumer-Based Brand Equity, Brand Awareness, Perceived Quality, Brand Associations, Brand Loyalty*

**Limitations:** The construct was tested in consumer product categories and not in services, B2B contexts, or the nonprofit sector.

**Notes:** Provides a potential conceptual starting point for nonprofit brand equity, but adaptation is required.

**Public awareness of nonprofit organizations is the most significant predictor of public confidence in their ability to provide quality services and spend money wisely.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Nonprofit Awareness, Public Confidence, Trust*

**Limitations:** The study is based on survey data from a single US county (San Diego) collected in 2007-2008. Awareness was measured as unaided "top-of-mind" recall.

**Notes:** This is the central finding of the study, empirically validating the link between the cognitive (awareness) and affective (confidence) stages of the hierarchy of effects model.

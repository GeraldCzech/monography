**A stakeholder satisfaction barometer, adapted from the for-profit ACSI model and augmented with organizational identification, is a practical tool for NPO performance measurement.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Performance Measurement, Donor Satisfaction, Organizational Identification*

**Limitations:** The tool was developed and validated within a single NPO; adaptation and validation are required for other NPO types and stakeholder groups.

**Notes:** This provides a diagnostic tool for managing an NPO's BEBA by measuring key intermediate states that drive behaviour.

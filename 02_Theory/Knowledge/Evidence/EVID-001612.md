**A donor's perception of an NPO's operational effectiveness has a significant positive effect on both their giving intention and their subsequent giving behavior.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Perceptual Effectiveness, Giving Intention, Giving Behavior*

**Limitations:** Based on a 2008 survey of a young, highly-educated, online sample in China; uses self-reported data.

**Notes:** Perceived effectiveness emerges as a robust predictor, more so than brand equity, influencing the entire path from perception to behavior. It is a critical non-brand construct for the BEBA model.

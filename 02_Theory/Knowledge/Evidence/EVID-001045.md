**In the blood donation context, satisfaction is a stronger driver of donor loyalty than identification constructs.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Donor Loyalty, Donor Satisfaction, Organizational Identification, Identity Salience, Blood Donation*

**Limitations:** The finding is context-specific to a blood-collecting service with higher personal involvement and tangible service quality aspects.

**Notes:** This qualifies the general claim that identity-based constructs dominate donor loyalty.

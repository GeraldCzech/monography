**Perceived brand equity, including its dimensions of brand personality, brand image, and brand awareness, has a significant positive effect on an individual's intention to donate to a nonprofit organization.**

*Strength: moderate*

**Limitations:** Based on a 2008 survey of a young, highly-educated, online sample in China; uses self-reported data.

**Notes:** This finding confirms that brand is a key component in shaping the architecture of donor behavior, specifically at the intention formation stage.

**High brand credibility strengthens the positive relationship between donor brand loyalty and overall brand equity.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Credibility, Brand Loyalty, Brand Equity*

**Limitations:** Brand credibility was measured as a unidimensional construct; its sub-dimensions (expertise, trustworthiness) might have distinct effects. The study's context is specific to Pakistani Muslim donors.

**Notes:** This is a core contribution, positioning credibility as a key system-level component that enhances the effectiveness of other brand assets like commitment.

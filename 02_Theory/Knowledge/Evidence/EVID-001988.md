**Watching-eye image on bottle-recycling machines showed no general treatment effect, but donations increased ~30% on low-attendance days (conditional situational cue effect).**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Situational Salience, Environmental Cue, Donation Behaviour*

**Limitations:** Context-dependent effect; null average effect in full sample.

Source: Ekström (2012). Do watching eyes affect charitable giving? Evidence from a field experiment. Experimental Economics.

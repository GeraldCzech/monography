**The relational environment, termed "The H Factor" (including trust, respect, cooperation), is a key antecedent dimension of VBE, strongly influencing volunteer Role Clarity and Brand Commitment.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Trust, Role Clarity, Brand Commitment, Internal Brand Management*

**Limitations:** "The H Factor" is a broad construct; the specific effects of its individual components (e.g., trust vs. respect) were not disaggregated.

**Notes:** This suggests that a successful BEBA is not purely informational or procedural but relies heavily on fostering a positive social and relational architecture.

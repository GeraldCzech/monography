**Nonprofit Brand Orientation (NBO) is empirically distinguishable from the related construct of Market Orientation.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Nonprofit Brand Orientation, Market Orientation, Discriminant Validity*

**Limitations:** The finding is based on self-report surveys from a single managerial respondent per organization.

**Notes:** This supports the treatment of BEBA as a distinct strategic focus, not merely a synonym for being market-oriented.

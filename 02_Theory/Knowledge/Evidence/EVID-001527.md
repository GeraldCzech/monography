*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Equity, Brand Attitude, Purchase Intention*

**Limitations:** The relationship is correlational, not causal, and tested only within the study's specific sample and product categories.

**Notes:** This aligns with BEBA's behaviour-centric framework by linking brand equity to behavioural antecedents.

**The lack of discriminant validity among the dimensions of Aaker's (1991) CBBE model is more severe for service and internet brands than for tangible goods.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Brand Equity, Discriminant Validity, Intangibility, Service Brands*

**Limitations:** Based on a broad categorization of goods, services, and internet brands; does not explore finer-grained sector differences.

**Notes:** This establishes brand intangibility as a critical boundary condition for the model's validity and has strong implications for its application to the nonprofit sector.

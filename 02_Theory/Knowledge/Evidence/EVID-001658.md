**Individuals who engage in multiple forms of donation possess sociodemographic and psychographic profiles that are systematically different from those who engage in only a single form.**

*Strength: moderate*

*Concepts: Behavioral Segmentation, Donor Profile, Pro-social Behavior*

**Limitations:** Profiles are based on correlational data without direct measures of motivation. The data's temporal resolution for different donation types varies.

**Notes:** This highlights the importance of modeling the entire portfolio of a supporter's behavior, a key tenet for BEBA.

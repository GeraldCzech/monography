**Price premium is proposed as a summary measure of brand equity because it reflects the willingness of customers to pay more for one brand than for a competitor.**

*Strength: weak*

*Concepts: Price Premium, Brand Equity, Willingness to Pay, Loyalty*

**Limitations:** The reviewed material notes boundary conditions, including markets with regulated pricing or limited price differentiation.

**Notes:** In a nonprofit context, this may require translation into donation, volunteering, or advocacy premium, but that translation is an implication rather than direct evidence.

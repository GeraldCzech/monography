# Commitment

## Definition

Commitment refers to a donor's relational attachment to an organisation and willingness to maintain support over time.

## Role in BEBA

Commitment links brand equity to repeated or more intensive forms of resource mobilisation. It is expected to be more behaviourally proximate than awareness.

- Commitment may emerge from ideology, habit or personal experience rather than brand equity alone.
- Commitment may differ across donation, volunteering and advocacy.

# Trust

## Definition

Trust refers to a donor's willingness to rely on a nonprofit organisation under conditions of uncertainty and vulnerability.

## Role in BEBA

Trust is both an institutional mechanism and a brand-equity component. It reduces perceived risk and can transform brand perception into donation intention and behaviour.

## Boundary Conditions

- Trust may be generalised to a sector rather than organisation-specific.
- Trust can be damaged by reputational crises.
- Very high institutional trust may make explicit brand evaluation less necessary.

## Relationship Statements

- Trust reduces Epistemic Uncertainty.
- Trust mediates Brand Equity to Donation Intention.
- Trust relates to Reputation and Authenticity.
- Trust may substitute for detailed impact information.

## Statement

Changes in a brand’s more volatile awareness measure may reflect random noise rather than systematic brand-performance change.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s need to distinguish behavioural signal from measurement noise under uncertainty.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports this as a managerial measurement concern rather than as a fully explained causal mechanism.

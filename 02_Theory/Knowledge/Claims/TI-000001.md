## Statement

The causal effect of Austrian tax deductibility rules on individual donation amounts and donation frequency is not established in the reviewed behavioural literature.

## Implications for BEBA

This indicates that BEBA should not make behavioural claims about tax incentives without verified empirical pipeline evidence.

## Notes

Not sufficiently covered in the reviewed literature.

## Statement

Public perception of nonprofits follows a sequential process where cognitive awareness precedes the formation of affective confidence.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a core causal mechanism for how nonprofit brands build equity, linking the cognitive and affective stages in the BEBA architecture.

## Notes

McDougle (2014) empirically tested and supported the link from the cognitive (awareness) to the affective (confidence) stage; the link to the conative (behavioural) stage was not tested.

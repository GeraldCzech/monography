## Statement

Perceived internal brand management practices in nonprofit organizations are positively correlated with volunteer brand commitment.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the BEBA proposition that organisational brand practices can generate affective conditions for brand-enabled behaviour.

## Notes

The evidence concerns formal volunteers in nonprofit organizations.

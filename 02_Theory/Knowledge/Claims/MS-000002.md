## Statement

Market share can be a misleading indicator of brand health when it is increased through short-term tactics that erode long-term brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim cautions BEBA against treating observed support volume or donation share as sufficient evidence of durable nonprofit brand equity.

## Notes

The claim concerns the interpretation of market behavior measures.

## Statement

Brand loyalty is a positive antecedent of brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the view that BEBA, as a form of brand equity, is an outcome of sustained, loyal supporter behaviours.

## Notes

This finding reinforces foundational brand equity models such as Aaker (1991).

## Statement

Perception of a nonprofit brand as a "Good Samaritan" archetype is positively associated with public trust in that brand.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim links a desirable brand archetype directly to trust, a key enabler in the BEBA model.

## Notes

The association is based on qualitative thematic analysis, not a statistical correlation.

## Statement

Brand attitude partially mediates the positive relationship between nonprofit brand salience and new donor brand choice intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA by specifying an indirect pathway from memory-based brand accessibility to behavioural intention through evaluative judgment.

## Notes

The mediation was reported in the context of large international aid charities.

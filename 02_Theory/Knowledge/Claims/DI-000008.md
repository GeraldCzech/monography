## Statement

Positive perceptions of nonprofit brand image are associated with stronger intentions to donate money to the nonprofit organization.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim links nonprofit brand perception to a core behavioural intention in the BEBA framework.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports self-reported intentions, not actual monetary donation behaviour.

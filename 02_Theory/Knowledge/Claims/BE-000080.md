## Statement

Bayesian updating, specifically Jeffrey Conditionalization, offers a coherent epistemological framework for integrating uncertain and heterogeneous evidence into a single evolving body of knowledge.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the epistemological justification for BEBA's cumulative, belief-updating approach to evidence.

## Notes

The reference to Jeffrey Conditionalization specifies the mechanism for handling uncertain evidence, a key feature for social science contexts.

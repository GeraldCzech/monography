## Statement

An integrated Evidence-Theory-Ontology (ETO) pipeline is a more effective methodology for producing cumulative and computationally valuable social science theory than disconnected approaches.

## Implications for BEBA

This is the core methodological thesis justifying the entire BEBA research architecture and workflow.

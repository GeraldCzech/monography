## Statement

Employment in the nonprofit sector is not a significant predictor of an individual's top-of-mind awareness of nonprofit organizations.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that even internal stakeholders may lack fundamental brand awareness, challenging assumptions about insider knowledge.

## Notes

This finding is in direct contrast to earlier work by Schlesinger et al. (2004).

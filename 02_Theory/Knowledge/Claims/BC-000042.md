## Statement

Brand credibility moderates the relationship between service quality and stakeholder behavioural outcomes.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests a testable hypothesis for how brand-related constructs strengthen the core service-logic behavioural chain.

## Notes

This positions brand constructs as enhancers of an existing process rather than as direct drivers of quality perception.

## Statement

Conceptual models of nonprofit brand image that exclude Reliability and Ethicality have weaker explanatory power for donation intentions than models that include these dimensions.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that trust-related dimensions are necessary for explaining how nonprofit brands enable supportive behaviour under uncertainty.

## Notes

The claim concerns intentions rather than observed donation behaviour.

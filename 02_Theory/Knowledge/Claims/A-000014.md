## Statement

The adoption of performance-based contracting in Austrian social services has increased the power of public funders relative to nonprofit service providers.

## Implications for BEBA

This points to a specific mechanism through which institutional demands can shape the behaviours a nonprofit brand must enable, such as signaling efficiency.

## Notes

The impact of this power shift is well-supported for social services but its generalizability to other sub-sectors is less clear.

## Statement

Nonprofit brand awareness is a prerequisite for the development of nonprofit brand trust and nonprofit brand commitment.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim specifies awareness as the entry condition for subsequent trust formation and relationship commitment in the BEBA architecture.

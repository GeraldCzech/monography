## Statement

In new donor acquisition for international aid charities, nonprofit brand salience positively predicts donor attitude toward the brand.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA by linking mental availability to evaluative brand processing in donor decision-making.

## Notes

The evidence is based on new donors in an Australian disaster-relief scenario.

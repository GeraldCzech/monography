## Statement

Brand Attachment motivates consumers to invest financial, social, and temporal resources to maintain and protect the brand relationship.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a behavioural mechanism for why attached nonprofit supporters may donate, volunteer, or advocate despite personal costs.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis presents this mechanism as an extension of Self-Expansion Theory.

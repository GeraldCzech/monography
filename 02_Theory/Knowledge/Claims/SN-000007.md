## Statement

Injunctive social norms are a more reliable and significant predictor of donation intention than descriptive social norms.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA's social components should focus more on communicating social approval for donating rather than just showing that many others are donating.

## Notes

The effect of descriptive norms is reported to be inconsistent and context-dependent, whereas injunctive norms show a more stable effect.

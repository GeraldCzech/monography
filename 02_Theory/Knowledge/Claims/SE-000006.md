## Statement

For stakeholders with positive attitudes but perceived financial constraints, offering non-monetary engagement pathways can build a long-term relationship.

## Implications for BEBA

A BEBA should be multi-modal, including pathways for non-monetary support to build a pipeline of future donors.

## Notes

This is a strategic recommendation from the paper, not a direct empirical finding.

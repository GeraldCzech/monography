## Statement

High Brand Engagement ("Brand Wise") leads to an automatic, non-compensatory volunteer choice process where alternative nonprofits are not considered.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a core mechanism for how NPO brands function as an efficient behavioural architecture, bypassing complex deliberation.

## Notes

This finding challenges the assumption of rational, comparative evaluation for high-involvement decisions.

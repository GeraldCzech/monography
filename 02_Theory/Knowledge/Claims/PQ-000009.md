## Statement

The direct positive relationship between donor-perceived service quality and a charity brand's overall brand equity is not significant.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests BEBA must differentiate between operational quality and mission credibility, as the former may not be a primary driver of behaviour under certain motivations.

## Notes

This finding is specific to the context of obligatory religious giving (Zakat in Pakistan) and may not generalize to secular or non-obligatory giving contexts.

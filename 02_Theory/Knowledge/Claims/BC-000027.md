## Statement

A supporter's level of commitment to a charity is positively associated with their level of behavioral involvement in the charity's activities.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim defines a feedback loop where commitment drives further behaviour, which is central to BEBA's idea of a self-reinforcing architecture.

## Notes

This is based on Proposition 3 (P3) from the Hassay & Peloza (2009) conceptual model.

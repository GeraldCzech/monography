## Statement

In the nonprofit sector, a member's positive perception of an organization's market orientation is positively and significantly associated with their positive perception of its brand orientation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes that the stakeholder perception of an organization's market-oriented behaviour directly shapes the perception of its brand.

## Notes

Finding is based on a single study in the context of Australian church organizations using convenience sampling.

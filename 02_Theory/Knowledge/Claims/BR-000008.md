## Statement

Effective recovery from perceived breaches of a brand promise safeguards organisational reputation and long-term brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA by showing that brand-enabled behaviour must include recovery mechanisms when consistency fails.

## Notes

The claim is particularly relevant for uncertainty management because service failures or perceived mission failures may threaten stakeholder trust.

## Statement

Adding moral norm and past behaviour to the standard Theory of Planned Behaviour model significantly increases the explained variance in donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must account for how brands influence not just rational beliefs but also moral obligations and habits.

## Notes

This suggests the standard three-predictor TPB model is likely insufficient for a full explanation of pro-social behaviour.

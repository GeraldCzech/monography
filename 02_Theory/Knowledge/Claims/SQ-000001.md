## Statement

Perceived service quality is a strong, positive antecedent of stakeholder satisfaction in nonprofit service contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the BEBA principle that tangible brand experiences are primary drivers of stakeholder psychological states.

## Notes

The finding is based on a blood donation service context with a path coefficient of .81, and may not generalize to all NPO types.

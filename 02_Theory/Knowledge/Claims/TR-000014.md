## Statement

The variance in public trust in a nonprofit organization is predominantly explained by individual-level factors rather than organizational-level factors.

## Implications for BEBA

This finding mandates that the BEBA model must prioritize stakeholder heterogeneity over purely organizational attributes.

## Notes

The study found individual-level factors (stable context + specific evaluation) accounted for over 90% of explainable variance, while organizational context was ~6%.

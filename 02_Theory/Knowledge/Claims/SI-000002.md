## Statement

Social-image concerns increase the amount donated among individuals who have already decided to give.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that public brand touchpoints can amplify donation magnitude by enabling prosocial signalling.

## Notes

Conditional on the decision to donate having already been made.

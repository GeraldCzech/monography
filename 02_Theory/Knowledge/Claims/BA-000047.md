## Statement

Perceived brand authenticity requires that the brand object be evaluated in relation to a recognizable category.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim defines a boundary condition for using authenticity as a behavioural mechanism in BEBA.

## Notes

The concept may be less applicable where categories are nascent or ill-defined.

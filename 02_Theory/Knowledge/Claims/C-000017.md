## Statement

Self-image enhancement is a key driver of the decision to participate in giving.

## Evidence

Grossman and Levy (2024) distinguish self-image and social-image drivers of giving and identify self-image as especially relevant for the decision to give.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should treat donor acquisition as partly identity-based. Nonprofit brands may activate giving by allowing individuals to see themselves as caring, generous, or morally consistent actors.

## Statement

Stakeholder satisfaction with a nonprofit is a positive predictor of repeat support behaviours.

## Implications for BEBA

This connects an internal psychological state (satisfaction) to a key behavioural outcome that BEBA aims to explain.

## Notes

Some contradictory evidence exists in other sectors (e.g., banking), suggesting boundary conditions may apply (Kusuma et al., 2021).

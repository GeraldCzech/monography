## Statement

The act of donating provides private utility to donors that is distinct from utility derived from the provision of the public good.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes warm glow as a core behavioural reinforcement mechanism that nonprofit brands can activate independently of verified beneficiary outcomes.

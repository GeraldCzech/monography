## Statement

The development of staff emotional brand attachment is a direct antecedent of staff service involvement in the nonprofit internal branding process.

## Implications for BEBA

This specifies the critical affective-to-behavioural sequence within the individual component of the BEBA framework.

## Notes

The study provides strong correlational evidence for this path; describing it as a necessary "antecedent" implies a causal direction supported by theory.

## Statement

The perceived quality of the relationship between a stakeholder and a nonprofit directly predicts loyalty and positive word-of-mouth.

## Implications for BEBA

This highlights a specific and critical dimension of 'quality' that a brand architecture must help create and maintain.

## Notes

This is identified as a secondary mechanism, possibly operating in parallel to the main Quality-Value-Satisfaction chain.

## Statement

The magnitude of tax-price elasticity on charitable giving is moderated by national context.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates BEBA's principle of contextual contingency, warning against universalist claims about financial incentives.

## Notes

Effect sizes are noted to be smaller in several European countries compared to the US.

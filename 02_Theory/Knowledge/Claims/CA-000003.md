## Statement

Reducing the net financial cost of giving generally increases the amount donated.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should include perceived cost and benefit framing as part of the behavioural effectiveness of nonprofit brands.

## Notes

The magnitude of the price effect varies across studies.

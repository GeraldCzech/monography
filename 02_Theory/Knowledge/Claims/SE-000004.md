## Statement

Perceived social expectations have a stronger effect on past volunteering of time compared to past donations of blood or money.

## Implications for BEBA

This mandates strategic differentiation; a BEBA for volunteering should emphasize social/community aspects more than one for financial giving.

## Notes

The authors speculate this is due to volunteering being a more "public" form of giving.

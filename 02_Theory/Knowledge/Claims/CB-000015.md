## Statement

Brands with higher consumer-based brand equity generate stronger purchase intentions than functionally similar brands with lower consumer-based brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim links brand equity to behavioural intention, which is central to explaining how brands become behaviourally effective.

## Notes

Purchase intention was measured through regression analysis in the reviewed study.

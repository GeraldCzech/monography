## Statement

The optimal awareness metric for a global brand differs across national markets when the brand’s market share differs across those markets.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim extends BEBA measurement architecture by requiring context-specific awareness metrics for organisations operating across heterogeneous markets.

## Notes

The reviewed material frames this as especially relevant for global brands with different local market positions.

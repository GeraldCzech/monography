## Statement

The effect of donor identity salience on donor loyalty is stronger for older donors in the money donation context studied by Boenigk and Helmig.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should allow demographic characteristics to condition the strength of identity-based behavioural mechanisms.

## Notes

This claim is based on the multigroup analysis described in the reviewed material.

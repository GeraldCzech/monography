## Statement

Nonprofit organizations use experiential, emotional, and reputational branding approaches as cost-effective alternatives to large-scale advertising.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that nonprofit brand-enabled behaviour may operate through stakeholder experience and affective connection rather than media spending.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports practitioner reliance on these approaches but does not provide comparative performance estimates.

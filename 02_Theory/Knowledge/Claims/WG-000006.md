## Statement

Voluntary giving activates reward-related neural responses in ways distinguishable from mandatory transfers.

## Evidence

Harbaugh et al. (2007)

## Implications for BEBA

Warm glow has an affective and hedonic basis that may precede deliberate brand evaluation.

## Notes

Neuroscientific evidence supports the distinction between voluntary and compulsory giving.

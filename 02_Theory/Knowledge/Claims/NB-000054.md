## Statement

NGOs require a donor-based brand equity model that differs from general nonprofit brand equity models.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should avoid assuming that a single nonprofit brand equity architecture applies across all nonprofit forms.

## Notes

The reviewed material frames this as the central argument of Romero and Abril.

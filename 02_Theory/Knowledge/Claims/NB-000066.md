## Statement

Nonprofit branding effectiveness cannot be adequately measured by brand identity metrics alone.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s rejection of internal-identity-only brand equity models for nonprofit contexts.

## Notes

The claim reflects the paper’s critique of inside-out measurement approaches.

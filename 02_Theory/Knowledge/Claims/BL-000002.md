## Statement

Brand loyalty manifests in customer behaviors such as repeat purchase, willingness to pay more, and willingness to recommend.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim directly supports BEBA’s behavioural focus by linking brand equity to repeated and supportive action.

## Notes

In nonprofit contexts, the equivalent behaviors require sector-specific operationalization.

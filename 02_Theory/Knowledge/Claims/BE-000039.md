## Statement

A comprehensive brand equity measurement system includes customer-based measures of loyalty, perceived quality, brand associations, and awareness, as well as market-based behavior measures.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a classical measurement architecture that BEBA can adapt for nonprofit brand equity.

## Notes

The claim is based on Aaker’s conceptual framework rather than empirical validation in nonprofit contexts.

## Statement

The negative effect on public trust from a "Marketer" perception is not significantly mitigated by the provision of financial transparency reports alone.

## Implications for BEBA

It warns that standard organisational remedies for distrust may fail if the brand architecture has fundamentally misaligned perceived motives.

## Notes

This claim is derived from the paper's argument that the distrust is about motives and ethics, which are not fully addressed by accountability metrics.

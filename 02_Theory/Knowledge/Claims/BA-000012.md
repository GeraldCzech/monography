## Statement

Brand authenticity was not retained as a valid measurement component in the final donor-based NGO brand equity model.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim cautions against assuming that authenticity functions as a distinct donor-perceived brand equity driver without empirical validation.

## Notes

The reviewed summary states that all brand authenticity items were eliminated from the final measurement model.

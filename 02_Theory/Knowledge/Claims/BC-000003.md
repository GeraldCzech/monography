## Statement

Existing nonprofit brand equity theories are better suited to deliberative planned giving than to impulsive or emergency-driven donations.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should distinguish brand-enabled planned support from situationally triggered emergency giving.

## Notes

This is presented as a boundary condition in the synthesis rather than as a directly tested empirical claim.

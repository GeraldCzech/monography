## Statement

General brand equity measures require adaptation to the specific brand, product category, and market context to remain valid.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies adapting commercial brand equity constructs before applying them to nonprofit contexts.

## Notes

This is a central boundary condition of the Brand Equity Ten framework.

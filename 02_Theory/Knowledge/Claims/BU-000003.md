## Statement

Controlling for trust and prior giving history, perceived brand uniqueness has a positive effect on donor retention and repeat donation frequency.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim pushes BEBA to test its propositions against longitudinal, behavioral data, moving beyond cross-sectional intentions.

## Notes

This claim is speculative and formulated to address the identified research gap concerning actual behavioral outcomes and long-term donor relationships.

## Statement

Standard B2B brand equity measurement models may require modification to achieve construct validity in a nonprofit context.

## Implications for BEBA

This finding underscores the necessity of a dedicated theoretical framework like BEBA for nonprofits, as direct model transfers are inadequate.

## Notes

The authors had to remove items from the Davis et al. (2008) B2B model to achieve an acceptable statistical fit for their nonprofit case.

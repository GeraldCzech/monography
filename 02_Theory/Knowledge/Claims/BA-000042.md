## Statement

Higher advertising investment is associated with more favourable brand association structures among objectively similar brands.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s view that communication can configure cognitive association structures that influence later behavioural responses.

## Notes

The reviewed study treats brand associations as one component of consumer-based brand equity.

## Statement

In online donation and crowdfunding environments, trust in the receiving organization or platform is a crucial facilitator of charitable donation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim implies that digital nonprofit brand effectiveness depends partly on reducing uncertainty about organizational and platform reliability.

## Notes

The reviewed material frames trust as especially important in digital environments.

## Statement

Perceived management support and positive relational factors ("The H Factor") are key dimensions of internal brand management that are strongly correlated with volunteer Role Clarity and Brand Commitment.

## Implications for BEBA

This highlights that a BEBA's effectiveness depends significantly on its social and relational components, not just informational ones.

## Notes

"The H Factor" refers to human, relational aspects like trust, respect, and cooperation. The claim is based on a single exploratory study.

## Statement

The reviewed nonprofit brand equity models do not provide an empirically tested path from brand perceptions to actual observed donation behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should explicitly connect brand-side constructs to behavioural theory and observed behaviour where pipeline evidence permits.

## Statement

Cultural context moderates the predictive salience of Theory of Planned Behavior constructs for charitable donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim cautions that BEBA should not assume culturally invariant pathways from brand perceptions to donation behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed material indicates that traditional TPB predictors may be less salient in some non-Western contexts.

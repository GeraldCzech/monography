## Statement

Brand trust positively influences stakeholder commitment and supportive intentions, particularly in contexts of high uncertainty about nonprofit effectiveness.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides direct evidence for the uncertainty reduction mechanism, a core component of the BEBA framework.

## Notes

This highlights the brand's function as a cognitive heuristic in complex or low-transparency decision environments.

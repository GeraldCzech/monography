## Statement

The effectiveness of a nonprofit's social media strategy in generating engagement is positively related to the degree of two-way, interactive communication it facilitates.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a specific, testable principle for what constitutes an 'effective' communication architecture within BEBA.

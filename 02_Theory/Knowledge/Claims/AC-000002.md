## Statement

Empirical studies testing integrated behavior models frequently fail to measure the 'actual control' component.

## Implications for BEBA

This highlights a methodological gap that BEBA's empirical work can address by explicitly measuring and modeling brand-related 'actual control' factors.

## Notes

This methodological failure may explain the lack of empirical support for the superiority of integrated models.

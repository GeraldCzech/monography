## Statement

The constructs of brand awareness and brand associations, as measured by standard survey items based on Aaker (1991), lack discriminant validity.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must propose an alternative structure for brand knowledge that avoids the empirical collapse of awareness and associations.

## Notes

This measurement failure is consistently observed across multiple national contexts (UK, Germany, Greece).

## Statement

The Brand Loyalty dimension of consumer-based brand equity can be validly measured as an attitudinal construct representing commitment and preferential choice.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports defining nonprofit loyalty as an attitudinal commitment to support, advocate, or donate, rather than as transactional frequency.

## Notes

This measurement approach contrasts with purely behavioural measures of loyalty, such as repeat purchase history.

## Statement

Process-based brand equity models predict willingness to pay a premium and repurchase intent better than static parallel-dimension models.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports testing whether process-based nonprofit brand equity models better explain donating, volunteering, and advocacy behaviours.

## Notes

Evidence concerns consumer behaviour in reviewed brand equity research rather than nonprofit stakeholder behaviour directly.

## Statement

Donor involvement level moderates which nonprofit brand cues influence supportive behavioural intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA's contingency logic by indicating that brand-enabled behaviour depends on donor involvement conditions.

## Notes

The reviewed material suggests highly involved donors may rely more on attitudes, whereas less-involved donors may rely more on salience or familiarity cues.

## Statement

Trust, intimacy, relevance, and partner quality function as core causal conditions in configurations that lead to high overall brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s focus on relational mechanisms as central pathways through which brands enable stakeholder behaviour.

## Notes

The finding concerns consumers’ favorite brands and may differ for neutral, utilitarian, or unfavorable brands.

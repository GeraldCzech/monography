## Statement

The objective characteristics of a nonprofit organization explain less than 10% of the total variance in public trust evaluations.

## Implications for BEBA

This quantifies the limited direct impact of brand signals, suggesting their role is heavily mediated by individual-level factors.

## Notes

The specific variance explained by the organization's context was 6.38% in the referenced study.

## Statement

Brand trust reduces perceived donation risk by signalling organisational integrity, competence, and ethical conduct under information asymmetry.

## Implications for BEBA

Trust should be a central BEBA mechanism for explaining how nonprofit brands become behaviourally effective under uncertainty.

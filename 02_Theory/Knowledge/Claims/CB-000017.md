## Statement

Aaker's (1991) four-dimensional model of consumer-based brand equity (awareness, associations, perceived quality, loyalty) does not demonstrate measurement invariance when tested on real consumers across multiple European countries.

## Implications for BEBA

This justifies rejecting the direct application of Aaker's CBBE model to the nonprofit context without rigorous, sector-specific validation.

## Notes

The finding contradicts some prior research that used student samples, suggesting sample type is a critical moderator.

## Statement

Brand categorization and brand awareness play a foundational role in consumer brand psychology.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim implies that nonprofit brands must first be identifiable and categorizable before they can enable higher-order behavioural effects.

## Notes

The reviewed material treats brand categorization and awareness as established building blocks in Schmitt’s framework.

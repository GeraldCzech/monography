## Statement

Congruence between a donor's self-concept and a nonprofit's brand personality positively influences donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the inclusion of identity congruence as a mechanism by which brands enable pro-social behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed material frames this effect through donor-brand congruence, identification, and personal relevance.

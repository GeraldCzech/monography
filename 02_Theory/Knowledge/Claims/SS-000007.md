## Statement

The population of monetary non-donors is not a homogeneous group and can be segmented into distinct clusters based on different reasons for non-support.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates a segmented approach to BEBA design, challenging the effectiveness of a universal, one-size-fits-all architecture.

## Notes

The study identifies five segments: Categorical, Unsolicited, Indifferent, Restricted, and Convincible.

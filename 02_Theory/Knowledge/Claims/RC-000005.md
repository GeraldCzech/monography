## Statement

Volunteer role clarity is a weaker predictor of positive word-of-mouth than volunteer brand commitment.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA by indicating that affective commitment may matter more than cognitive clarity for advocacy behaviour.

## Notes

The evidence concerns formal volunteers.

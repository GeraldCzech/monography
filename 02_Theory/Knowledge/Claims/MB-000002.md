## Statement

The use of unconditional gifts or thank-you gifts as incentives does not reliably increase donations.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should focus on intrinsic and efficacy-based mechanisms rather than extrinsic material rewards.

## Notes

These incentives can sometimes reduce giving, especially among existing donors.

## Statement

Charitable giving functions as a mechanism for converting a donor's economic capital into symbolic capital such as prestige or social standing.

## Implications for BEBA

This establishes the core sociological mechanism for which nonprofit brand equity serves as the organizational counterpart.

## Notes

This is a foundational concept from Bourdieu's theory of practice, framing giving as a social exchange.

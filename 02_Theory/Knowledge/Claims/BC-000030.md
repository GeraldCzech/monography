## Statement

A supporter's perceived sense of community with a charity is positively and directly associated with their level of commitment to that charity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim highlights a social pathway to commitment, complementing the individual identity-based pathway and strengthening the brand's social architecture.

## Notes

This is based on Proposition 6 (P6) from the Hassay & Peloza (2009) conceptual model.

## Statement

Perceived quality, effectiveness, transparency, and accountability are underdeveloped constructs in the reviewed nonprofit brand equity literature.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should specify how performance-related beliefs and accountability perceptions contribute to trust and behavioural effectiveness.

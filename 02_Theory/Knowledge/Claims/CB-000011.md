## Statement

A consumer-based brand equity scale validated in UK and Spanish samples has not been shown to be invariant in nonprofit, service, B2B, or non-Western contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This limits direct transfer of commercial CBBE scales to nonprofit BEBA research without additional validation.

## Notes

This claim reflects the boundary conditions reported in the reviewed material.

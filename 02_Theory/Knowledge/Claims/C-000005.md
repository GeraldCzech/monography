## Statement

The social-image component of warm glow depends on whether the act of giving is observable to a relevant social audience.

## Evidence

The synthesis states that social-image signalling is conditional on observability, whereas self-image operates even in private.

Supported in the synthesis by: Grossman & Levy, 2024.

## Implications for BEBA

Brand-enabled giving environments should distinguish private giving contexts from socially visible giving contexts. Visibility features may activate different mechanisms than private donation flows.

## Notes

This claim concerns a boundary condition rather than a direct behavioural effect.

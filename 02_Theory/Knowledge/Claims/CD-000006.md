## Statement

The psychometric validity of nonprofit brand equity models differs across geographic and cultural contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This indicates that BEBA should treat measurement architectures as context-sensitive rather than universally invariant.

## Notes

The reviewed material infers this boundary condition from the Austrian setting and the superior performance of a German-developed model.

## Statement

Senior marketing personnel in UK nonprofit organizations do not perceive limited marketing budgets as a major impediment to successful nonprofit brand building.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim challenges budget-centred accounts of brand effectiveness and supports BEBA’s emphasis on nonfinancial organizational capabilities.

## Notes

The finding is exploratory and may not generalize beyond the sampled UK nonprofit organizations.

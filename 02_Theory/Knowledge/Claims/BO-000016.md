## Statement

A nonprofit's strategic communication orientation moderates the long-term relationship between its brand activities and performance outcomes.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that the content and tone of the brand architecture are critical variables for its long-term effectiveness.

## Notes

The source found a "supportive" positioning outperformed a "combative" one over time regarding donations and survival.

## Statement

A significant portion of charitable giving is motivated by the private, non-pecuniary utility an individual receives from the act of giving, independent of the beneficiary outcome.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes warm glow as a behavioural mechanism through which nonprofit brands can motivate giving beyond communicated impact.

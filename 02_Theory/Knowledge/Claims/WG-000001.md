## Statement

Warm glow giving refers to the private emotional utility donors derive from the act of giving itself, independently of the donation’s consequential impact on recipients or aggregate charitable output.

## Evidence

Andreoni (1990); Crumpler & Grossman (2008)

## Implications for BEBA

Warm glow should be treated as a motivational antecedent to giving that can operate independently of nonprofit brand equity.

## Notes

This is the canonical definitional claim in the warm-glow literature.

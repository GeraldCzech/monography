## Statement

Extending the Theory of Planned Behavior to include moral norm and past behavior significantly increases its predictive power for donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA's model of brand influence must account for moral and habitual drivers, not just reasoned attitudes and social norms.

## Notes

The summary describes moral norm as the most powerful and replicated extension.

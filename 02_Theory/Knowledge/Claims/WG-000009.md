## Statement

Donors derive private, emotional utility (warm glow) from the act of giving itself, independent of the actual outcome.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a mechanism for how brand-related feelings, separate from impact, can enable giving behaviour.

## Notes

This mechanism explains why individuals donate even when their contribution has no marginal impact.

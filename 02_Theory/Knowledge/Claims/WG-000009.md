## Statement

Self-image and social-image mechanisms affect different margins of giving: self-image primarily influences whether people give, while social image primarily influences how much they give.

## Evidence

Grossman & Levy (2024)

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should distinguish donation participation from donation amount when modelling motivational pathways.

## Notes

The SKU reports this as replicated in laboratory and field designs.

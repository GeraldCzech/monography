## Statement

A nonprofit brand's perceived credibility reduces stakeholder uncertainty and increases the likelihood of resource provision.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes risk reduction as a core mechanism through which a BEBA influences stakeholder behaviour under uncertainty.

## Statement

A nonprofit brand's perceived image is associated with resource provider support intentions.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports treating brand image as a cognitive component of the BEBA architecture.

## Notes

The evidence indicates multidimensional effects rather than a uniform effect of brand image.

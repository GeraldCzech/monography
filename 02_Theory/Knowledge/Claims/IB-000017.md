## Statement

Perceived internal brand management practices in nonprofit organizations are positively correlated with volunteer role clarity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the BEBA proposition that brand architecture can increase behavioural effectiveness by clarifying stakeholder roles.

## Notes

The evidence concerns formal volunteers in nonprofit organizations.

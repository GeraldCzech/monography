## Statement

The symbolic capital of a nonprofit brand is contingent on the structure of its philanthropic field and can change independently of the organization's actions.

## Implications for BEBA

It highlights that brand equity is not solely an organizational asset but is co-determined by the market-level field structure.

## Notes

The review identifies a gap in longitudinal studies needed to empirically trace these field-level dynamics.

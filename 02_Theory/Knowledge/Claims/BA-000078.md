## Statement

A nonprofit's brand image and brand awareness are positively associated with a potential donor's intention to donate.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes the relevance of brand constructs for donation intention, a necessary precursor to studying behavioral effectiveness.

## Notes

This claim concerns donation *intention*, not actual donation *behavior*, a distinction highlighted in the literature as a critical gap.

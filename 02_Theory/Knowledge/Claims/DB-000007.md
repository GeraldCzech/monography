## Statement

Donor attitudes toward beneficiaries are relevant to explaining nonprofit brand equity and donor behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports extending BEBA from a donor-organisation dyad to a donor-organisation-beneficiary triad.

## Notes

The document presents this as a future research direction rather than as a validated measurement result.

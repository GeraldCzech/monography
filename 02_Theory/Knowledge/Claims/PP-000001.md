## Statement

Consumer price perception moderates the relationship between brand equity dimensions and purchase intention.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should consider whether perceived cost, sacrifice, or affordability conditions the brand-equity-to-intention pathway in nonprofit contexts.

## Notes

The direct construct is price perception in consumer purchase contexts, so nonprofit transfer requires theoretical adaptation.

## Statement

The ability of Theory of Planned Behavior constructs to predict long-term sustained charitable donation behavior is less established than their ability to predict donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies a temporal limitation that BEBA should address when theorising durable brand-enabled behavioural architectures.

## Notes

The reviewed material identifies this as a research gap rather than an established empirical finding.

## Statement

Marketing policies and practices can be designed to enhance an organization's competitiveness while simultaneously advancing social and environmental conditions.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA's premise that brand equity (competitiveness) and social impact (advancing conditions) are not mutually exclusive but can be jointly produced.

## Notes

This is the core tenet of Shared Value Creation.

## Statement

Volunteer role clarity positively predicts volunteer satisfaction among formal nonprofit volunteers.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that clear role understanding is part of the behavioural architecture that sustains volunteer satisfaction.

## Notes

The evidence is cross-sectional.

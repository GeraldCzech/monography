## Statement

The Boenigk & Becker three-dimensional nonprofit brand equity model comprising Awareness, Trust, and Commitment fits Austrian nonprofit market data better than the Faircloth and Rios Romero et al. models.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides BEBA with a parsimonious candidate measurement architecture for the Austrian nonprofit context.

## Notes

The claim is based on one Austrian empirical comparison using EFA/CFA.

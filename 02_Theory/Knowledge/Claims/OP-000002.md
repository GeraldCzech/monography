## Statement

An NPO's level of professionalization is positively associated with its reliance on public funding sources.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the BEBA premise that internal organizational capacity (professionalism) enables access to specific external resources.

## Notes

Based on descriptive data reported in the primary study within a Czech Republic context.

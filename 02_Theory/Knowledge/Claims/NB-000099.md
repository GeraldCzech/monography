## Statement

The relational component of nonprofit brand equity can be modeled as a second-order formative construct created by Brand Trust and Brand Commitment.

## Implications for BEBA

Provides a methodological precedent for modeling higher-order BEBA constructs as formative, reflecting that dimensions can be distinct causes.

## Notes

This is a specific finding from the PLS path model used in the study and may be model-dependent rather than a generalizable theoretical truth.

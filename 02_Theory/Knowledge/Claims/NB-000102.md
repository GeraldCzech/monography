## Statement

Brand equity models imported from commercial contexts are misspecified for the nonprofit sector when they omit essential constructs such as trust, authenticity, and mission alignment.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim mandates that the BEBA ontology must include trust, authenticity, and value alignment as first-class concepts.

## Notes

The consensus in the literature is strong that direct application of commercial scales is inappropriate.

## Statement

Identification-based constructs have a stronger total effect on donor loyalty than satisfaction in the money donation context studied by Boenigk and Helmig.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should allow the relative weight of identity mechanisms to vary by donation context.

## Notes

The claim applies to the money-collecting cancer charity context in the reviewed study.

## Statement

Consumers possess measurable, stable dispositions, such as brand schematicity or brand engagement in self-concept, that influence their general orientation toward brands.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should consider these dispositions as potential moderators or segmentation variables for explaining stakeholder behaviour.

## Notes

These are trait-like variables that can explain heterogeneity in responses to nonprofit branding efforts.

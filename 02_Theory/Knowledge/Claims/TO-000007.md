## Statement

An extended Theory of Planned Behavior model incorporating moral norms and past behavior explains significantly more variance in donation intention than the standard three-predictor TPB model.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must incorporate moral norms and mechanisms for habit formation to effectively model and influence donation intention.

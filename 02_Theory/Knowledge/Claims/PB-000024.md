## Statement

A member's perception of a nonprofit organization's brand orientation has no significant direct effect on the personal benefits they perceive from participation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies the role of brand, suggesting brand perception alone is insufficient to generate value and its effect is mediated by behaviour.

## Notes

Finding is based on a single study in the context of Australian church organizations; the primary structural model showed this non-significant path.

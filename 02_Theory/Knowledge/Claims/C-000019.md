## Statement

The warm glow experienced from donating is stronger when donated funds are earned by the donor than when they are received as a windfall.

## Evidence

Luccasen and Grossman (2017) find that donating earned income generates stronger warm glow than donating windfall gains.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model donation context as behaviourally relevant. Workplace giving, earned-income campaigns, or effort-linked fundraising may generate different motivational effects than campaigns based on windfall or effortless giving.

## Notes

Established boundary-condition claim in the reviewed literature. The review notes a methodological gap regarding additional field experiments.

## Statement

Price perception moderates relationships within integrated Customer-Based Brand Equity and Theory of Planned Behavior models.

## Implications for BEBA

This indicates that BEBA should account for perceived cost or sacrifice when modelling supportive behaviours.

## Notes

The nonprofit analogue may include perceived financial sacrifice, time cost, or opportunity cost.

## Statement

The perceived structure of nonprofit brand equity, defined by brand awareness and brand image, can be functionally similar for both internal and external stakeholder groups.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the brand's role as a shared cognitive architecture that coordinates perceptions and behaviour across diverse stakeholder groups.

## Notes

Finding is based on a single case study (Finnish Defence Forces driving school) and required removal of some measurement items to achieve model fit.

## Statement

The effect of nonprofit brand personality on giving intention is partially mediated by the donor's self-concept.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a specific identity-based pathway through which nonprofit brand personality becomes behaviourally effective.

## Notes

Evidence concerns giving intention rather than observed donation behaviour.

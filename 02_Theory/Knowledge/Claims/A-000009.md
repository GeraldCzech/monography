## Statement

Public awareness of nonprofit organizations is a positive and significant predictor of public confidence in their performance.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim validates the foundational role of brand awareness in building the affective components of brand equity, such as confidence.

## Notes

The study operationalizes awareness as unaided recall (top-of-mind awareness) and confidence as belief in an NPO's ability to provide quality services and spend money wisely.

## Statement

Satisfaction increases when perceived performance exceeds prior expectations and decreases when perceived performance falls short of prior expectations.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a baseline mechanism for explaining how brands become behaviourally effective under uncertainty.

## Notes

Tam extends this mechanism by showing that its strength varies by brand familiarity.

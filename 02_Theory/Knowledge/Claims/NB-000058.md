## Statement

The four-dimension nonprofit brand image scale developed by Michel and Rieunier (2012) shows poor factor structure and inadequate convergent and discriminant validity when tested in a UK context.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA's principle that nonprofit brand constructs require context-sensitive validation before being used as canonical measurement objects.

## Notes

The reviewed material describes Study 1 as a failed replication in the UK context.

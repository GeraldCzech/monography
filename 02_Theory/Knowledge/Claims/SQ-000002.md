## Statement

Improving the perceived quality of service delivery is an effective strategy for a nonprofit to increase donor loyalty.

## Implications for BEBA

This highlights that the "brand" in BEBA is not primarily advertising, but the managed architecture of stakeholder interaction and experience.

## Notes

The model shows service quality influences loyalty via the mediators of satisfaction and identification.

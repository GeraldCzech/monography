## Statement

Perceived brand equity does not have a statistically significant direct effect on an individual's actual giving behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim highlights the critical intention-behavior gap and suggests brand's influence on behavior is indirect, reinforcing the need for a multi-component architecture.

## Notes

This finding suggests that brand equity's influence is mediated by giving intention.

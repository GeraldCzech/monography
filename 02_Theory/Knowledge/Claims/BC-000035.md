## Statement

High brand credibility establishes a positive relationship between perceived quality and brand equity that is otherwise non-significant.

## Implications for BEBA

This demonstrates that Brand Credibility acts as a system-level component that can enable or disable the effectiveness of other brand signals.

## Notes

This interaction effect provides an explanation for the non-significant main effect of Perceived Quality found in the same study.

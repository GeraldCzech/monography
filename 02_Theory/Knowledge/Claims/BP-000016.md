## Statement

Brand Prominence is especially important when Brand–Self Connection is based on instrumentality rather than identity.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should distinguish nonprofit brands that are valued as identity expressions from those valued as instruments for achieving supporter goals.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis presents this as an apparent boundary condition.

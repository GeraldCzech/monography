## Statement

Nonprofit brand equity research has insufficiently developed perceived quality, effectiveness, transparency, and accountability as distinct drivers of brand trust.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should avoid using trust as an unexplained proxy and should specify the evaluative bases from which trust is formed.

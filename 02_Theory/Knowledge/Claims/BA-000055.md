## Statement

For high market-share brands, aided awareness is more stable over time than recall-based awareness measures.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that established nonprofit brands may require stable recognition-based indicators to detect meaningful shifts in mental availability.

## Notes

The reviewed material identifies top-of-mind awareness as more volatile for large brands.

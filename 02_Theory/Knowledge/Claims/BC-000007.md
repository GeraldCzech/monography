## Statement

Moral identification is more relevant for cause-based advocacy NGOs than for nonprofit organizations whose support depends primarily on perceived reliability or service effectiveness.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model nonprofit type as a moderator of which brand mechanisms become behaviourally effective.

## Notes

This is presented as a boundary-condition inference in the reviewed synthesis.

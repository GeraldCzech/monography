## Statement

When a salient new cause emerges, donors divert funds from other causes rather than drawing entirely on new charitable resources.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the BEBA proposition that brand salience matters for competitive allocation under uncertainty.

## Notes

The reviewed material specifically links this mechanism to the Covid-19 pandemic, so generalization to other crisis types remains uncertain.

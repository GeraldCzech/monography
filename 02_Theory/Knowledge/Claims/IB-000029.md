## Statement

The primary theoretical value of integrated behavior models lies in identifying specific, context-dependent post-intentional moderators rather than in superior overall predictive power.

## Implications for BEBA

This guides BEBA to focus on identifying and manipulating specific, context-dependent behavioral moderators rather than building a single monolithic predictive model.

## Notes

This shifts the focus from model-vs-model 'horse races' to identifying which moderators work under what conditions.

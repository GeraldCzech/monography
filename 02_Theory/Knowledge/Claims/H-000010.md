## Statement

The effectiveness of a nonprofit's brand-enabled behaviour architecture is moderated by its alignment with the habitus of target stakeholders.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim introduces habitus as a key moderating factor, suggesting BEBA's effectiveness depends on socio-cultural fit, not just psychological persuasion.

## Notes

This claim proposes a 'Habitus-Architecture Fit' hypothesis, where misalignment creates behavioural friction.

## Statement

Perceived high administrative costs are a primary reason given by non-donors for not giving money to nonprofits.

## Implications for BEBA

This elevates trust, signalled via perceived efficiency, to a foundational prerequisite for a BEBA's behavioural effectiveness.

## Notes

This was the top-ranked reason for non-support in the reviewed study.

## Statement

The co-creation of nonprofit brand equity is facilitated when internal stakeholders transition to become members of the external stakeholder group.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides an empirical example of a stakeholder behavioural loop that is critical for a BEBA's self-sustaining operation.

## Notes

This mechanism was inferred from the specific context of the study (recruits becoming professional drivers) rather than being a directly measured process.

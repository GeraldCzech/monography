## Statement

The Theory of Planned Behavior, extended with moral norms, is a highly predictive cognitive framework for explaining variance in charitable donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must integrate the cognitive deliberation pathways of TPB (attitudes, norms, PBC) as a core component of its individual layer.

## Notes

The original source identifies Perceived Behavioral Control (PBC) as a particularly strong predictor within the TPB framework.

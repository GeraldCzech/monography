## Statement

A consumer-based brand equity model comprising brand awareness, perceived quality, brand associations, and brand loyalty demonstrates configural invariance between UK and Spanish consumer samples.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the use of a multidimensional brand equity architecture as a starting point for nonprofit brand equity measurement.

## Notes

The evidence is limited to UK and Spanish consumer samples and does not establish invariance in nonprofit contexts.

## Statement

Senior nonprofit marketers in the UK perceive leadership support, clear vision, and employee buy-in as the most critical internal prerequisites for successful nonprofit branding.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s view that nonprofit brand effectiveness depends on internal organisational architecture rather than communication outputs alone.

## Notes

Based on an exploratory qualitative study with 20 senior nonprofit marketers in the UK.

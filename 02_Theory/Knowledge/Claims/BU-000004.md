## Statement

A donor's perception of a nonprofit brand's uniqueness has a causal effect on their subsequent actual donation behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies a critical, currently untested core proposition of the BEBA framework.

## Notes

The reviewed literature identifies this as a high-priority research gap with insufficient direct evidence from prospective behavioural studies.

## Statement

Lowering the material cost of giving (e.g., via tax deductions or subsidies) increases donation amounts.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA can conceptualize brand as a mechanism for reducing the *perceived* psychological or effort costs of acting.

## Notes

This finding is considered robust, though recent studies show smaller effect sizes than older ones.

## Statement

The effect of donor loyalty on actual donation behavior can become non-significant when physical or regulatory ceilings constrain the behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should include non-brand constraints in the final transition from behavioural disposition to realised behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed example concerns limits on blood donation frequency.

## Statement

Positive perceptions of nonprofit brand image are associated with stronger intentions to donate time to the nonprofit organization.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim links nonprofit brand perception to non-monetary supportive behaviour within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports self-reported intentions, not observed volunteering behaviour.

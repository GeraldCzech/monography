## Statement

The relative importance of brand name as a determinant of consumer preference is greater in low-involvement product categories than in higher-involvement service categories.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA by suggesting that brand-enabled behaviour may depend on involvement and perceived decision risk.

## Notes

The comparison is based on one low-risk product category and one higher-risk service category.

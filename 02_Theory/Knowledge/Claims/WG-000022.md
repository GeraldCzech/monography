## Statement

Warm glow corresponds most closely to the affective-attitude component of behavioural models such as TPB and IBM.

## Evidence

Andreoni (1990); TPB/IBM mapping in reviewed synthesis

## Implications for BEBA

Warm glow should not be treated as identical to intention; it is better positioned upstream of intention as affective attitude or emotional reward expectation.

## Notes

This is a conceptual mapping rather than a directly tested empirical claim.

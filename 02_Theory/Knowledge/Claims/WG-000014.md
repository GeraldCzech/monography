## Statement

Standard warm-glow and impure-altruism models may be insufficient for explaining giving that is primarily driven by faith, duty, obligation, or community norms.

## Evidence

Fraser (2018, 2022)

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should treat religious or duty-based giving as a possible boundary condition for warm-glow and brand-equity explanations.

## Notes

This is a critical/minority position in the reviewed literature.

## Statement

High-familiarity consumers rely less on single-encounter disconfirmation when forming satisfaction than low-familiarity consumers.

## Implications for BEBA

This qualifies BEBA by indicating that familiar brands may reduce dependence on effortful comparison processes.

## Notes

The reviewed study interprets this as reduced cognitive effort among consumers with more developed knowledge structures.

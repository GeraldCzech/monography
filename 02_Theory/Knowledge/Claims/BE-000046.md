## Statement

Brand equity consists of added value that a brand gives to a product or service beyond its functional attributes.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a baseline definition for translating commercial brand equity into nonprofit behavioural value.

## Notes

The reviewed material presents this as broad consensus in marketing literature.

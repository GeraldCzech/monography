## Statement

Standard industrial classifications like ISIC systematically underestimate the heterogeneity of the nonprofit sector by collapsing diverse activities into a limited number of residual categories.

## Implications for BEBA

This justifies the need for a specialised classification beyond standard government codes to conduct meaningful BEBA research.

## Notes

The authors specifically critique the "catch-all" categories in ISIC/NACE for obscuring the sector's true diversity and economic significance.

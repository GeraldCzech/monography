## Statement

The effect of public observability on giving is moderated by cultural norms.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides strong evidence that the social-signaling component of a BEBA is culturally dependent and cannot be assumed.

## Notes

A study in Morocco found public observability *reduced* giving, counter to expectations in other contexts.

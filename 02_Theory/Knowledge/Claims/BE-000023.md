## Statement

Boenigk and Becker’s model defines nonprofit brand equity as a stakeholder-based relational asset that is conceptually distinct from behavioural outcomes.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides the clearest ontological foundation for BEBA’s distinction between brand architecture and behaviour.

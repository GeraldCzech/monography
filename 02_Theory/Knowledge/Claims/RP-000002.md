## Statement

In the nonprofit sector, brand-related perceptions are significantly associated with resource provider support intentions after controlling for general altruistic volunteerism.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s premise that nonprofit brands can influence behaviour beyond stakeholders’ pre-existing altruistic dispositions.

## Notes

Evidence is based on a single nonprofit organization and should be treated as exploratory.

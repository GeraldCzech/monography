## Statement

The effect of perceived brand differentiation on donation behavior is fully mediated by its influence on perceived trust, perceived efficacy, and identity congruence.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a precise, testable structural hypothesis for the core BEBA model, integrating multiple mechanisms.

## Notes

The claim of *full* mediation is a strong, falsifiable assertion that addresses a central point of uncertainty identified in the literature summary.

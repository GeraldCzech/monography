## Statement

The factor structure of brand personality varies across cultural contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim cautions BEBA against assuming that nonprofit brand meanings or personality dimensions are culturally universal.

## Notes

The reviewed material notes that brand personality dimensions differ between the United States, Japan, and Spain.

## Statement

Behavioural process models of brand equity are not sufficiently integrated with financial and business performance metrics in the reviewed literature.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies an opportunity for BEBA to distinguish behavioural effectiveness from financial valuation while specifying links between them.

## Notes

The synthesis identifies this as a research gap rather than as an empirically established finding.

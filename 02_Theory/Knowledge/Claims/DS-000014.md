## Statement

The reported effect size of social norms on charitable giving is at risk of being inflated due to publication bias in non-preregistered studies.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must treat the current effect size of social norms with caution and prioritize evidence from pre-registered research when calibrating models.

## Notes

The source meta-analysis found significant effects only in non-preregistered, published studies, which is the basis for this methodological claim.

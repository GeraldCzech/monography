## Statement

Donor satisfaction is a causal antecedent to donor-nonprofit organizational identification.

## Implications for BEBA

This defines a key causal direction, suggesting that satisfying brand interactions are a necessary foundation for building deeper identification.

## Notes

The study formally tests competing causal directions and finds strong support for the satisfaction -> identification path over the reverse.

## Statement

The primary and most encompassing dimension of nonprofit brand personality is 'Social Competence and Trust', which integrates traits of sincerity, reliability, and integrity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies 'Social Competence and Trust' as a candidate for a core construct in the BEBA ontology that links brand perception to behaviour.

## Notes

This dimension is presented as the most prominent factor emerging from the exploratory factor analysis.

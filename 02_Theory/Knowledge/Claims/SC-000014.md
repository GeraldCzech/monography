## Statement

The nonprofit sectors of developing countries cannot be accurately represented without a distinct category for development, housing, and community-building organisations.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the use of ICNPO as it demonstrates the system's sensitivity to international contexts, a key requirement for BEBA's generalisability.

## Notes

The creation of ICNPO Group 6 ("Development and Housing") is a key innovation over previous systems.

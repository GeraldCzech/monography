## Statement

A consumer's level of ecological consciousness alters the pathways and strength of brand-equity effects.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should consider value-based stakeholder orientations as moderators of brand-enabled pro-social behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis identifies ecological consciousness as a contextual boundary condition.

## Statement

A single measurement model for nonprofit brand equity is not invariant across key stakeholder groups such as donors, volunteers, and beneficiaries.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides direct empirical support for BEBA's core premise of a multi-stakeholder 'behaviour architecture' over a monolithic brand equity model.

## Notes

The lack of measurement invariance implies stakeholder-specific brand equity architectures are necessary.

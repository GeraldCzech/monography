## Statement

The effect of emotional appeals on donation intentions is moderated by the congruence between the appeal's framing and the donor's psychological orientation.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must account for audience segmentation and message-recipient congruence, as a one-size-fits-all communication strategy is unlikely to be effective.

## Notes

This adds a layer of sophistication to the general claim about emotional appeals (SVM-05).

## Statement

The behavioural effect of a nonprofit's brand personality is contingent on specific dimensions that signal competence, care, or shared values.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should not model brand personality as a monolithic construct, but must identify which facets are behaviourally relevant.

## Notes

This implies that not all positive personality traits will necessarily increase stakeholder support.

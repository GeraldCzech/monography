## Statement

Moral norms often predict charitable donation intention more strongly than subjective norms.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that BEBA should distinguish internal moral obligation from perceived social pressure when modelling brand-enabled behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed material characterises this as a recurring finding rather than a universal result.

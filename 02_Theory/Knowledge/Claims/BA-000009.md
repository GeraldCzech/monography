## Statement

Top-of-mind recall of a nonprofit brand is not a significant predictor of resource provider support intentions in Faircloth's exploratory study.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that salience alone may be insufficient for behavioural enablement in nonprofit branding.

## Notes

The finding concerns one dimension of awareness in a single-organisation study.

## Statement

Nonprofit brand valuation is used to estimate a brand’s capacity to mobilize financial resources and strengthen leverage in partnerships.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim connects brand valuation to behavioural efficacy by framing valuation as an attempt to quantify resource-mobilizing capacity.

## Notes

The reviewed material treats brand valuation as an emerging and contested practice.

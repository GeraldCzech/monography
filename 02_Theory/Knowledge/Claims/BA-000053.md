## Statement

The relationship between aided, unaided, and top-of-mind brand awareness measures follows a logarithmic rather than a linear pattern.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that BEBA measurement models should account for non-linear relationships among awareness indicators.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports replication and extension of the Laurent et al. awareness structure using whisky-brand data from the UK, Greece, and Taiwan.

## Statement

The successful implementation of a Brand Orientation strategy is negatively impacted by the presence of organizational silos.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies a critical implementation barrier (silos) that the BEBA framework must be explicitly designed to overcome.

## Notes

This is identified as a major impediment, suggesting cross-functional integration is a key success factor.

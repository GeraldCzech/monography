## Statement

In markets with informational asymmetry, consumer-based brand equity increases when consumers perceive a brand signal as credible.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s view that nonprofit brands become behaviourally effective by functioning as credible signals under uncertainty.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports empirical tests in jeans and juice categories.

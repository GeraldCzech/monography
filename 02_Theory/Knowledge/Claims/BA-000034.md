## Statement

Brand Attitude Strength predicts easy-to-perform brand-related behaviours as effectively as Brand Attachment.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should specify that attachment may be less necessary when the target behaviour imposes low resource demands.

## Notes

This claim applies to low enactment-difficulty behaviours.

## Statement

The direct effect of perceived brand differentiation on actual stakeholder donation behaviour is not sufficiently supported by the reviewed literature.

## Implications for BEBA

Identifies a major research gap that represents a high-value empirical target for the BEBA project to address.

## Notes

The confidence is 'high' in the statement about the lack of evidence. The review suggests any effect on behaviour may be mediated by perceived efficacy.

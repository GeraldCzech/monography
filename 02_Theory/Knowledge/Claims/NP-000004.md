## Statement

The measurement scale for non-profit brand image exhibits metric invariance across UK, Indian, and Bosnian-Herzegovinian national cultures.

## Implications for BEBA

This enables the BEBA framework to model and compare the relationships between brand image and other constructs (e.g., trust, behaviour) across these cultures.

## Notes

Metric invariance means that the scale items relate to the latent constructs with the same strength, implying a shared meaning.

## Statement

The positive effect of organisational identification on donor loyalty is comparable in magnitude to the direct positive effect of donor satisfaction.

## Implications for BEBA

This reinforces the need to include relational constructs like identification as key components of a brand-enabled behaviour architecture.

## Notes

Finding is specific to the context of a German blood donation service and the identification scale was adapted for the case partner.

## Statement

Donor identification with a nonprofit brand has a direct, positive effect on donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports modeling the brand as an identity resource that directly enables donation behaviour outside of purely deliberative pathways.

## Notes

The cited evidence suggests this effect holds even when controlling for core Theory of Planned Behavior constructs.

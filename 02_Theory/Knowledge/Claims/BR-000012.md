## Statement

Brand Responses positively predict Brand Resonance in the context of a nonprofit health-promotion brand.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the third link in the causal chain, showing that positive judgments and feelings are necessary to build a deep psychological bond with the brand.

## Notes

The study reported a standardized path coefficient of β = 0.586 (p < .001) for this relationship.

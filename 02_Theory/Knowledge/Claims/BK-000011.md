## Statement

Volunteer knowledge of a nonprofit brand is accumulated from national (Macro), local community (Micro), and personal (Mego) sources.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a structural model for the sources of brand information that BEBA must account for.

## Notes

Derived from a qualitative study with volunteers from large UK charities.

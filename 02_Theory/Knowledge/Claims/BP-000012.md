## Statement

Brand personality is more relevant as a brand equity measure when physical differentiation is low or social visibility is high.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim helps specify when nonprofit brand personality may matter most for supporter behaviour.

## Notes

The claim is presented as a boundary condition for using brand personality measures.

## Statement

An individual's habitus is the primary generator of their nonprofit supporter behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA's architecture must include a pre-reflexive, habit-driven pathway in addition to cognitive-deliberative pathways.

## Notes

This claim directly challenges purely rational-choice models of supporter behaviour.

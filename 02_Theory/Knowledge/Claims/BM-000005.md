## Statement

Brand Meaning positively predicts Brand Responses in the context of a nonprofit health-promotion brand.

## Implications for BEBA

This confirms the second link in the causal chain, where stakeholders' beliefs about the brand's function and imagery shape their judgments and feelings.

## Notes

The study reported a standardized path coefficient of β = 0.399 (p < .001) for this relationship.

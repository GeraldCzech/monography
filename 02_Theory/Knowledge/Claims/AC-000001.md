## Statement

Actual control, defined as environmental constraints and individual skills, is a critical moderator of the intention-behavior relationship.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates BEBA's focus on modeling environmental constraints and user skills as first-class components of the behavior architecture.

## Notes

The concept of 'Actual Control' bridges the gap between psychological intention and real-world enactment.

## Statement

When both are measured, injunctive norms are a stronger and more consistent predictor of donation intention than descriptive norms.

## Implications for BEBA

This implies BEBA must conceptually and operationally differentiate between norm types rather than using a single 'social influence' construct.

## Notes

This claim is specific to donation *intention*. A meta-analysis (Chapman et al., 2025) suggests this distinction is complex and may not hold as a unique moderator in multivariate models.

## Statement

A clearly defined internal brand identity fosters organisational cohesion among nonprofit staff and volunteers.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the BEBA assumption that brand identity functions as an internal behavioural coordination mechanism.

## Notes

The claim concerns internal stakeholders and should not be generalized to external stakeholder behaviour without further evidence.

## Statement

Multidimensional conceptualizations of brand authenticity risk confounding the core construct with its antecedents, consequences, or related constructs.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim cautions BEBA against using authenticity measures that combine brand perceptions with behavioural outcomes or antecedent conditions.

## Notes

The paper identifies dimensions such as familiarity, credibility, heritage, and personal utility as examples of potentially confounded elements.

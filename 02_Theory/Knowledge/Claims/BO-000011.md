## Statement

The direct effect of Brand Orientation on brand performance is non-significant in small firms.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests the impact of a BEBA may be contingent on organizational size, potentially requiring different implementation logic for smaller nonprofits.

## Notes

This finding is presented as a point of disagreement, suggesting firm size is a key moderating variable that qualifies the general performance link.

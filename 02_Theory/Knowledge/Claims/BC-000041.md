## Statement

A volunteer's Brand Commitment is more strongly correlated with their positive word-of-mouth behaviour than their Role Clarity is.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that fostering affective commitment may be a primary pathway to generating advocacy behaviours within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

Based on correlational data (r = .772 for commitment to positive WOM) from a single study.

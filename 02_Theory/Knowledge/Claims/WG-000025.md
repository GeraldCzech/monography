## Statement

The reviewed warm-glow literature insufficiently explains how repeated giving or donor habit changes the intensity or role of warm-glow motivation over time.

## Evidence

Not sufficiently covered in the reviewed literature.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should avoid assuming that warm-glow motivation remains stable across first-time, repeated, and habitual giving.

## Notes

Relevant to brand commitment and donor retention mechanisms.

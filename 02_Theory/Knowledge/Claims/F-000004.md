## Statement

Pro-social behaviour occurs within structured, competitive social spaces (fields) where organisations and individuals compete for legitimacy and position.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim defines the 'Market' layer in BEBA as a structured competitive field, shifting the focus from simple market share to relational positioning and legitimacy.

## Notes

This provides the theoretical basis for modelling the competitive environment in BEBA.

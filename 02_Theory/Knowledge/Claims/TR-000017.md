## Statement

The public display of a recognized, third-party accountability certification is positively associated with public trust in a nonprofit organization.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the BEBA premise that brands function as architectures of signals to reduce uncertainty for stakeholders.

## Notes

The specific signal studied was the German DZI Seal-of-Approval.

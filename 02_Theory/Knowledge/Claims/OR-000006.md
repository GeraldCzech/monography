## Statement

The positive effect of a nonprofit's reputation on volunteer retention is mediated by the feeling of organizational pride among its volunteers.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies pride as a critical affective mechanism linking brand constructs to sustained behavior in the 'retention' phase.

## Notes

This claim concerns retention (intent to continue) rather than initial attraction.

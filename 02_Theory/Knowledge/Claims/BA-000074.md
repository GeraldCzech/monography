## Statement

The "Good Samaritan" archetype functions as the public's dominant normative ideal for nonprofit organisations.

## Implications for BEBA

It defines the normative expectation that any effective BEBA must align with to succeed.

## Notes

The authors describe this ideal as a "persistent yardstick for judgment" against which actual NGO behaviour is measured.

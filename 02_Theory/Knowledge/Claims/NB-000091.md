## Statement

The Integrity dimension in nonprofit brand personality subsumes and reframes commercial brand sincerity, focusing on reliability, stewardship, and public commitment.

## Implications for BEBA

This refines a key construct, providing a more precise perceptual target for a nonprofit's behaviour architecture.

## Notes

This is a reconceptualization based on the study's qualitative and quantitative findings, moving from wholesomeness to accountability.

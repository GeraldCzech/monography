## Statement

The 18-item six-factor nonprofit brand image scale demonstrates reliability, convergent validity, discriminant validity, and nomological validity in a UK children's charity context.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies a candidate measurement instrument for operationalising nonprofit brand image in BEBA-related empirical work.

## Notes

Cross-national and cross-sector validation are not sufficiently covered in the reviewed literature.

## Statement

Volunteer brand commitment positively predicts volunteer satisfaction among formal nonprofit volunteers.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that affective attachment to the nonprofit brand contributes to positive volunteer experience outcomes.

## Notes

The evidence is cross-sectional.

## Statement

Donor-Nonprofit Identification and Donor Identity Salience are empirically distinct constructs in nonprofit donor behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model organizational identification and donor role salience as separate psychological mechanisms rather than as interchangeable identity constructs.

## Notes

The claim rests on one empirical study across two nonprofit contexts.

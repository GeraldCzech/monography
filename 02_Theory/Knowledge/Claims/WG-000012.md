## Statement

In cause-related marketing contexts, warm glow functions as a positive affective response that can mediate consumer outcomes such as purchase intention or loyalty.

## Evidence

Chaabouni et al. (2020); Giebelhausen et al. (2017); Wu et al. (2022)

## Implications for BEBA

Warm glow may explain behaviour in hybrid nonprofit-commercial contexts where brand equity and prosocial affect overlap.

## Notes

Relevant if BEBA addresses charity partnerships or corporate giving interfaces.

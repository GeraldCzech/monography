## Statement

Brand awareness or familiarity alone is insufficient to generate positive nonprofit brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This prevents BEBA from treating salience alone as behaviourally effective without trust, commitment, or positive associations.

## Notes

The reviewed material notes that familiarity can have non-significant or negative effects in some studies.

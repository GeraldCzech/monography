## Statement

An augmented American Customer Satisfaction Index (ACSI) model, incorporating organisational identification, is a valid causal model for donor loyalty in a nonprofit service context.

## Implications for BEBA

This demonstrates a viable method for building a BEBA by adapting robust for-profit models to the specificities of the nonprofit sector.

## Notes

Validity is demonstrated within a single study using PLS-SEM on self-selected online survey data from a blood donation service.

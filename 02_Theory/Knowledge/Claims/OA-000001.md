## Statement

Organizational associations are more important brand equity measures for services, durable goods, and corporate brands than for some other product-market contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports treating organizational trust, credibility, and admiration as especially important in nonprofit brand equity.

## Notes

The claim is context-dependent and requires nonprofit-specific validation.

## Statement

Perceived familial benefit from a donation has a significant positive effect on giving intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests specific types of perceived benefits are relevant drivers of intention that may interact with brand perceptions.

## Notes

This effect was not observed for demonstrable (self-oriented) or emotional benefits.

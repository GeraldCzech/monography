## Statement

Psychological habit automaticity has a significant predictive effect on future actual donation behaviour, independent of donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

Validating this claim is essential for justifying the inclusion of a distinct psychological 'habit' pathway in the BEBA model.

## Notes

The reviewed literature identifies this as a high-priority research gap, noting a near-complete absence of studies that correctly operationalise and test habit as automaticity against observed behaviour.

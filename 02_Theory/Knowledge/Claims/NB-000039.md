## Statement

Nonprofit brand personality has a direct positive effect on an individual's giving intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the BEBA proposition that nonprofit brand characteristics can directly enable donation-related behavioural intention.

## Notes

Evidence is based on cross-sectional survey data from China and concerns giving intention rather than observed giving behaviour.

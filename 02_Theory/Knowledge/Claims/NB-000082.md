## Statement

The perceived typicality of a nonprofit within its cause category is a significant positive predictor of an individual's intention to donate money.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies typicality as a key heuristic within the BEBA that enables behaviour by reducing cognitive load and conferring legitimacy.

## Notes

Typicality independently explained 29% of the variance in monetary giving intentions, separate from brand image.

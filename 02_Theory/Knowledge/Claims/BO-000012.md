## Statement

The interaction between Brand Orientation and Entrepreneurial Orientation on market performance is context-dependent.

## Implications for BEBA

The implementation of a BEBA must account for its interaction with other prevailing strategic orientations within the nonprofit.

## Notes

The reviewed literature presents contradictory findings on the nature of the interaction (synergistic vs. antagonistic), supporting the claim that the relationship is complex and context-specific.

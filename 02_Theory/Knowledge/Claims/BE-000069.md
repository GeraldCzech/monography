## Statement

A high-equity nonprofit brand can partially mitigate the identifiable victim effect, increasing stakeholder willingness to contribute to solving large-scale, statistically-defined problems.

## Implications for BEBA

This proposes a key mechanism through which a BEBA can overcome known cognitive biases in giving.

## Notes

This is a candidate claim proposed by the BEBA project itself for future testing.

## Statement

Large-scale philanthropy functions as a private exercise of power that influences public policy outside of democratic processes.

## Implications for BEBA

This introduces a critical, normative dimension to brand-enabled behaviour, questioning its social legitimacy.

## Notes

This claim is central to political philosophy critiques of philanthropy.

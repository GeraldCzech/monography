## Statement

For-profit brand equity concepts can be empirically applied to nonprofit resource provider support intentions.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA's use of brand equity theory as a basis for nonprofit behavioural architecture.

## Notes

The empirical application is exploratory and limited to one nonprofit context.

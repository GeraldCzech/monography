## Statement

Adding a distinct descriptive norm construct to models based on the Theory of Planned Behaviour improves the explained variance in intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA's more granular approach to social influence, avoiding the under-specification of normative factors found in older models.

## Notes

The TPB's "subjective norm" construct is widely considered an operationalization of injunctive norms, making the addition of descriptive norms necessary.

## Statement

Direct transfer of for-profit brand equity models to nonprofit organizations is insufficient because such models do not adequately account for public trust and accountability.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies developing a nonprofit-specific brand equity architecture rather than importing commercial brand equity dimensions unchanged.

## Notes

The claim challenges reliance on general brand image and brand personality as central nonprofit brand equity dimensions.

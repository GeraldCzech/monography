## Statement

The relationship between donor loyalty and actual donation behavior is weak and context-dependent in the nonprofit contexts studied by Boenigk and Helmig.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should distinguish loyalty as a proximal behavioural disposition from actual donation behavior as a constrained behavioural outcome.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports limited explanatory power for actual donations.

## Statement

The influence of descriptive social norms on pro-social intention is moderated by referent group proximity, with proximal groups being more influential than distal groups.

## Implications for BEBA

This translates into a design principle for brand communication, suggesting brands should signal norms from proximal or highly relevant groups.

## Notes

This is a key boundary condition for the effectiveness of normative appeals.

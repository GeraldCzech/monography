## Statement

A valid measurement scale for brand authenticity should measure perceived category exemplarity rather than multiple heterogeneous authenticity dimensions.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim informs BEBA measurement design by prioritising construct validity and parsimony.

## Notes

The paper proposes sample items but identifies scale development as a future research need.

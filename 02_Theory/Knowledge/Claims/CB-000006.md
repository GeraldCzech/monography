## Statement

Consumer-based brand equity develops through a sequential multi-stage process rather than functioning as a static monolithic construct.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s premise that brand equity should be modelled as an architecture of behavioural enablement rather than as a single static asset.

## Notes

The study is cross-sectional, so the proposed sequential process is modelled rather than directly observed over time.

## Statement

The perceived Nurturance of a nonprofit brand is positively correlated with an individual's likelihood of contributing.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies Nurturance as a second key perceptual lever within the brand architecture for influencing donor behavior.

## Notes

The study measured self-reported likelihood to contribute (money, time, or goods), not actual donation behavior.

## Statement

In the NGO context, brand associations have a significant positive effect on donor-based brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s view that nonprofit brand effectiveness depends on evaluative meaning structures rather than mere recognition.

## Notes

Evidence is based on a PLS-SEM study using a Spanish convenience sample focused on childhood-related NGOs.

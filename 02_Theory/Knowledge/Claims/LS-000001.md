## Statement

Brand equity measures are more sensitive and interpretable when analyzed separately for loyalty segments than when analyzed only as aggregate market scores.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim implies that BEBA should distinguish loyal supporters, occasional supporters, switchers, and non-supporters when interpreting nonprofit brand equity.

## Notes

The claim concerns measurement design rather than a causal mechanism.

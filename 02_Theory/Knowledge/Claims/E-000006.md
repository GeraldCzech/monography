## Statement

The influence of pre-purchase expectations on satisfaction formation diminishes as brand familiarity increases.

## Implications for BEBA

This qualifies expectation-based accounts of behavioural effectiveness by showing that expectations matter differently across familiarity states.

## Notes

The reviewed study reports an attenuated role of prior expectations for high-familiarity customers.

## Statement

Existing nonprofit brand equity models are primarily developed for individual donors or the general public rather than for corporate partners, foundations, or government agencies.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should treat stakeholder type as a boundary condition for brand-enabled behavioural mechanisms.

## Statement

The strategic orientations of a nonprofit organization, specifically market orientation and brand orientation, can be constructed and measured as valid and reliable perceptual constructs from the customer's (member's) perspective.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a methodological precedent for operationalizing and measuring core BEBA concepts from the stakeholder's point of view.

## Notes

The "Perceived Brand Orientation" (PBO) scale was newly developed for this study in a specific nonprofit context (churches).

## Statement

Warm glow utility does not fully explain charitable giving in some religious contexts where duty or salvation motives are salient.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim cautions BEBA against treating warm glow as a universal mechanism across all cultural and religious giving systems.

## Notes

The reviewed material presents this as a debated limitation of the standard impure altruism model.

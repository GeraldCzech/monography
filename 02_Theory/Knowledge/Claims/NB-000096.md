## Statement

A stakeholder-based brand equity model incorporating context-specific dimensions like Brand Trust provides a more valid conceptualization for the nonprofit sector than direct applications of generic for-profit models.

## Implications for BEBA

This justifies BEBA's core premise of developing a sector-specific theory rather than uncritically adapting commercial models.

## Notes

This claim represents the central theoretical argument against the 'full transferability' of for-profit brand equity models.

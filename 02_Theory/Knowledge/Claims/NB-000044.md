## Statement

Nonprofit brand image does not have a significant effect on an individual donor's self-concept.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA by suggesting that not all brand equity dimensions activate identity-based behavioural pathways.

## Notes

Evidence is based on cross-sectional survey data from China and may depend on the study's operationalisation of brand image.

## Statement

For-profit brand management principles can be adapted into a valid and parsimonious measurement model for the nonprofit sector.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim validates BEBA's approach of adapting and integrating established branding theories for the nonprofit context.

## Notes

The study adapted Keller's (2000) brand report card, demonstrating that while a direct one-to-one transfer may be inappropriate, a careful adaptation is effective.

## Statement

A significant gap exists between individuals' stated intention to donate and their actual donation behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This gap is the primary theoretical problem that the BEBA framework is designed to explain and address.

## Notes

Models that predict intention well are significantly weaker at predicting behavior.

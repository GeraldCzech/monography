## Statement

In the context of charitable giving, the influence of injunctive norms on donation intention is stronger than the influence of descriptive norms.

## Implications for BEBA

This finding guides BEBA to model injunctive norms as potentially more potent drivers than descriptive norms within the specific donation context.

## Notes

This contrasts with broader consumer and health domains, where descriptive norms can be equally or more predictive of actual behavior.

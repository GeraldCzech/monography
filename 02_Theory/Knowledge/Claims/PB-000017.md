## Statement

Past donation behaviour can be negatively associated with future donation intentions under certain conditions.

## Implications for BEBA

This finding poses a significant challenge to simple models of habit formation within the behavioural architecture.

## Notes

This is an anomalous empirical finding that contradicts established habituation theory (e.g., Ouellette & Wood, 1998) and requires further investigation.

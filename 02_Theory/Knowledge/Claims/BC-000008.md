## Statement

The perceived character of a nonprofit brand is not a significant predictor of resource provider support intentions in Faircloth's exploratory study.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA by indicating that not all favourable brand associations necessarily translate into support intentions.

## Notes

The finding is study-specific and should not be generalised without replication.

## Statement

The act of donating to a nonprofit organization positively reinforces the donor's perception of brand equity, an effect mediated by the donor's strengthened commitment and brand-self congruence.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim specifies the core bidirectional mechanism BEBA seeks to model, including its psychological pathway.

## Notes

This mechanism is widely theorized but lacks sufficient empirical testing with bidirectional research designs.

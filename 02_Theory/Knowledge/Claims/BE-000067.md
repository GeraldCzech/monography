## Statement

A nonprofit's brand serves as the institutional locus facilitating the conversion of a donor's economic capital into symbolic capital.

## Implications for BEBA

This directly links the abstract concept of symbolic capital conversion to the tangible asset of a nonprofit's brand.

## Notes

This is a BEBA-specific interpretation of the sociological mechanism identified by Bourdieu.

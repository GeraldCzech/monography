## Statement

Brand equity is not a mandatory component of the Integrated Model of Behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should position brand equity as an enabling or enhancing architecture rather than as a universal requirement of behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed material frames brand equity as an enhancer of IBM rather than as a core IBM construct.

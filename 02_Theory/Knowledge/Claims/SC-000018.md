## Statement

The conversion of a donor's economic capital into symbolic capital is moderated by the nonprofit's symbolic capital.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the core mechanism for why a nonprofit brand has value to a donor beyond its functional aspects.

## Notes

This is a core theoretical proposition from the framework, but the review notes direct empirical evidence for this process is sparse.

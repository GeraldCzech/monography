## Statement

Positive stakeholder attitudes toward marketing are positively associated with perceptions of the nonprofit's brand orientation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies a permissive cultural factor (pro-marketing attitude) that may enable the development of a BEBA.

## Notes

Confidence is low as this finding is based on a single study using a marketing attitude scale with questionable reliability (alpha = .679).

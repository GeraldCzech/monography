## Statement

Digital brand equity requires metrics such as share of search, digital awareness, and online sentiment that are not captured by traditional brand equity measurement alone.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that BEBA may need to model digital signals as behavioural affordances or indicators rather than relying only on legacy survey constructs.

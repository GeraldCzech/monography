## Statement

The effect of stakeholder trust on donation behaviour is moderated by other variables.

## Implications for BEBA

This directly informs the need for BEBA to model the contingent nature of the trust-behaviour link as a core theoretical challenge.

## Notes

This is a proposed explanation for the inconsistent findings documented in claim TR-01, based on the review's call for research into moderators.

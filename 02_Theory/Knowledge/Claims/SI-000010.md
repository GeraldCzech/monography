## Statement

Volunteering practices reproduce existing social inequalities by favouring individuals with higher cultural and social capital.

## Implications for BEBA

The BEBA model should not assume uniform access or motivation for brand engagement across different social strata.

## Notes

This highlights that nonprofit engagement is not inherently an equalising force in society.

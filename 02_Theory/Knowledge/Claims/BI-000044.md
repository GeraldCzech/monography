## Statement

The scientific measurement of brand image is characterized by high methodological fragmentation, with the majority of studies employing unique, non-standardized scales.

## Implications for BEBA

This fragmentation justifies BEBA's research principle of establishing one canonical definition per construct to ensure internal consistency.

## Notes

Based on the finding from a systematic review (1991-2016) that most measurement scales were applied in only a single study.

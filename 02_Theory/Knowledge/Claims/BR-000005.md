## Statement

Consumer psychology of brands can be organized around five core processes: identifying, experiencing, integrating, signifying, and connecting.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim gives BEBA a process vocabulary for describing how nonprofit brands become psychologically meaningful before they become behaviourally effective.

## Notes

The five-process structure is proposed as an integrative theoretical model rather than as a fully validated empirical taxonomy.

## Statement

Past volunteering behavior is a positive and significant predictor of future volunteering behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This underpins the architectural importance of designing for repeated action, as past behavior is a key ingredient for future behavior.

## Notes

BEBA posits this effect is largely mediated by the identity reinforcement loop (see VRI-03), but the direct association is a robust finding.

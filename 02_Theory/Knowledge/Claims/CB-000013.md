## Statement

Higher long-term advertising investment is positively associated with higher consumer-based brand equity among objectively similar brands.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s assumption that communication investment can build cognitive brand structures that later enable behaviour.

## Notes

The study uses advertising expenditure as a proxy for communication investment and does not definitively establish causality.

## Statement

Fundraising appeals framed around donor benefit outperform recipient-only appeals in increasing giving.

## Evidence

List et al. (2021); Ferguson et al. (2023)

## Implications for BEBA

Situational appeal framing can influence donation behaviour independently of stable nonprofit brand equity.

## Notes

Important for model competition between appeal-level and brand-level explanations.

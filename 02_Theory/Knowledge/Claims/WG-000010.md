## Statement

Individuals derive private, self-regarding utility (warm glow) from the act of giving itself, independent of the outcome for the recipient.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies a key psychological motive that a brand architecture must accommodate and potentially facilitate.

## Notes

Based on the economic theory of "impure altruism."

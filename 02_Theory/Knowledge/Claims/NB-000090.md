## Statement

Nurturance is a core dimension of nonprofit brand personality that is not prominent in for-profit models.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies a unique architectural component specific to the prosocial exchange context of nonprofits.

## Notes

The emergence of this dimension highlights the importance of perceived mission alignment in the nonprofit sector.

## Statement

Nonprofit brand orientation can be conceptualised as a behavioural construct expressed through internal activities, structures, and practices that build and sustain the brand.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a theoretical bridge between brand orientation and BEBA’s behaviour-centred architecture.

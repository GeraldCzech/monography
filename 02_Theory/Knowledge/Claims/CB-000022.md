## Statement

The psychological value of a brand to a consumer is a latent, multi-dimensional phenomenon that can be reliably measured through psychometric scales.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the project's core methodological approach of using psychometric scales to measure nonprofit brand equity and its antecedents.

## Notes

The existence of a comprehensive handbook compiling numerous validated scales serves as strong evidence for this claim.

## Statement

The six-dimensional factor structure of non-profit brand image exhibits configural invariance across UK, Indian, and Bosnian-Herzegovinian national cultures.

## Implications for BEBA

This finding supports the BEBA premise that a common brand architecture can be applied and understood across different cultural contexts.

## Notes

Configural invariance suggests that individuals in these different cultures use the same conceptual framework for non-profit brand image.

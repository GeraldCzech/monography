## Statement

Brand equity literature is divided between financial approaches that value brands as assets and customer-based approaches that explain brand effects through consumer cognition and response.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA's need to connect cognitive mechanisms with resource outcomes rather than privileging only one paradigm.

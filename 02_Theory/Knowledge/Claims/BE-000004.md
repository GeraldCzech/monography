## Statement

Customer-based brand equity is a multi-dimensional construct that includes perceptual inputs, relational aspects, and quality perceptions.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model nonprofit brand equity as a multidimensional perception architecture rather than as a single undifferentiated construct.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis identifies awareness, image, trust, loyalty, and perceived quality as recurring dimensions.

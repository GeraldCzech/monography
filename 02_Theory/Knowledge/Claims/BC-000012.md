## Statement

Object-centered brand engagement is associated with brand attitude, self-centered brand engagement is associated with brand attachment, and social brand engagement is associated with brand community.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s distinction between weaker evaluative connections and stronger personal or collective forms of brand-enabled behaviour.

## Notes

The statement reflects Schmitt’s conceptual mapping of connecting constructs across engagement levels.

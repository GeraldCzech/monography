## Statement

Consumer-based brand equity can be operationalized as a three-dimensional construct comprising Brand Loyalty, Perceived Quality, and a combined Brand Awareness/Associations factor.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a validated, parsimonious structure for measuring brand equity that can be adapted for the nonprofit context.

## Notes

This operationalization is derived from a multistep psychometric validation process and serves as a refinement of Aaker's (1991) and Keller's (1993) models.

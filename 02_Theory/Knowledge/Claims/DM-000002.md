## Statement

High-involvement decisions, such as choosing a regular volunteer role, do not necessarily entail a complex, rational, or comparative evaluation of alternatives.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies BEBA's focus on heuristics and automaticity, even for high-commitment behaviours.

## Notes

This contrasts with standard rational choice models that link high involvement to high-effort decision processes.

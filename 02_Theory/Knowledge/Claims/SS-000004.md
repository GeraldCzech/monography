## Statement

Social and health services form the economic core of the Austrian nonprofit sector in terms of value added, employment, and revenue.

## Implications for BEBA

This qualifies the target of analysis, suggesting BEBA models must differentiate between the welfare core and other, less-researched nonprofit sub-sectors.

## Notes

Findings from this core sub-sector may not be generalizable to the entire Austrian nonprofit landscape.

## Statement

There is no significant direct statistical relationship between stakeholder attitudes toward marketing and their perceptions of a nonprofit's organizational effectiveness.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim strengthens the argument for an architectural-level construct like BEBA, as simple pro-marketing attitudes are shown to be insufficient.

## Notes

This is a null finding from a single study. The finding is potentially weakened by the low reliability of the marketing attitude scale.

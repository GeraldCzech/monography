## Statement

A positive attitude toward a charity mediates the relationship between nonprofit brand perceptions and intention to donate.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim specifies attitude formation as a pathway through which nonprofit brands translate perception into behavioural intention.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis presents attitude as a key mediator but does not settle the relative size of direct versus indirect effects.

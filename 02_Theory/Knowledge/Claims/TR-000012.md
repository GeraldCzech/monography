## Statement

International nonprofit brands function as trust proxies for donors who cannot directly observe the services or impact produced for beneficiaries.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s premise that nonprofit brands enable behaviour under uncertainty by reducing perceived risk for resource providers.

## Notes

Based on qualitative case evidence from ten international nonprofit organizations.

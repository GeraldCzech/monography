## Statement

Student identification with a university brand increases brand-supportive behaviours such as positive word-of-mouth, advocacy, and loyalty.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim specifies identification as a psychological mechanism through which nonprofit brands enable behaviour.

## Notes

The claim is grounded in Social Identity Theory as applied to university branding.

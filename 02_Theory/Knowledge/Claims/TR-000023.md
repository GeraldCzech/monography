## Statement

Organizational age is a weak but positive predictor of public trust in a nonprofit organization.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the role of organizational heritage and perceived stability as a trust-building signal within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

The effect size for this predictor was small but statistically significant.

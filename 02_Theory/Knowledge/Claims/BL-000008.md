## Statement

Brand loyalty results from brand awareness and brand associations rather than constituting a direct component of brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This affects whether repeat donation or volunteering should be modelled as a BEBA outcome rather than as part of brand equity itself.

## Notes

Campbell’s position contrasts with Aaker’s model, which treats brand loyalty as a core brand equity asset.

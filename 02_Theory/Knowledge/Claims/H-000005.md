## Statement

The components of the Theory of Planned Behaviour (attitudes, subjective norms, perceived behavioural control) are shaped by the individual's habitus.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a theoretical bridge for creating a dual-process BEBA model where habitus is an antecedent to TPB constructs.

This claim represents a theoretical synthesis proposed in the summary rather than an established finding from a specific cited source.

## Statement

Nonprofit Brand Orientation mediates the relationship between stakeholder attitudes toward marketing and perceived organizational effectiveness.

## Implications for BEBA

This proposes a testable mediation model that places a BEBA-like construct as the central causal mechanism for translating attitudes into outcomes.

## Notes

This is an untested hypothesis derived from the study's correlational findings, not an empirically verified mediation.

## Statement

The direct influence of satisfaction on behavioural intentions is stronger for individuals with high brand familiarity than for individuals with low brand familiarity.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the idea that familiar brands can make satisfaction function as a behavioural shortcut.

## Notes

Behavioural intentions include repurchase and recommendation in the reviewed study.

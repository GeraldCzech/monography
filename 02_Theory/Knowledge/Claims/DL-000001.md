## Statement

Donor loyalty is simultaneously and directly influenced by donor satisfaction, donor-nonprofit identification, and donor identity salience.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA can represent loyalty as a convergence point for experiential, organizational-identity, and role-identity mechanisms.

## Statement

The psychological antecedents for donating time differ systematically from the antecedents for donating money.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA models must differentiate between different target behaviours (e.g., donating time vs. money) as they are driven by distinct architectures.

## Notes

The original source suggests donating time is more influenced by social and emotional factors, while donating money is more utility-oriented.

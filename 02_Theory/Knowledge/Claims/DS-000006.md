## Statement

Analysing different pro-social behaviours (e.g., donating money vs. donating time) in separate models risks generating misleading donor profiles by failing to differentiate between single-behaviour and multiple-behaviour individuals.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the methodological imperative within BEBA to model multiple behavioural outcomes simultaneously.

## Notes

This is a core methodological conclusion of the source paper, challenging a common practice in prior research.

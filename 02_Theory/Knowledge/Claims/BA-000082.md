## Statement

The behavioural effectiveness of a nonprofit brand is a function of its architecture's capacity to simultaneously satisfy stakeholder motivations for affective rewards, social recognition, and perceived efficacy.

## Implications for BEBA

This defines the "architecture" in BEBA as a multi-motive appeal structure.

## Notes

This is a candidate claim proposed by the BEBA project itself for future testing.

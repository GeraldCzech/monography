## Statement

The independent effect of descriptive norms on donation behaviour, when controlling for injunctive norms, is not reliably established.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model the link from descriptive norms to behaviour with caution, possibly as mediated or moderated, rather than as a strong direct effect.

## Notes

This reflects an area of disagreement in the literature, with some studies showing no effect after controlling for injunctive norms.

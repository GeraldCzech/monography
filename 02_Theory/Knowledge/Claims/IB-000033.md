## Statement

A substantial discrepancy exists between stated behavioral intentions and subsequent actions across diverse domains.

## Implications for BEBA

This establishes the core problem that BEBA must address: generating intent is insufficient for achieving behavioral outcomes.

## Notes

This gap is a structural problem for intention-based behavioral models.

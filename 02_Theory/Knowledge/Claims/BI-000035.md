## Statement

Effective nonprofit brand building requires internal organizational capabilities, processes, and cultural attributes that enable the brand to be constructed, communicated, and managed.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s architecture premise that nonprofit brand effectiveness depends on internal enabling conditions rather than external communication alone.

## Notes

Based on an exploratory qualitative study of 20 senior marketing personnel in UK nonprofit organizations.

## Statement

The effects of satisfaction and identification on donor loyalty are stronger for older donors and higher-income donors in the blood donation context studied by Boenigk and Helmig.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should consider demographic heterogeneity when modeling satisfaction-based and identification-based loyalty mechanisms.

## Notes

This claim is based on the multigroup analysis described in the reviewed material.

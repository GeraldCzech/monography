## Statement

Organisational associations can be empirically distinguished from perceived value and brand personality within a consumer-based brand equity measurement model.

## Implications for BEBA

This is relevant because nonprofit brands are often closely identified with organisational trust, credibility, and liking.

## Notes

The original study tested company-related associations in consumer product contexts, not nonprofit organisational associations.

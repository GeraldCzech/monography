## Statement

In cause-related marketing, small donation amounts can enhance warm glow, whereas large visible donation amounts may increase skepticism and reduce positive consumer responses.

## Evidence

Chaabouni et al. (2020)

## Implications for BEBA

Perceived authenticity, skepticism, and donation framing may interact in nonprofit-brand-related giving contexts.

## Notes

This claim is context-specific to cause-related marketing.

## Statement

Fundraising appeals that emphasize the donor's emotional benefit generate higher charitable response than appeals focused solely on recipient benefit.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the idea that nonprofit brands can architect behaviour by making donor-experienced emotional utility salient.

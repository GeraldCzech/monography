## Statement

Public perception of nonprofit brands is primarily structured by two opposing archetypes: the "Good Samaritan" (altruistic, mission-driven) and the "Marketer" (corporate, self-serving).

## Implications for BEBA

This finding establishes the key perceptual archetypes that a BEBA must successfully navigate.

## Notes

Finding is based on thematic analysis of UK focus groups; the archetypes are empirically derived from this context.

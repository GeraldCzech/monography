## Statement

The Interaction and Orchestration dimensions of NBO are positively associated with a nonprofit's perceived ability to provide superior value to its stakeholders.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim links specific BEBA architectural components (feedback loops, internal consistency) to the creation of stakeholder value.

## Notes

The 'Affect' dimension was not found to be a significant predictor for this specific performance criterion (providing superior stakeholder value).

## Statement

Perceived organisational support from a nonprofit is a positive predictor of volunteer retention.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies an experiential, brand-delivery factor that BEBA must account for in its retention architecture.

## Notes

The cited evidence for this claim comes from a study focusing on mandatory volunteers, which is a key boundary condition.

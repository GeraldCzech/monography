## Statement

High perceived fit between a sponsor and a nonprofit positively influences stakeholder behavioural intentions, including donating, volunteering, and recommending.

## Implications for BEBA

This directly supports BEBA's core thesis that the brand architecture's primary function is to enable and guide stakeholder behaviour.

## Notes

The study measured behavioural intentions, not actual behaviour, in a single-exposure experimental setting.

## Statement

The empirical lack of discriminant validity between brand awareness and brand associations stems from a conceptual dependency, where awareness is a necessary precondition for the formation of associations.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model brand knowledge as a process or a causally dependent structure, not as a set of independent factors.

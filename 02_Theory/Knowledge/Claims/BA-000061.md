## Statement

In new donor acquisition for international aid charities, favourable donor attitude toward a nonprofit brand positively predicts intention to donate to that brand.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA by identifying brand attitude as an evaluative pathway through which nonprofit brands become behaviourally effective.

## Notes

The outcome is donation intention rather than observed donation behaviour.

## Statement

The Interaction, Orchestration, and Affect dimensions of NBO are all positively associated with a nonprofit's perceived ability to achieve its goals.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim demonstrates that all three measured components of the internal BEBA contribute to organisational goal attainment.

## Notes

Performance was measured as the self-reported ability to achieve short- and long-term goals.

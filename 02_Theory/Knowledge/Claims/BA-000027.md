## Statement

NGO brand associations should represent moral values and objectives rather than only brand personality traits.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should encode NGO brand meaning in terms of moral and cause-related associations rather than generic personality dimensions.

## Notes

The reviewed literature is fragmented on the specific form of nonprofit brand associations.

## Statement

For nonprofit brands, attitudinal commitment is a more robust measure of donor loyalty than exclusive repeat-donation frequency.

## Implications for BEBA

This encourages BEBA to prioritize modeling psychological commitment as the key state variable for long-term donor value over transactional metrics.

## Notes

This is a conceptual claim about measurement and theory, reflecting an interpretive consensus rather than direct comparative empirical evidence.

## Statement

For brand non-users, brand knowledge has a weak or non-existent relationship with purchase propensity.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should distinguish awareness among non-supporters from behaviourally effective brand knowledge among existing supporters.

## Notes

The reviewed summary describes this as a boundary condition of the proposed mechanism.

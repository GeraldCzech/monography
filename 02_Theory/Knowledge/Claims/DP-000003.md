## Statement

Cognitive-intentional models best explain supporter behaviour in novel situations, while practice-based models best explain established, routine support behaviours.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the core logic for when to apply the "acquisition path" versus the "retention path" in the BEBA model.

## Notes

This frames the two theoretical traditions (rational choice vs. practice theory) as complementary rather than mutually exclusive.

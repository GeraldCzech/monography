## Statement

A 10-item scale composed of three loyalty items, two perceived quality items, and five awareness/associations items demonstrates metric invariance across US and Korean student samples, allowing for valid cross-cultural comparison of its latent dimensions.

## Implications for BEBA

It provides a methodological blueprint for testing the cross-context validity of BEBA's own measurement instruments (e.g., across sectors or stakeholder groups).

## Notes

Invariance was established using multi-group confirmatory factor analysis. The scale is known as the Multidimensional Brand Equity (MBE) scale.

## Statement

Models of brand equity formation that include brand trust as a mediating variable have better explanatory power than more parsimonious satisfaction-loyalty models.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides evidence that including trust as a core mechanism in the BEBA architecture is empirically necessary for a better explanation.

## Notes

The superior fit was demonstrated via SEM fit indices in the original study.

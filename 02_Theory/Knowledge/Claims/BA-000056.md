## Statement

For low market-share brands, recall-based awareness measures remain consistently low while aided awareness shows greater variation over time.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that emerging nonprofit brands may need recognition-based measures to capture early-stage mental availability.

## Notes

The reviewed material characterises low recall scores for small brands as stable because they remain near the bottom of the awareness curve.

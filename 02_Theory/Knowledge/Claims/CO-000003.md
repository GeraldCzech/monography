## Statement

The effect of government funding on private charitable donations is empirically indeterminate across studies.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should avoid assuming a universal relationship between public funding signals and private donor behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports evidence for crowding out, crowding in, and neutral effects.

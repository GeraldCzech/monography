## Statement

A brand cannot become a strategic organisational asset unless its identity, purpose, and values are explicitly defined.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes explicit brand meaning as a prerequisite for coordinated brand-enabled behaviour.

## Notes

The claim concerns internal strategic clarity and does not by itself establish external stakeholder response.

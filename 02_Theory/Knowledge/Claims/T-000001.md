## Statement

Perceived trustworthiness is not a significant predictor of propensity to donate when familiarity is accounted for in Katz's national study.

## Implications for BEBA

This challenges a universal trust-centred account and indicates that trust effects may depend on measurement and study context.

## Notes

The reviewed material suggests the discrepancy may reflect methodological differences, sample characteristics, or the specific measurement of trustworthiness.

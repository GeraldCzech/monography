## Statement

The self-image component of warm glow arises from donors’ motivation to maintain or enhance their self-concept as generous, moral, or socially responsible persons.

## Evidence

Grossman & Levy (2024)

## Implications for BEBA

Self-image may function as a bridge between donor identity, brand-self congruence, and giving behaviour.

## Notes

Relevant to identity-based nonprofit brand effects.

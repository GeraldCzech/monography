## Statement

Measurement invariance testing is required before consumer-based brand equity scores are compared across national samples.

## Implications for BEBA

This establishes a methodological requirement for any BEBA study comparing nonprofit brand equity across countries or cultural contexts.

## Notes

The claim is methodological and follows from the study’s use of multi-group confirmatory factor analysis.

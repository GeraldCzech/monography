## Statement

The conceptual dimensions of Employee Brand Equity (EBE) are applicable to formal volunteers in nonprofit organizations when contextually adapted.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim validates using an established internal branding framework as a concrete BEBA implementation for volunteers.

## Notes

The study is exploratory and limited to the Portuguese context, requiring further confirmatory and cross-cultural validation.

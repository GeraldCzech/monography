## Statement

The nomological role of exemplar-based brand authenticity in relation to brand strength and behavioural outcomes remains empirically unresolved.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that BEBA should treat authenticity’s mediating or moderating role as a candidate mechanism rather than a settled proposition.

## Notes

The reviewed material explicitly raises whether authenticity mediates or moderates the effects of brand strength on target outcomes.

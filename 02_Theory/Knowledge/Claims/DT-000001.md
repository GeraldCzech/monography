## Statement

Platform credibility and trust in the system are necessary preconditions for the Theory of Planned Behavior logic to apply in digital donation and crowdfunding contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that digital brand-enabled behaviour depends on infrastructure-level credibility as well as organization-level brand trust.

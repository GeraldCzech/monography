## Statement

Moral identification is more directly applicable to cause-based advocacy NGOs than to nonprofit organisations whose support depends primarily on service reliability or effectiveness.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model nonprofit type as a boundary condition for the relative importance of identity-based brand mechanisms.

## Notes

This claim is supported as a theoretical boundary condition in the reviewed synthesis rather than as a directly tested comparative finding.

## Statement

Acute emotional responses such as empathy or guilt can trigger charitable donation behavior among individuals without prior donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim extends BEBA by requiring a cue-driven pathway to charitable behaviour in addition to an intention-driven pathway.

## Statement

The relative contribution of brand equity dimensions to the overall brand equity construct varies across cultures; Perceived Quality is the most heavily weighted dimension for US consumers, while Brand Loyalty is most heavily weighted for Korean consumers.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that the relative importance of BEBA's architectural components may vary across different societal or stakeholder contexts.

## Notes

This finding is derived from the factor weights of a higher-order factor model and, while statistically significant, is specific to the student samples and cultures studied.

## Statement

Low perceived fit between a nonprofit and a for-profit sponsor weakens or dilutes the nonprofit's brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This demonstrates that incoherent architectural elements (like a poorly-aligned partner) can actively damage the BEBA's structure and function.

## Notes

This challenges the operational assumption that any corporate funding is inherently beneficial for a nonprofit.

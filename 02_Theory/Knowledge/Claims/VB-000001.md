## Statement

The 13-dimensional Employee Brand Equity model is empirically applicable to formal volunteers in nonprofit organizations after context-specific linguistic and conceptual adaptation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports Volunteer Brand Equity as a candidate BEBA concept for modelling internal stakeholder behaviour.

## Notes

The study was exploratory and based on Portuguese formal volunteers.

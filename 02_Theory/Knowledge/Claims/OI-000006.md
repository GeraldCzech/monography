## Statement

Higher organizational identification with a nonprofit is positively associated with non-monetary prosocial behaviors, including volunteer retention and positive word-of-mouth.

## Implications for BEBA

This extends the relevance of OI within BEBA to a wider range of valuable stakeholder behaviors beyond just financial giving.

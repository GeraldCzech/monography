## Statement

In the nonprofit sector, stakeholder perceptions of brand differentiation are positively associated with intentions to provide resource support.

## Implications for BEBA

This indicates that perceived distinctiveness may help nonprofit brands become behaviourally effective.

## Notes

Evidence is based on one exploratory empirical study.

## Statement

Brand Attachment predicts a brand’s need share against substitutable alternatives more strongly than Brand Attitude Strength.

## Implications for BEBA

This extends BEBA beyond direct nonprofit competition by linking attachment to behavioural allocation across substitutable ways of satisfying a need.

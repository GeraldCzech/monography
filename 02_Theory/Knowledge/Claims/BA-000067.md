## Statement

The Brand Associations dimension of brand equity can be effectively measured by including items for brand personality and organizational associations.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates prioritizing organizational and personality associations, which are central to nonprofit branding, in the BEBA measurement model.

## Notes

This represents an enrichment of the measurement of associations beyond just product attributes.

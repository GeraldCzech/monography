## Statement

Brand authenticity is conferred by audience perception rather than determined solely by an organisation’s internal values, ethics, or consistency.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s emphasis on stakeholder perception as the mechanism linking brands to behaviour.

## Notes

The claim challenges internally focused authenticity definitions.

## Statement

The relationship between a nonprofit's brand equity and stakeholders' supportive behaviours is negatively moderated by perceptions of brand hypocrisy.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim represents a key testable hypothesis for BEBA to explore how brand effectiveness is maintained under conditions of uncertainty.

## Notes

The source document identifies this as a significant research gap with little direct testing, making it a speculative but important claim.

## Statement

Nonprofit brand equity is conceptualised in the reviewed models as a stakeholder-based mental asset rather than as a financial asset.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should locate nonprofit brand equity in stakeholder cognition, evaluation, and relationships rather than in organisational financial valuation.

## Statement

Brand awareness alone is insufficient for NGO donor-based brand equity when it is not accompanied by positive familiarity with the organization’s cause.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model awareness as behaviourally effective only when awareness carries positive cause-relevant meaning.

## Notes

The literature reports mixed effects of awareness and familiarity.

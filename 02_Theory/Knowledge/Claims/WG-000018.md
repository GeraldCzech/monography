## Statement

Warm glow constitutes a partially independent, non-brand-mediated pathway to charitable giving.

## Evidence

Andreoni (1990); Crumpler & Grossman (2008); List et al. (2021)

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should include or acknowledge a warm-glow bypass route from motivational antecedents to donation behaviour.

## Notes

This is an integration claim derived from the warm-glow evidence base and its implications for BEBA.

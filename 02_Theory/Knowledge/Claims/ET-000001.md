## Statement

The Evidence-Theory-Ontology (ETO) workflow is a transparent, replicable, and logically sound procedure for transforming empirical evidence into a formal, reusable scientific theory.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the methodological justification for the BEBA-ROS knowledge pipeline and its structured artifacts.

## Notes

This claim connects philosophy of science, knowledge engineering, and systematic review methods into a single procedural framework.

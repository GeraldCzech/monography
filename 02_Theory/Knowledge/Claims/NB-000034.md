## Statement

Nonprofit brand commitment represents an enduring desire by stakeholders to maintain a valued relationship with a nonprofit brand.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies commitment as the relational state through which trust can become stabilized before behavioural support occurs.

## Notes

The reviewed material describes commitment as a perceptual relationship dimension but does not empirically test its link to actual donation, volunteering, or advocacy behaviour.

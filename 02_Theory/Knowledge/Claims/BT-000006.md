## Statement

Brand trust reduces perceived donation risk by signalling nonprofit integrity, competence, and ethical conduct under conditions of information asymmetry.

## Implications for BEBA

This directly supports BEBA’s central mechanism of brand-enabled behaviour under uncertainty.

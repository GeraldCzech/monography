## Statement

Customer-based brand equity models differ in their specified dimensions, with Aaker emphasising loyalty, awareness, perceived quality, and associations while Keller emphasises awareness and image.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim cautions BEBA against assuming a fixed universal component structure for brand-enabled behaviour.

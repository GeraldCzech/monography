## Statement

An affective response to a specific, identifiable individual in need is a stronger trigger for giving than a cognitive response to statistical victims.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies a powerful cognitive bias that a brand architecture must either leverage or attempt to overcome.

## Notes

This is a well-established finding in behavioural science.

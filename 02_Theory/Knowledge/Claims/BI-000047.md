## Statement

The conceptual basis for brand image has remained anchored in associative network models from the early 1990s, with limited theoretical evolution to address digitalization.

## Implications for BEBA

This theoretical inertia in the field validates BEBA's focus on behaviour as a necessary and novel evolution of brand theory.

## Notes

The claim is supported by the finding that Aaker (1991) and Keller (1993) are the primary theoretical anchors and 'conceptual rethinking' is a key research gap.

## Statement

Past donation behavior is a direct predictor of future donation intention, with an effect size comparable to or exceeding that of core TPB constructs like attitude.

## Implications for BEBA

The BEBA framework must include components that facilitate and reinforce repeat giving to build behavioral habits.

## Notes

The literature suggests past behavior may operate as a proxy for habit or as an informational cue for forming future intentions.

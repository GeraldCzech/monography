## Statement

Donation intention models that include moral norms explain more variance than models limited to subjective social norms.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that BEBA should distinguish internalised moral obligation from perceived social pressure when modelling brand-enabled giving.

## Notes

The reviewed material indicates that moral norms can be stronger predictors than social norms in charitable giving contexts.

## Statement

The evaluative content of nonprofit brand equity differs from commercial brand equity by emphasizing mission fit, moral legitimacy, and perceived efficacy rather than product quality.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA by specifying that nonprofit brand-enabled behaviour depends on nonprofit-specific evaluative criteria.

## Statement

Donor loyalty in nonprofits is positively influenced by both a direct effect from donor satisfaction and a parallel, indirect effect from donor satisfaction mediated by organizational identification.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides empirical support for modeling both transactional and relational pathways to behaviour within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

The model tested is an initial approach and does not claim to capture all exhaustive causal relationships.

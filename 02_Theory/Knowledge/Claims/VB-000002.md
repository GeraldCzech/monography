## Statement

Corporate employee brand equity measures require linguistic and conceptual adaptation before application to nonprofit volunteer contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim cautions BEBA against transferring corporate brand constructs into nonprofit settings without contextual adaptation.

## Notes

Examples include adapting terms such as job training, goals, and intention-to-stay time horizons.

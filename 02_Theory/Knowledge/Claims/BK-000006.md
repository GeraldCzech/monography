## Statement

Brand knowledge has a stronger influence on purchase propensity in subscription markets than in repertoire markets.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should specify that brand knowledge becomes more behaviourally consequential in high-commitment and higher-uncertainty contexts.

## Notes

The empirical comparison is between banking and soft drinks in the UK.

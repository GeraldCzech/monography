## Statement

Nonprofit practitioners often conflate marketing with fundraising, rather than viewing it as a strategic function for managing all stakeholder relationships.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim validates the need for BEBA to provide a clear ontology that distinguishes fundraising from a broader, multi-stakeholder marketing strategy.

## Notes

Finding based on exploratory interviews with 43 small to medium NPOs in Michigan in 2006-2007.

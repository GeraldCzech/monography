## Statement

Personal norms of moral obligation are a stronger predictor of a blood donor role-identity compared to a volunteer or financial donor role-identity.

## Implications for BEBA

A BEBA for blood donation should lean more heavily into messaging about moral duty than a BEBA for fundraising or volunteering.

## Notes

This finding further highlights the unique psychological profile of blood donation.

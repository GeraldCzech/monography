## Statement

Some donors continue to give even when their donation has no effect on the charity’s total receipts, indicating that giving can be motivated by the act of giving itself.

## Evidence

Crumpler & Grossman (2008)

## Implications for BEBA

A direct motivation-to-behaviour path may be needed alongside brand-mediated pathways.

## Notes

The full crowd-out experimental design provides a clean operationalisation of warm glow.

## Statement

Academic brand image measurement has shown significant latency in adopting new data sources, relying almost exclusively on primary survey data through 2016.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies a strategic research gap concerning User-Generated Content that the BEBA-ROS is designed to help address.

## Notes

The source review found that 215 of 224 articles used primary data, and identified integrating UGC as a major research gap.

## Statement

Donor brand commitment is a positive predictor of a portfolio of supportive intentions, including donation, volunteering, and advocacy.

## Implications for BEBA

This justifies modeling commitment as a state that unlocks a range of value-creating behaviors, validating BEBA's focus on behavioral portfolios.

## Notes

This claim concerns a portfolio of behavioral *intentions*, not necessarily executed behaviors.

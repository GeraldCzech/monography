## Statement

Existing integrated CBBE-IBM studies are predominantly cross-sectional and provide limited longitudinal evidence for causal effects over time.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should avoid strong causal claims unless supported by longitudinal or experimental evidence.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis identifies longitudinal validation as a major research gap.

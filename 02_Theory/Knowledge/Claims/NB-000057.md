## Statement

Nonprofit brand image is empirically represented by six distinct first-order dimensions: Usefulness, Efficiency, Affect, Dynamism, Reliability, and Ethicality.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim specifies the perceptual components through which nonprofit brands can be modelled as behaviour-enabling architectures.

## Notes

The evidence is based on studies conducted in the UK children's charity sector.

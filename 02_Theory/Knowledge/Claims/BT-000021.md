## Statement

Brand trust is a bi-dimensional construct comprising a reliability dimension and an intentions dimension.

## Implications for BEBA

This bi-dimensional view allows BEBA to model supporter trust along both competence (reliability) and integrity (intentions) axes.

## Notes

The paper conceptualizes intentions as including benevolence, which is highly relevant for the nonprofit context.

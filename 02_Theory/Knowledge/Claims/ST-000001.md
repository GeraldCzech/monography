## Statement

Stakeholder evaluation of nonprofit brand equity is a nonlinear function of its constituent signals, with signals exhibiting asymmetric and substitution effects.

## Implications for BEBA

This requires BEBA to model signal integration as a complex, non-additive process rather than using a simple composite index.

## Notes

Recent evidence for this is concentrated in online crowdfunding contexts, and generalizability to traditional nonprofits is not yet established.

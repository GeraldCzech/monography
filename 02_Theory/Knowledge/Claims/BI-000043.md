## Statement

Brand Identity positively predicts Brand Meaning in the context of a nonprofit health-promotion brand.

## Implications for BEBA

This establishes the first link in the causal chain, showing that brand awareness is a necessary precursor to building deeper brand meaning.

## Notes

The study reported a standardized path coefficient of β = 0.387 (p < .001) for this relationship.

## Statement

A research design that integrates Bayesian evidence aggregation, a structured ETO workflow, and a knowledge graph representation enables a more robust, transparent, and cumulative development of theory in complex social domains compared to designs based on disconnected, significance-tested studies.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the primary justification for the entire methodological architecture of the BEBA project.

## Notes

This is a comparative claim about methodological superiority that frames the core contribution of the BEBA research design.

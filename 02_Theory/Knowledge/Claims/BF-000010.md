## Statement

Brand familiarity moderates the relationship between performance-expectation disconfirmation and customer satisfaction.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s view that prior brand-related experience changes how stakeholders translate service encounters into behavioural readiness.

## Notes

Evidence comes from a restaurant-service context using longitudinal survey data and structural equation modeling.

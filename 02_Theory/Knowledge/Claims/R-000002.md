## Statement

Reputational effects on giving are stronger when observers belong to the donor’s social network.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model social context as a boundary condition for brand-enabled reputational benefits.

## Notes

The reviewed material refers to stronger effects in contexts involving strong ties and smaller, less anonymous groups.

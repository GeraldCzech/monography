## Statement

An effective Behaviour Architecture for an NPO must be based on a segmentation model that accounts for the complete portfolio of a supporter's past pro-social behaviours, not just their interaction with one donation type.

## Implications for BEBA

This translates the study's empirical findings into a normative principle for designing and applying the BEBA framework in practice.

## Notes

This is a direct normative implication derived from the study's findings rather than a direct empirical finding itself.

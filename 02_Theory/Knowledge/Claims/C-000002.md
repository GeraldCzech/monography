## Statement

Nonprofit employees are less likely to have confidence in the ability of nonprofit organizations to spend money wisely compared to the general public.

## Implications for BEBA

This indicates that internal brand perceptions can be more critical or skeptical than external ones, affecting internal behaviour architectures.

## Notes

The original study found this relationship to be only marginally significant.

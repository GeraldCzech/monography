## Statement

Brand typicality mediates the relationship between perceptions of a nonprofit's brand image and the intention to donate time.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests brand typicality is a key cognitive mechanism linking brand perception to behavioural intention within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

The study's structural equation model identified brand typicality as a significant mediator between brand image dimensions and donation intentions.

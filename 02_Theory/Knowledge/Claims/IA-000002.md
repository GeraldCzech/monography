## Statement

Charitable giving is better explained by an impure altruism model that combines concern for recipient welfare with warm glow utility than by a pure altruism model.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim positions nonprofit brand effects as operating through both outcome-oriented and donor-experienced utility mechanisms.

## Statement

Brand communications that frame stakeholder action as reparative justice will foster higher long-term commitment than communications that frame action as voluntary charity.

## Implications for BEBA

This proposes a testable hypothesis about the long-term behavioural effects of different brand framings.

## Notes

This is a candidate claim proposed by the BEBA project itself for future testing.

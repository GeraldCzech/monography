## Statement

The 'Efficiency' dimension of nonprofit brand image has a stronger positive effect on the intention to donate money than on the intention to donate time.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides strong evidence for behaviour-specific pathways, where functional brand components channel resources toward financial support.

## Notes

This finding supports the theory that monetary donation decisions are driven more by rational or calculative brand perceptions.

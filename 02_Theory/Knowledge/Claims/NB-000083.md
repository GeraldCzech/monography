## Statement

The perceived typicality of a nonprofit within its cause category is a significant positive predictor of an individual's intention to donate time.

## Implications for BEBA

This reinforces typicality's role as a foundational BEBA heuristic applicable to multiple forms of supportive behaviour.

## Notes

Typicality independently explained 23% of the variance in time giving intentions, separate from brand image.

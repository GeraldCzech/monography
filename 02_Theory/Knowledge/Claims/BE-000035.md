## Statement

Brand image, brand awareness, and brand personality function as antecedents of brand equity in customer-based brand equity models.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides candidate antecedent classes for modelling how nonprofit brands enable stakeholder behaviour.

## Notes

Faircloth tests selected antecedents in a nonprofit context, but the broader antecedent structure is drawn from for-profit brand equity theory.

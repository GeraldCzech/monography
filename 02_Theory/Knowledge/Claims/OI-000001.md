## Statement

Integration quality in an omnichannel environment alters the pathways and strength of brand-equity effects.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should account for channel configuration when modelling how nonprofit brands enable behaviour across digital and offline touchpoints.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis identifies omnichannel integration quality as a contextual boundary condition.

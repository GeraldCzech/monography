## Statement

Brand awareness may be less salient for high-involvement products than for other consumer decision contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that the behavioural role of awareness in nonprofit brand equity should be tested under different involvement conditions.

## Notes

The reviewed material gives electric vehicles as an example of a high-involvement context.

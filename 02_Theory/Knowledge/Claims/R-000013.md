## Statement

Situational reputation cues such as "watching eyes" images do not reliably increase prosocial giving.

## Implications for BEBA

This challenges naive models of brand-as-reputation, suggesting BEBA should focus on durable trust over situational social pressure.

## Notes

This finding is based on replication failures.

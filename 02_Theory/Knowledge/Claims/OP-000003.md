## Statement

An NPO's level of professionalization is negatively associated with its reliance on individual donations.

## Implications for BEBA

This qualifies the role of organizational professionalism, suggesting it may be tailored to specific stakeholder groups and is not universally optimal for all funding types.

## Notes

Based on descriptive data in the primary study. This finding is specific to the Czech context and may reflect national funding structures.

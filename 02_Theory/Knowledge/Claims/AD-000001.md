## Statement

Boenigk and Helmig’s structural model explains only 4–5% of the variance in actual donation behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should treat actual donation behavior as requiring additional explanatory inputs beyond satisfaction, identification, salience, and loyalty.

## Notes

The reviewed material identifies income, fundraising intensity, and situational factors as possible omitted drivers.

## Statement

High brand strength insulates a brand from competitive threats.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim is relevant to BEBA because behavioural effectiveness under uncertainty may depend on resistance to competing appeals.

## Notes

The claim is reported for competitive brand markets and requires translation to nonprofit competition for attention, trust, and support.

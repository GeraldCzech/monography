## Statement

An organisation’s perceived expertise and capability to deliver on its brand promise increases brand credibility.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim connects organisational capability with stakeholder perceptions of credible brand performance.

## Notes

The claim is especially relevant to nonprofit contexts where stakeholders may face uncertainty about organisational competence and impact delivery.

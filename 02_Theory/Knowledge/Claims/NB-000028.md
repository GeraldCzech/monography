## Statement

Stakeholder-based nonprofit brand equity is a multidimensional construct composed of nonprofit brand awareness, nonprofit brand trust, and nonprofit brand commitment.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a candidate dimensional structure for modelling nonprofit brand equity as a behaviour-enabling architecture.

## Notes

The framework is conceptually proposed and empirically operationalised in a German sample of well-known nonprofit brands.

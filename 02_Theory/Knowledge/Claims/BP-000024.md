## Statement

The five-factor brand personality structure identified by Aaker (1997) for commercial brands does not replicate within the nonprofit sector.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies the development of a sector-specific NPO brand personality model, a foundational component of the BEBA framework.

## Notes

Finding is based on an empirical study in the German market and challenges the direct applicability of for-profit models.

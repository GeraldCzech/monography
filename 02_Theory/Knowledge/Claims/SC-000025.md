## Statement

The primary behavioural function of a nonprofit brand is to serve as a store of symbolic capital, which facilitates the conversion of stakeholder resources into symbolic returns.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim reframes nonprofit brand equity as symbolic capital, providing a core sociological mechanism for its value and function within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

This is a theoretical synthesis that equates the BEBA concept of 'brand equity' with the Bourdieusian concept of 'symbolic capital'.

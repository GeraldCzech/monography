## Statement

In the nonprofit sector, stakeholder perceptions of brand scale are positively associated with intentions to provide resource support.

## Implications for BEBA

This implies that perceived organizational size or substance may function as a decision heuristic in nonprofit support behaviour.

## Notes

Brand scale refers to perceptions such as being large, rich, or dominating.

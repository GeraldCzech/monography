## Statement

Retractions of published studies in philanthropy research are rare.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that BEBA cannot rely on the absence of retractions as a signal of a theory's validity and must conduct its own critical evidence review.

## Notes

The authors suggest this may signal a lack of scrutiny rather than an indicator of high research quality.

## Statement

Comprehensive integrated behavior models do not offer substantially greater predictive validity for behavior than more parsimonious reasoned-action models.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA's principle of parsimony, cautioning against adding complexity without clear explanatory gains for behavior.

## Notes

More et al. (2022) found an integrated model explained only 0.3% more variance in exercise behavior than the Theory of Planned Behavior (TPB).

## Statement

Trust in a nonprofit, perceived organizational efficacy, and donor-brand value alignment are stronger direct predictors of donation behavior than perceived brand differentiation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim grounds BEBA by asserting that brand effects must be understood in the context of more powerful, fundamental drivers of giving.

## Notes

This claim is comparative, establishing the relative predictive power of brand-related versus core philanthropic constructs.

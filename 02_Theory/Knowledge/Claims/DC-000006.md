## Statement

Donor satisfaction has a stronger effect on donor loyalty than identification-based constructs in the blood donation context studied by Boenigk and Helmig.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model service-heavy and high-involvement donation contexts as potentially more satisfaction-driven than identity-driven.

## Notes

The claim applies to the blood-collecting service context in the reviewed study.

## Statement

The primary branding challenge reported by senior marketing personnel in UK nonprofit organizations is the implementation of branding rather than the identification of branding success factors.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s focus on the translation of brand meaning into organizational routines and stakeholder-facing behaviour.

## Notes

The claim reflects the distinction in the reviewed material between knowing relevant factors and embedding them in practice.

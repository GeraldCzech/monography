## Statement

The relationship between stakeholder trust and donation behaviour is not consistently positive and significant across all contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This challenges any simplistic BEBA model, highlighting the need to theorize moderators or mediators in the trust-behaviour relationship.

## Notes

The review explicitly identifies this as an area of disagreement, with some studies finding a strong link and others finding no significant predictive relationship.

## Statement

Brand Attachment predicts intentions to perform difficult brand-related behaviours more strongly than Brand Attitude Strength.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the BEBA proposition that high-commitment nonprofit behaviours require attachment-based mechanisms beyond positive evaluation.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis defines difficult behaviours as behaviours requiring significant consumer resources.

## Statement

Brand Prominence increases the accessibility of relationship-sustaining brand behaviours by making brand-related thoughts and feelings easier to retrieve.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that salient nonprofit brand bonds may increase the likelihood that supportive actions are considered and enacted.

## Notes

This is presented as a proposed mechanism rather than a separately evidenced empirical finding in the reviewed synthesis.

## Statement

Aversion to personal donation appeals is a contributing reason for non-donation.

## Implications for BEBA

The action-enabling pathways (affordances) within a BEBA must be designed to avoid triggering reactance.

## Notes

The authors note that the underlying "Solicitation" factor in their analysis was statistically weak, suggesting a need for more robust measurement.

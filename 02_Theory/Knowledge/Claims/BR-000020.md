## Statement

The BEBA research design provides a practical application of the semantic view of scientific theories, where the ETO workflow serves as the process for specifying and refining a set of interconnected models that constitute the theory.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim grounds the BEBA methodology in a mainstream, defensible philosophy of science paradigm.

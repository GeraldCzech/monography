## Statement

Perceptions of a nonprofit brand as a "Marketer" are more strongly associated with attributions of self-serving motives than with attributions of operational incompetence.

## Implications for BEBA

It specifies that the trust mechanism in BEBA is highly sensitive to perceived motives, not just perceived effectiveness.

## Notes

This highlights that distrust is not a simple issue of poor accountability but a fundamental questioning of the organisation's core ethics.

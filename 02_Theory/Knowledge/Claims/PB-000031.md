## Statement

The statistical association between past donation frequency and future giving represents a compound effect of stable preferences, donor identity, and solicitation exposure, and cannot be interpreted solely as evidence of psychological habit.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must treat 'past behaviour' as a control variable and a composite outcome, not as a clean proxy for the psychological construct of 'habit'.

## Notes

This is an interpretive claim about the nature of a commonly used variable, warning against a specific causal misattribution.

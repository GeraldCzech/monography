## Statement

None of the reviewed nonprofit brand equity models provides a complete empirically tested path from brand perceptions to actual observed donation behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should address the intention-behaviour gap by connecting brand architecture to observed behavioural outcomes.

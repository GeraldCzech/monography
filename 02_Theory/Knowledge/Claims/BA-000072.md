## Statement

Brand authenticity is a measurable, multi-dimensional construct distinct from traditional brand evaluation metrics like perceived quality or brand image.

## Implications for BEBA

Brand authenticity is a likely key mediator for building trust and encouraging pro-social behaviour in the nonprofit sector.

## Notes

The emergence of this construct as a focus of measurement reflects a significant shift in consumer values relevant to NPOs.

## Statement

The influence of subjective norm on donation intention is inconsistent across different cultural and social contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA can propose that a strong brand community is a mechanism that makes subjective norms salient and effective.

## Notes

Its effect is often non-significant in individualistic contexts for monetary giving but stronger in collectivist or community-oriented settings.

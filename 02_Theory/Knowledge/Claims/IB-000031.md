## Statement

A significant and persistent gap exists between behavioral intentions and subsequent actions.

## Implications for BEBA

The existence of this gap is the primary justification for a framework like BEBA that focuses on behavior enactment, not just intention formation.

## Notes

This is a well-established finding across numerous behavioral domains and serves as the starting point for this line of inquiry.

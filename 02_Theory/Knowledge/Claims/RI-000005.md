## Statement

The strength of a pro-social role-identity is positively predicted by personal norms of moral obligation.

## Implications for BEBA

A BEBA can strengthen identity by aligning brand messaging with the target audience's internal moral values.

## Notes

This draws on the work of Schwartz and Howard (1981) on altruism and moral norms.

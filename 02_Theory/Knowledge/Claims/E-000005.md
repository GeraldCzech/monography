## Statement

Post-consumption expectations have a stronger influence on behavioural intentions among low-familiarity consumers than among high-familiarity consumers.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports a BEBA learning pathway in which unfamiliar stakeholders update future behavioural intentions through revised expectations.

## Notes

Post-consumption expectations are treated as a mediator between satisfaction and behavioural intentions.

## Statement

A three-factor model of brand associations comprising perceived value, brand personality, and organisational associations provides better psychometric fit than a unidimensional model in UK and Spanish consumer samples.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports modelling nonprofit brand associations as a differentiated construct rather than as a single undifferentiated perception.

## Notes

The specific association dimensions may require adaptation for nonprofit organisations.

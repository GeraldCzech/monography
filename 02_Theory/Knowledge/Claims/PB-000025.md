## Statement

The relationship between a member's perception of a nonprofit's brand orientation and their perceived benefits is fully mediated by their perception of the organization's market orientation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides empirical validation for a core BEBA causal pathway where a brand's influence is channelled through perceived behaviour.

## Notes

Finding is based on an alternative model tested in a single study in the context of Australian church organizations.

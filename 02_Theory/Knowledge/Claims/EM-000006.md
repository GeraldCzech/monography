## Statement

Formalising a social science theory as a computational ontology transforms it from a static narrative into a dynamic, verifiable, and extensible research asset.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim posits that the final BEBA ontology will be a computationally valuable knowledge asset, not just a static dissertation chapter.

## Notes

This transformation enables capabilities like automated reasoning and the systematic integration of future research findings.

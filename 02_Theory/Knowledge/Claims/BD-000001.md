## Statement

The perceived differentiation of a nonprofit brand from competing organisations is positively associated with resource provider support intentions.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that behavioural effectiveness may depend on a nonprofit brand being distinguishable from alternatives.

## Notes

Based on one exploratory empirical study of a single nonprofit organisation.

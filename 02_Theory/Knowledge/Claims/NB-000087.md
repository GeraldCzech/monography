## Statement

The implementation of a nonprofit brand orientation is inhibited by organizational cultures characterized by high employee autonomy and decentralized management.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies a critical cultural barrier that any BEBA implementation framework must address.

## Notes

The study discusses this as a cultural tension and corroborates prior literature but does not empirically test the constraining effect itself.

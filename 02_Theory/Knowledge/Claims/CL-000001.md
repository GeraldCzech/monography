## Statement

Charismatic leadership strengthens the positive relationship between an NPO's brand orientation behaviour and staff's emotional brand attachment.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies a specific leadership behaviour as a key leverage point for accelerating the initial stage of brand value internalization.

## Notes

This represents the first of two significant moderation effects found in the study's model.

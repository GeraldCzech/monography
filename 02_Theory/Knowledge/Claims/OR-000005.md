## Statement

A nonprofit organization's perceived reputation is a positive and significant predictor of an individual's willingness to volunteer for that organization.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the role of brand-related constructs like reputation as a key input in the 'attraction' phase of the volunteer journey.

## Notes

The source text notes this effect can be moderated by socio-demographic characteristics.

## Statement

The translation of brand experience into actionable organizational programmes is a major implementation challenge for nonprofit branding.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim highlights the need for BEBA to specify how abstract brand experience becomes operational behaviour.

## Notes

The claim is based on practitioner-reported implementation difficulties in an exploratory qualitative study.

## Statement

Strong nonprofit brands improve an international nonprofit organization’s ability to mobilize financial, human, and relational resources.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the idea that nonprofit brand equity should be evaluated by its capacity to enable multiple stakeholder behaviours.

## Notes

The qualitative evidence includes fundraising, volunteer attraction, corporate partnerships, and policymaker leverage.

## Statement

The construct of loyalty is less developed in nonprofit brand equity research than the constructs of awareness and associations.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should treat nonprofit loyalty constructs as theoretically unsettled and avoid importing for-profit loyalty assumptions uncritically.

## Statement

The measurement scale for non-profit brand image does not exhibit scalar invariance across UK, Indian, and Bosnian-Herzegovinian national cultures.

## Implications for BEBA

This mandates that BEBA must not permit direct comparison of mean brand image scores across cultures without first establishing scalar invariance.

## Notes

The lack of scalar invariance indicates a systematic response bias, making direct comparison of brand image levels between these countries misleading.

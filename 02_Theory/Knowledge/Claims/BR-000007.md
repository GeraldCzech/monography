## Statement

Brand research has generated many domain-specific constructs without adequately specifying how those constructs relate within an integrative psychological framework.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies BEBA’s knowledge-architecture approach, which requires explicit relationships among constructs rather than isolated brand-equity variables.

## Notes

This is Schmitt’s field-level diagnosis of fragmentation in consumer brand psychology.

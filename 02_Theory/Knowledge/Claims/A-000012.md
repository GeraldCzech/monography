## Statement

Low public awareness of nonprofit organizations can act as a barrier to service utilization by potential beneficiaries.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim connects the cognitive foundation of brand equity (awareness) directly to the NPO's operational mission (service delivery).

## Notes

This is a proposition from the discussion section of McDougle (2014), citing Kissane (2003), not a direct empirical finding of the study's primary model.

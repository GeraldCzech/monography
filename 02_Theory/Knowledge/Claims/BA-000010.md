## Statement

Nonprofit brand awareness has heterogeneous effects on resource provider support intentions across awareness dimensions.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim implies that BEBA should distinguish between awareness as salience and awareness as familiarity with evaluative content.

## Notes

Derived from the differing findings for top-of-mind recall and familiarity.

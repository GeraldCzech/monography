## Statement

Perceived demonstrable and emotional benefits do not have a significant effect on giving intention or giving behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This challenges the assumption that all forms of perceived personal benefit are key behavioral drivers, especially in certain cultural contexts.

## Notes

The study was conducted in China, which may be a significant boundary condition for this finding.

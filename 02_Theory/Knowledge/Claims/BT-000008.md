## Statement

High consumer-brand trust is contingent upon a configuration of pre-existing brand reputation and strong brand associations.

## Implications for BEBA

This extends BEBA by specifying a configurational antecedent structure for trust formation within the brand-enabled behaviour architecture.

## Notes

The claim reflects configurational causality and should not be interpreted as a simple additive linear effect.

## Statement

The Integrated Behavioral Model (IBM), particularly its concepts of environmental constraints and skills, is empirically underutilized in charitable donation research.

## Implications for BEBA

This justifies BEBA's positioning as an applied and extended IBM for the nonprofit context.

## Notes

The summary consistently highlights this as a major research gap despite the IBM's theoretical potential to explain the intention-behavior gap.

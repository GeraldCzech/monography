## Statement

Past donation behaviour is a strong and reliable predictor of future donation behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

The BEBA model must include a pathway representing behavioural persistence or the influence of past behaviour.

## Notes

This is a robust finding in longitudinal and transactional datasets and represents a core empirical regularity to be explained.

## Statement

High nonprofit brand equity can function as a trust and quality seal for stakeholders evaluating nonprofit organizations.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim frames nonprofit brand equity as a cognitive shortcut that simplifies stakeholder decision-making under uncertainty.

## Notes

The claim is linked to the authors’ argument that brands can reinforce the weakened signalling function of the nondistribution constraint.

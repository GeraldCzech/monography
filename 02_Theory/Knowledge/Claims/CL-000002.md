## Statement

Charismatic leadership strengthens the positive relationship between staff's emotional brand attachment and their service involvement.

## Implications for BEBA

It identifies a second leverage point where leadership can deepen the internalization process, converting emotional connection into behavioural engagement.

## Notes

This represents the second significant moderation effect found in the study's model.

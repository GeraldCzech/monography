## Statement

Nonprofits primarily perceive competition in the fundraising market rather than in the client service delivery market.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must account for the distinct competitive dynamics in the donor, volunteer, and client markets, broadening the typical NPO's strategic view.

## Notes

This perception may be specific to the types of service-oriented NPOs in the study who reported having no trouble finding clients.

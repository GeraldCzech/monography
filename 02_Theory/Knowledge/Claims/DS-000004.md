## Statement

The population of nonprofit supporters is not a single group but is composed of distinct behavioural segments, including those who donate a single resource (money, time, or blood) and those who donate multiple resources.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the core BEBA premise that pro-social behaviour is a structured architecture, not a monolithic activity.

## Notes

This finding is based on a representative sample of the German population and may not generalize to other cultural contexts.

## Statement

The effect of perceived brand differentiation on the probability of donation is positively moderated by situational salience.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim is core to the "architecture" aspect of BEBA, highlighting the importance of contextual cues in activating brand effects.

## Notes

This is a moderation hypothesis, suggesting the effect is stronger when the brand is cued in the choice environment.

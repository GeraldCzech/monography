## Statement

Donor-based brand equity captures a different stakeholder perspective than volunteer-based, beneficiary-based, or staff-based brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should make stakeholder role explicit when defining brand equity mechanisms.

## Notes

The reviewed material focuses explicitly on donor-based brand equity and indicates that other stakeholder perspectives may differ.

## Statement

The conceptual structure of non-profit brand image comprises six distinct dimensions: usefulness, efficiency, affect, dynamism, reliability, and ethicality.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a robust, multi-dimensional definition of Non-profit Brand Image for inclusion as a core concept in the BEBA information model.

## Notes

The stability of this six-factor structure was demonstrated across three diverse national cultures (UK, India, Bosnia and Herzegovina).

## Statement

The likelihood of a potential donor avoiding a solicitor is moderated by the physical cost of avoidance.

## Implications for BEBA

The effectiveness of a BEBA is contingent on the choice architecture of the environment in which it is deployed.

## Notes

For example, avoidance is less likely in extreme cold weather.

## Statement

The stability of the donation context positively moderates the strength of the relationship between past behavior and cognitive automaticity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the theoretical rationale for why a brand, as a stable cue, is crucial for fostering the automatic pathway of giving.

## Notes

The review suggests this has not been sufficiently tested by comparing different donation modalities (e.g., recurring vs. campaign-based).

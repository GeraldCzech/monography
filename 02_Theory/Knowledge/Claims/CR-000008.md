## Statement

The corporatist state-nonprofit partnership acts as a stabilizing financial and institutional force for affiliated nonprofits during systemic crises.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies the state partnership as a key variable moderating the effect of environmental uncertainty on nonprofit viability.

## Notes

The claim is derived from the description of the partnership as "resilient" during the 2008 and COVID-19 crises.

## Statement

The effectiveness of digital communication in building nonprofit brand equity is contingent on its use for fostering engagement, not merely for one-way information dissemination.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim specifies the type of communication behaviour (engagement) required for a BEBA to be effective in digital environments.

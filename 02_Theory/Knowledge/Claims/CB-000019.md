## Statement

The universal applicability of a single, invariant structure for consumer-based brand equity is not supported across different national, cultural, and product-sector contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a strong rationale for developing a context-sensitive framework like BEBA, which is tailored to the specific nonprofit sector.

## Notes

The study speculates that macro-economic uncertainty may also act as a contextual moderator, which is highly relevant to BEBA's core research question.

## Statement

A higher degree of nonprofit brand orientation is positively associated with fundraising success.

## Implications for BEBA

This directly links brand strategy to a primary desired behaviour (donation), a core focus for BEBA.

## Notes

The effect is specified as being particularly strong for voluntary income, but not statutory income (Hankinson, 2002).

## Statement

The positive effect of brand heritage on donation intention is mediated by brand trust.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides evidence for modeling trust as a mediator variable that translates specific brand signals into behavioral intentions.

## Notes

This highlights a specific pathway, suggesting trust's mediating role is not universal across all brand attributes but is context-dependent.

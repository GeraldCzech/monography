## Statement

A six-factor nonprofit brand image scale including Usefulness, Efficiency, Affect, Dynamism, Reliability, and Ethicality has stronger psychometric properties than the earlier four-factor model.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports using a multidimensional image construct rather than a narrower brand-image specification in BEBA measurement architecture.

## Notes

The evidence is limited to the empirical settings reported in the focal paper.

## Statement

The effectiveness of direct solicitation on giving is moderated by the stakeholder's perceived cost of avoiding the request.

## Implications for BEBA

This implies BEBA models must account for the context of the behavioural ask, not just the brand signal.

## Notes

This is a boundary condition identified by specific cited studies.

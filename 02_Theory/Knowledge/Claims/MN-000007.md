## Statement

Brand attitudes toward a nonprofit organisation can become non-significant predictors of donation outcomes when moral norms and identity-based constructs are controlled.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim challenges brand-attitude-centric explanations and supports including moral and identity pathways in BEBA.

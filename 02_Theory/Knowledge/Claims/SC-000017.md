## Statement

Situational cues indicating social observation, such as eye images, increase the amount of money donated in public settings, but this effect is conditional on ambient human traffic.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that situational activators are not universal but are context-dependent moderators in the BEBA model.

## Notes

The universality of this effect is debated, with some studies finding null average effects or effects that are conditional on environmental factors like low vs. high store traffic.

## Statement

The relative influence of satisfaction versus identity-based constructs on donor loyalty is contingent on the donation context.

## Implications for BEBA

This demonstrates that the BEBA model cannot be monolithic and must adapt its pathways based on the nature of the helping behaviour.

## Notes

Identity constructs were stronger drivers for money donations, while satisfaction was a stronger driver for blood donations.

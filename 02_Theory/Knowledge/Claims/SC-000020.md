## Statement

Corporate philanthropy is often a strategic mechanism for converting economic capital into symbolic capital (e.g., legitimacy, reputation, stakeholder trust).

## Implications for BEBA

This provides strong evidence for conceptualizing nonprofit brand equity as a form of symbolic capital.

## Notes

This claim is specific to corporate philanthropy and may not fully generalize to individual donors without further evidence.

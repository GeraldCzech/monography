## Statement

A significant portion of non-donation is characterized by a lack of conscious thought or activation rather than by active opposition.

## Implications for BEBA

This highlights the need for a BEBA to maintain cognitive availability and provide timely action cues to overcome passivity.

## Notes

Relates to the "Indifferent" and "Unconscious" factors in the study.

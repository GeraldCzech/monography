## Statement

Past donation behaviour improves the explanation of future donation intention and future donation behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim implies that BEBA should account for behavioural history and habit-like continuity in donor action.

## Notes

The reviewed material treats past donation behaviour as an extension to the core intention model.

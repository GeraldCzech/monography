## Statement

Causal claims about the effect of nonprofit brand equity on stakeholder support that are based solely on cross-sectional survey data are likely inflated by common method bias.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim validates BEBA's methodological focus on actual behaviour and causal inference over correlational, intention-based studies.

## Notes

The claim is about the high likelihood of bias, which underpins the need for alternative research designs.

## Statement

In the nonprofit sector, top-of-mind brand recall is not significantly associated with intentions to provide resource support.

## Implications for BEBA

This cautions BEBA against treating salience alone as sufficient for behavioural effectiveness.

## Notes

The study operationalized this as first recall within the nonprofit service category.

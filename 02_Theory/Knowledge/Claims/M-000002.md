## Statement

Nonprofit brand equity research lacks validated generalizable scales for brand image and brand personality across nonprofit types, causes, and countries.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA measurement work should avoid assuming that commercial brand image or personality scales transfer unchanged to nonprofit contexts.

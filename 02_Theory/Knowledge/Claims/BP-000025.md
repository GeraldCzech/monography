## Statement

The brand personality of nonprofit organisations, in the German context, is best represented by a three-dimensional structure comprising 'Social Competence and Trust', 'Emotion and Assertiveness', and 'Sophistication and Exclusivity'.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim offers a specific, three-factor structure that can be used to operationalize the 'Brand' component within the BEBA information model.

## Notes

The three-factor structure was identified in the German context and requires cross-cultural validation.

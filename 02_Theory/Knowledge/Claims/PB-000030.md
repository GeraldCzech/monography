## Statement

The predictive power of Perceived Behavioral Control (PBC) on donation intention is heightened for behaviors that involve significant physical or psychological barriers.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that a key function of a BEBA is to increase PBC and self-efficacy for specific desired behaviors.

## Notes

The example given is blood donation, which has higher perceived barriers than monetary giving.

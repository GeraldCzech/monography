## Statement

A potential volunteer's level of Brand Engagement with a nonprofit determines their subsequent brand choice process (Brand Discovery).

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes the critical link between a volunteer's cognitive/affective state (engagement) and their behavioural path (discovery), central to the BEBA model.

## Notes

The study conceptualises Brand Engagement as Brand Wise, Brand Aware, or Brand Ignorant.

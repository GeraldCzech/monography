## Statement

The exemplar-based definition of brand authenticity is applicable to nonprofit organisations as brand objects.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the use of brand authenticity in nonprofit brand-equity theory without requiring nonprofit-specific purity assumptions.

## Notes

The paper argues that the definition applies across goods, services, people, ideas, places, and organisations.

## Statement

Volunteer Role Clarity and Brand Commitment mediate the relationship between internal brand management practices and brand-aligned behavioural outcomes.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides specific, empirically supported mediating constructs (Role Clarity, Brand Commitment) for the BEBA model.

## Notes

The study used Exploratory Factor Analysis and correlation; a full structural equation model is needed to formally test the mediation.

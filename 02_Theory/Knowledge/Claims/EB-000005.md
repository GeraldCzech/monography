## Statement

The university branding literature reviewed by Yaping et al. (2023) is geographically biased toward the UK, the US, and multi-country studies.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim limits the generalisability of BEBA-relevant brand mechanisms across geographic and institutional contexts.

## Notes

The review identifies a lack of research focused on developing countries, particularly in Asia.

## Statement

Brand Attachment predicts a brand’s purchase share against direct competitors more strongly than Brand Attitude Strength.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that attachment may help explain why supporters allocate behaviour toward one nonprofit rather than comparable alternatives.

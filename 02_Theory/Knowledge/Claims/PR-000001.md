## Statement

Reductions in perceived risk function as antecedents of consumer-based brand equity rather than as consequences of it.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim positions risk reduction as a causal mechanism through which nonprofit brand equity is built.

## Notes

The reviewed material notes that the direct path from perceived risk to expected utility was not significant, although an indirect effect was suggested.

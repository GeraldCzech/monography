## Statement

For stakeholders with financial capacity but low intrinsic motivation, a brand must frame support as a compelling value proposition to enable behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests a BEBA must not only reduce friction but also actively create and communicate meaning and value for certain segments.

## Notes

This is a strategic recommendation from the paper, not a direct empirical finding.

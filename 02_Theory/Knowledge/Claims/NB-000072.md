## Statement

Nonprofit Brand Orientation is an empirically distinguishable construct from Market Orientation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the theoretical distinctiveness of BEBA from the more general concept of market orientation.

## Notes

The study established discriminant validity between the Nonprofit Brand Orientation Scale (NBOS) and a measure of market orientation.

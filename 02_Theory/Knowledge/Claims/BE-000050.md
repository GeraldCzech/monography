## Statement

The constructs of Brand Awareness and Brand Associations, while conceptually distinct, lack empirical discriminant validity in consumer surveys and are better modelled as a single latent factor.

## Implications for BEBA

This cautions against assuming a priori that all conceptually distinct constructs in BEBA will be empirically separable at the measurement level.

## Notes

The study found very high correlations (0.80-0.90) between the two factors and failed discriminant validity tests across three independent samples.

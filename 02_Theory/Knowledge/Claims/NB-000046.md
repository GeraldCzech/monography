## Statement

The effect of nonprofit brand awareness on giving intention is partially mediated by the donor's self-concept.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim extends the role of awareness in BEBA from cognitive availability to partial identity-based behavioural enablement.

## Notes

Evidence concerns giving intention rather than observed donation behaviour.

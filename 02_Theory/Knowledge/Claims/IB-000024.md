## Statement

The implementation of internal branding strategies is positively associated with employee brand performance and intent to stay.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the internal alignment mechanism, a core causal pathway within the BEBA model.

## Notes

The reviewed literature notes that empirical evidence for this effect on volunteers is less established than for paid staff.

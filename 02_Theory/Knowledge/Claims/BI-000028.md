## Statement

In the nonprofit sector, stakeholder perceptions of brand character are not significantly associated with intentions to provide resource support.

## Implications for BEBA

This qualifies BEBA by suggesting that not all positive brand image dimensions necessarily translate into behavioural support.

## Notes

Brand character refers to perceptions such as honesty, value, and kindness.

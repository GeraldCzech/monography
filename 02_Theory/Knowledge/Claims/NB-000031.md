## Statement

Nonprofit brand trust reduces stakeholder-perceived risk by signalling that a nonprofit will act ethically, use funds appropriately, and remain aligned with its mission.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim directly supports the BEBA proposition that brands enable behaviour under uncertainty by functioning as risk-reducing heuristics.

## Notes

The reviewed material presents this as a central mechanism rather than as a separately tested behavioural outcome.

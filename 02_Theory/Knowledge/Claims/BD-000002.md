## Statement

Declines in perceived brand differentiation can precede declines in other brand equity measures and market performance.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that perceived nonprofit distinctiveness may function as an early warning indicator of weakening behavioural effectiveness.

## Notes

The reviewed material describes this dynamic as compelling but preliminary and requiring further longitudinal research.

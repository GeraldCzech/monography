## Statement

Brand awareness through recall and recognition increases the likelihood that a brand enters a consumer’s consideration set.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a salience mechanism for how nonprofit brands may become behaviourally available to stakeholders.

## Notes

Faircloth’s empirical results qualify this mechanism because top-of-mind recall was not significantly associated with support intentions in the nonprofit study.

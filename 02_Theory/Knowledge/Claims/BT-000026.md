## Statement

The primary function of Brand Trust within the nonprofit brand equity framework is to reduce stakeholder perceived risk concerning an organization's mission fidelity and use of resources.

## Implications for BEBA

Provides a direct link between the Brand Trust construct and BEBA's core research question about brand effectiveness under uncertainty.

## Notes

The paper describes a strong brand as functioning like a "trust and quality seal" for stakeholders.

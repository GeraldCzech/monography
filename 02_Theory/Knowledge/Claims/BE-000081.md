## Statement

A significant intention-behaviour gap exists in nonprofit brand research, with most studies measuring attitudinal or intentional outcomes rather than actual behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

Justifies BEBA's research principle of "Behaviour before brands" and validates its focus on actual behaviour as a necessary and novel approach.

## Notes

This claim concerns the state of the research field itself.

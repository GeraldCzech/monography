## Statement

A measurable discrepancy exists between individuals' stated intentions to donate to charity and their subsequent donation behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s premise that nonprofit brand effectiveness must be evaluated at the level of actual behaviour rather than intention alone.

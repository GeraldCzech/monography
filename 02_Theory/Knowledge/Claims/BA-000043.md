## Statement

Brand authenticity is a unidimensional construct representing the perceived degree to which a brand object is the quintessential exemplar of its type.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides BEBA with a parsimonious perceptual mechanism through which brands can reduce uncertainty by functioning as category exemplars.

## Notes

The claim is conceptual and requires empirical scale validation.

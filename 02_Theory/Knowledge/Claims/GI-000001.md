## Statement

Giving intention has a significant positive effect on subsequent giving behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This confirms the role of intention as a direct antecedent and mediator of behavior, a core structural element of the BEBA model.

## Notes

While confirmed in this study, this finding aligns with and is foundational to broader behavioral theories like the Theory of Planned Behavior.

## Statement

Consumer-Based Brand Equity is a higher-order construct composed of four distinct, correlated first-order dimensions: Brand Awareness, Brand Associations, Perceived Quality, and Brand Loyalty.

## Implications for BEBA

This four-dimensional structure provides a robust, theoretically-grounded starting point for modeling nonprofit brand equity.

## Notes

The finding was validated using CFA on a non-student consumer sample for high-involvement goods (cars, televisions) in an Australian context.

## Statement

Religious giving contexts can involve moral motivations such as duty or salvation that are not fully captured by the warm glow model.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies a boundary condition for BEBA by showing that brand-enabled emotional utility may not fully explain giving in normatively structured contexts.

## Notes

The reviewed material frames this as an area where the sufficiency of the standard impure altruism model is debated.

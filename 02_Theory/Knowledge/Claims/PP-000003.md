## Statement

The price premium a customer is willing to pay for a brand over a competitor reflects the combined influence of multiple brand equity dimensions.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports translating price premium into nonprofit support premium, such as willingness to donate more, volunteer more, or advocate more strongly.

## Notes

Applicability is limited in markets where pricing is regulated or where price differentiation is not meaningful.

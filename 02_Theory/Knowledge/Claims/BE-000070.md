## Statement

The power of a brand resides in the knowledge structures held by customers in their minds.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates BEBA's core premise that a brand is a knowledge-based architecture enabling behaviour.

## Notes

This is the core premise of the Customer-Based Brand Equity (CBBE) model.

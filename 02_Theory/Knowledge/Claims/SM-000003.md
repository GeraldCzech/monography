## Statement

Small nonprofits often fail to develop and integrate distinct marketing strategies for their volunteer constituency.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA's multi-constituency structure by showing a practical failure to strategically address the volunteer stakeholder group.

## Notes

The study found NPOs struggle with targeted outreach to volunteers and donors, in contrast to their relative ease of finding clients.

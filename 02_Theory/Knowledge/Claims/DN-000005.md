## Statement

Descriptive norm appeals that reference a local or psychologically proximate group are more effective at increasing actual donations than appeals that reference a global or distal group.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a specific, testable moderator for the norm-behaviour link within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

This highlights the importance of the referent group's characteristics in the design of norm-based interventions.

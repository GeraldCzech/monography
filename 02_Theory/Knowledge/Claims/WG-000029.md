## Statement

Fundraising appeals that emphasize the donor’s emotional self-benefit increase donation outcomes more effectively than appeals focused solely on recipient benefits.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that nonprofit brand communications can deliberately increase behavioural effectiveness by making warm glow salient.

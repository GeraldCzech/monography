## Statement

In digital donation contexts, trust in the technological platform positively predicts donation intention in addition to trust in the beneficiary organization.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim extends BEBA by requiring digital trust infrastructure to be modelled separately from organizational brand trust.

## Notes

The claim applies specifically to online giving and crowdfunding contexts.

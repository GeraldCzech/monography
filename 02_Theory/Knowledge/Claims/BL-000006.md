## Statement

The strength of the relationship between brand knowledge and purchase propensity increases as consumers move from non-user to light-user to heavy-user segments.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should model brand-enabled behaviour as segment-dependent rather than assuming a uniform brand architecture across all actors.

## Notes

Based on consumer brand contexts; nonprofit supporter segments require empirical validation.

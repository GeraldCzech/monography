## Statement

In co-created nonprofit brand equity models, brand image has a greater relative influence on brand equity for external stakeholders than for internal stakeholders.

## Implications for BEBA

This qualifies how the BEBA operates, suggesting external actors' brand perceptions rely more heavily on brand image as a heuristic for quality.

## Notes

The "external stakeholders" in this context were logistics service providers who are potential employers of the internal group.

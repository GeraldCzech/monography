## Statement

Tracking the most stable awareness measure can provide a more reliable indicator of meaningful brand change than tracking the most volatile awareness measure.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s emphasis on reliable behavioural architecture signals rather than merely sensitive but noisy indicators.

## Notes

The reviewed material presents this as a managerial recommendation conditioned by the trade-off between stability and sensitivity.

## Statement

Perceived behavioral control is frequently the strongest predictor of charitable donation intention among the core Theory of Planned Behavior constructs.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that nonprofit brands may become behaviourally effective by increasing donors’ perceived ease, efficacy, and control over giving.

## Notes

The reviewed material states that PBC often or frequently emerges as the strongest predictor, not that it is always the strongest predictor.

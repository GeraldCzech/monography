## Statement

Externally priming a relevant donor identity at the point of decision increases giving behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a mechanism for how a brand can 'enable' behavior by activating a relevant identity in a specific choice context.

## Notes

This highlights the importance of situational activation in bridging the intention-behavior gap.

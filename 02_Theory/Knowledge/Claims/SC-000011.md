## Statement

A classification system's utility for comparative nonprofit research is a function of its optimised balance between economy, significance, rigour, combinatorial richness, and organising power.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the meta-criteria for justifying BEBA's adoption of a specific classification system like ICNPO.

## Notes

These five criteria form the analytical framework used by the authors to evaluate existing systems and design the ICNPO.

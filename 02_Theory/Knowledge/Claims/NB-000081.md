## Statement

The 'Affect' dimension of nonprofit brand image has a stronger positive effect on the intention to donate time than on the intention to donate money.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the existence of behaviour-specific pathways, linking affective brand components to emotionally-involved behaviours.

## Notes

This finding supports the theory that time donation decisions are driven more by emotional or symbolic brand perceptions.

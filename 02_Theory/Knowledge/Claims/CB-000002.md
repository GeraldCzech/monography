## Statement

Integrating Customer-Based Brand Equity into the Theory of Planned Behavior explains more variance in behavioral intentions than using Customer-Based Brand Equity or Theory of Planned Behavior models in isolation.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s premise that brand equity functions as part of a behavioural explanation rather than as a purely marketing outcome.

## Notes

Evidence is drawn from cross-sectoral for-profit contexts rather than nonprofit settings.

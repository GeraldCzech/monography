## Statement

A brand’s market share affects the relationship among aided, unaided, and top-of-mind awareness measures.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA measurement by showing that awareness indicators must be interpreted relative to market position.

## Notes

The evidence is based on whisky brands across three national markets.

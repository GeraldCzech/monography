## Statement

A knowledge graph, defined via a formal ontology, is an effective and powerful medium for operationalizing and representing complex, multi-level theoretical structures.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies the use of a knowledge graph as the target artifact for representing the BEBA theory.

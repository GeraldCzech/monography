## Statement

Acts of giving create social bonds and implicit obligations of reciprocity between the giver and the recipient or community.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that brand-enabled behaviour is not merely transactional but also builds relational and societal fabric.

## Notes

Rooted in anthropological theories of the gift and reciprocity.

## Statement

Higher brand usage is associated with stronger brand perceptions and beliefs.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should account for feedback effects in which prior behaviour strengthens brand knowledge and future behavioural propensity.

## Notes

The reviewed summary describes this as consistent with a long-standing principle in consumer behaviour research.

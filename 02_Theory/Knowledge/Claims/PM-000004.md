## Statement

A member's perception of a nonprofit organization's market orientation is a direct and significant positive predictor of the personal benefits they perceive from participation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides direct empirical support for the BEBA principle that perceived organizational behaviour creates perceived stakeholder value.

## Notes

Finding is based on a single study in the context of Australian church organizations; "perceived benefits" were operationalized as spiritual and social benefits.

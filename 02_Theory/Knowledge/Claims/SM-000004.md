## Statement

Nonprofits frequently use social media for one-way information dissemination rather than for two-way, dialogic communication.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that the mere presence of a channel (architecture) is insufficient; its mode of use determines its behavioural effectiveness.

## Notes

This claim represents a "practice gap" between the potential of social media for engagement and its typical application.

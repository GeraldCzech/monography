## Statement

The conceptual separation of evidence strength (Likelihood), degree of belief (Posterior), and recommended action (Decision) is essential for the correct interpretation of quantitative model outputs.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim guides the interpretation of all empirical results, separating statistical evidence from theoretical belief or managerial decision.

## Notes

This claim directly challenges the common practice of misinterpreting p-values as measures of evidence or theoretical truth.

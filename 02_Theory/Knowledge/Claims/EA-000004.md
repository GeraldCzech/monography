## Statement

Giving can be motivated by the rational, impartial goal of maximizing positive outcomes based on evidence.

## Implications for BEBA

This presents a competing, non-brand-centric model of giving that BEBA must account for or integrate.

## Notes

This is the core tenet of the Effective Altruism movement.

## Statement

The perceived Integrity of a nonprofit organization is a key antecedent of donor trust.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim links a specific architectural component (Integrity) to a key psychological mediator (trust) for enabling donor behaviour.

## Notes

The paper's argument is theoretical and supported by the factor structure; it does not present a path model directly testing this relationship.

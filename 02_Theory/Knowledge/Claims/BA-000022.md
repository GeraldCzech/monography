## Statement

Recognition is a more appropriate awareness measure for new or niche brands than for established brands.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that nonprofit awareness measures should vary by organizational maturity and public visibility.

## Notes

The reviewed material contrasts recognition with recall or top-of-mind awareness.

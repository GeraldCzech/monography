## Statement

Models like the Theory of Planned Behavior (TPB) and Theory of Reasoned Action (TRA) effectively explain intention formation but are weak at explaining the conversion of intention into behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must incorporate post-intentional theoretical mechanisms rather than relying solely on TPB/TRA antecedents.

## Statement

The effectiveness of an NPO's internal branding process is contingent on leadership style.

## Implications for BEBA

It establishes that the BEBA is not a static system and its effects are amplified or dampened by contextual factors like leadership.

## Notes

This general claim is supported by a specific finding related to charismatic leadership; its generalizability to other leadership styles is not tested.

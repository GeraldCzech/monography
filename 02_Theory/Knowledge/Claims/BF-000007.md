## Statement

In the nonprofit sector, greater stakeholder familiarity with an organization can be negatively associated with intentions to provide resource support.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s emphasis on the valence and substance of brand knowledge rather than awareness alone.

## Notes

The author speculates that the negative effect may reflect pre-existing negative perceptions of the focal organization.

## Statement

The operation of warm glow in digital and crowdfunding environments is not sufficiently established.

## Evidence

The synthesis identifies limited evidence on how social sharing, real-time feedback, and anonymity on digital platforms affect self-image and social-image components of warm glow.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should treat digital giving features as possible behavioural architecture elements but should avoid assuming their effects without further evidence.

## Notes

Not a confirmed effect claim. This is a gap claim.

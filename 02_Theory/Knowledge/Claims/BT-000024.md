## Statement

The antecedent model for donating time is more similar to the model for donating money than either is to the model for donating blood.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that BEBA strategies can be grouped for time/money donations but that blood donation requires a distinct strategic approach.

## Notes

This implies that blood donation is a more "specialized" form of helping, psychologically distinct from other forms of institutionalized giving.

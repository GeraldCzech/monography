## Statement

Supporter commitment can be developed via a cognitive-learning pathway initiated by identification with the charity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim defines one of two core architectural pathways for brand effectiveness, aligning with BEBA's search for competing explanations.

## Notes

This describes the `Identification -> Behavioral Involvement -> Perceived Sense of Community` mechanism from Hassay & Peloza (2009).

## Statement

Providing unconditional material benefits, such as thank-you gifts, can reduce subsequent donations.

## Implications for BEBA

This introduces motivational crowding-out as a critical risk for a Brand-Enabled Behaviour Architecture to manage.

## Notes

This effect is counterintuitive to some fundraising practices but is supported by replication evidence.

## Statement

Organizational philanthropy is a strategic practice aimed at securing legitimacy and competitive position within a social field.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the BEBA assumption that brand management is a strategic, relational activity within a competitive field.

## Notes

The summary notes this is widely understood, especially in the context of the nonprofit sector and Corporate Social Responsibility.

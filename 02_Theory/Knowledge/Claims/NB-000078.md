## Statement

A positive nonprofit brand image is a significant positive predictor of an individual's intention to donate money.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the core BEBA proposition that a brand's cognitive and affective architecture directly enables supportive financial behaviours.

## Notes

The model explained 31% of the variance in monetary giving intentions. The study measured intention, not actual behaviour.

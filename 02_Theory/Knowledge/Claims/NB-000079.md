## Statement

A positive nonprofit brand image is a significant positive predictor of an individual's intention to donate time.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the core BEBA proposition that a brand's architecture enables non-financial supportive behaviours like volunteering.

## Notes

The model explained 24% of the variance in time giving intentions. The study measured intention, not actual behaviour.

## Statement

Earned income generates greater warm-glow utility than windfall income in giving contexts.

## Evidence

Luccasen & Grossman (2017)

## Implications for BEBA

Donor resource origin may moderate motivation-to-giving pathways and should not be collapsed into income level alone.

## Notes

The SKU states that lab evidence is robust but field replication is still needed.

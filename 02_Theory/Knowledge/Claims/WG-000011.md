## Statement

The presence of warm glow utility explains why individuals give even when their personal donation has no marginal impact on the provision of the public good.

## Implications for BEBA

This explains the persistence of giving behaviours in large-scale contexts where individual impact is negligible.

## Notes

This is a key empirical prediction of the impure altruism model, relevant for mass-market fundraising.

## Statement

High-arousal affective states toward brands, such as brand love, are measurable constructs distinct from lower-arousal states like brand attitude.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that strong emotional bonds could be a key mechanism for driving pro-social behaviour, especially under conditions of uncertainty.

## Notes

While measurable, the source document indicates a lack of full consensus on the precise dimensionality of these high-affect constructs.

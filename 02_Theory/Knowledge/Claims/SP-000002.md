## Statement

The presence of a formal strategic plan in a nonprofit organization is a positive predictor of the presence of a long-term financial plan.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes a link between different components of the internal architecture, supporting the idea that brand-enabling systems are internally consistent.

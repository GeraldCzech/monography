## Statement

The positive association between social identification and charitable giving is stronger for self-reported intentions than for objectively measured behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim directly addresses the intention-behavior gap, a core phenomenon BEBA seeks to explain, by qualifying the predictive power of identity constructs.

## Notes

Based on meta-analytic evidence, this highlights the well-documented intention-behavior gap in the pro-social domain.

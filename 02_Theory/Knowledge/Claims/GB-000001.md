## Statement

Efforts to implement a consistent global nonprofit brand can create conflict between headquarters and decentralized country offices.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim shows that brand-enabled behaviour depends on governance structures and internal acceptance, not only on external stakeholder perception.

## Notes

The claim is particularly relevant to international nonprofits with decentralized organizational structures.

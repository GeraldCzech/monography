## Statement

Role clarity provided to volunteers by a nonprofit is a positive predictor of their retention.

## Implications for BEBA

This points to the importance of the 'service design' aspect of the brand experience in the BEBA framework.

## Notes

The cited evidence for this claim comes from a study focusing on mandatory volunteers, which is a key boundary condition.

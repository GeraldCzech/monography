## Statement

A donor's self-concept does not mediate the relationship between nonprofit brand image and giving intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that brand image may influence donation intention through direct signalling rather than self-concept integration.

## Notes

Evidence is based on cross-sectional survey data from China and may depend on the specific brand equity model used.

## Statement

Nationally-specific, highly detailed taxonomies like the NTEE are not robust for international comparison due to a lack of economy and organising power.

## Implications for BEBA

This explains why a US-centric model is inappropriate and why an internationally-designed system is required for BEBA's comparative scope.

## Notes

The critique focuses on the NTEE's complexity, cultural specificity, and difficulty of application in data-poor environments.

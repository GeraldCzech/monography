## Statement

No single, universally accepted model adequately explains the intention-behavior gap across all behavioral domains.

## Implications for BEBA

This justifies BEBA's approach as an integrative framework rather than a monolithic, single-pathway model.

## Notes

The literature points towards fragmentation and the need for theory integration.

## Statement

The predictive validity of standard intention-based donation models is moderated by cultural context.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that BEBA should avoid assuming universal stability of brand-enabled behavioural pathways across cultural settings.

## Notes

The reviewed material suggests attenuation in collectivist, developing, or non-Western contexts but does not establish a single universal moderation pattern.

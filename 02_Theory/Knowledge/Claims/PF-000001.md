## Statement

Service-providing nonprofits in Austria, particularly within the welfare sub-sector, are characterized by a high degree of dependence on government funding.

## Evidence

Pape et al., 2019; Pennerstorfer et al., 2020; Meyer et al., 2023

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that for many NPOs, the primary "customer" for brand-related behaviour is the state, not the public.

## Notes

The summary indicates this funding can exceed 80% of revenue in some segments.

## Statement

Within the Brand Understanding Block, brand self-connection and brand associations function as core conditions directly contributing to high overall brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This indicates that BEBA should treat stakeholder meaning structures and identity linkage as central mechanisms in brand-enabled behaviour.

## Notes

Brand awareness and reputation are described as peripheral rather than core contributors in this block.

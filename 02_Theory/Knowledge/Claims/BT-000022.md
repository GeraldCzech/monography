## Statement

Overall satisfaction with a brand is a positive antecedent of brand trust.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that perceived supporter value or impact (the BEBA equivalent of satisfaction) is a key input for building supporter trust.

## Notes

Finding is based on low-involvement consumer goods (shampoo, beer) in a Spanish context.

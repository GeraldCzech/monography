## Statement

Network and process-oriented brand equity models provide greater explanatory adequacy for consumer behaviour than static linear brand equity indices.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s architectural choice to model nonprofit brand equity as a behavioural process rather than as a static index.

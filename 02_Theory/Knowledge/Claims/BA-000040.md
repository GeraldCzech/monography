## Statement

The Brand Attachment model in Park et al. (2010) applies to positive brand bonds rather than aversive brand relationships.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should not use positive attachment theory to explain nonprofit brand rejection or antagonism without additional theory.

## Notes

This is a boundary-condition claim from the reviewed synthesis.

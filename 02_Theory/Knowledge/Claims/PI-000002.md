## Statement

Perceived income inequality has no direct effect on charitable giving in Austria and Germany.

## Implications for BEBA

This cautions BEBA against assuming a simple direct pathway from macro-social inequality perceptions to donation behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed material notes that competing indirect pathways may mask the direct effect.

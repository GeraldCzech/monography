## Statement

Brand familiarity does not have a significant direct effect on donor-based brand equity for childhood-related NGOs in the tested Spanish sample.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies awareness-based models by indicating that being known may be insufficient for behavioural effectiveness in nonprofit branding.

## Notes

The authors suggest that familiarity may still operate indirectly or conditionally.

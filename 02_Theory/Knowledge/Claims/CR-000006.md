## Statement

Cause-Related Marketing (CRM) campaigns can be perceived by consumers as more selfish than direct corporate donations.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must model the potential for stakeholder skepticism and perceived inauthenticity as a failure condition for brand-based behavioural interventions.

## Notes

Highlights a key tension and potential negative outcome of a popular social value strategy.

## Statement

Fundraising appeals emphasizing donor-centric benefits (e.g., warm glow) are more effective in increasing donations than appeals emphasizing recipient-centric benefits.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes brand communications as a key mechanism within BEBA for activating specific pro-social motivations.

## Notes

The cited evidence from a large-scale field experiment found that recipient-benefit appeals had no significant average effect on giving.

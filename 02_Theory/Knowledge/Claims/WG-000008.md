## Statement

The social-image component of warm glow derives from being perceived by others as generous and is sensitive to the observability of giving.

## Evidence

Grossman & Levy (2024); Harbaugh (1998)

## Implications for BEBA

Social visibility may moderate the effect of brand reputation and public association with nonprofit brands on giving behaviour.

## Notes

Closely related to prestige and signalling models.

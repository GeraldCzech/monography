## Statement

An NPO's brand orientation behaviour functions as an extrinsic motivator that initiates a psychological process of motivation internalization among staff.

## Implications for BEBA

It provides a core psychological theory (SDT) to explain *how* the BEBA translates organisational-level brand strategy into individual-level, self-determined behaviour.

## Notes

This is the theoretical interpretation of the empirical mediation model, not a direct empirical test of Self-Determination Theory itself.

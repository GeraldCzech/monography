## Statement

International nonprofit brands are perceived as more legitimate when they are authentically linked to the organization’s mission.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the BEBA view that brand-enabled behaviour depends on perceived mission coherence rather than brand visibility alone.

## Notes

The evidence is qualitative and focused on international nonprofit organizations.

## Statement

The intention to perform an institutionalized pro-social behavior is positively predicted by the strength of the associated role-identity.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates role-identity as a direct antecedent of behavioral intention, making it a key target for brand-enabled interventions.

## Notes

The effect size is likely underestimated due to low reliability (Cronbach's alpha .39-.48) of the two-item role-identity scale used in the study.

## Statement

Brand image is a multidimensional construct representing the overall mental perception of a brand held by consumers.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports treating brand image as a structured cognitive representation rather than a single undifferentiated attitude.

## Notes

The reviewed material presents this as established in the for-profit literature.

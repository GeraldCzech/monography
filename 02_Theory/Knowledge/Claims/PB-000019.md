## Statement

Past behavior is the strongest predictor of future intentions for institutionalized pro-social behavior, relative to other antecedents in the tested model.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests a BEBA should prioritize behavioral activation and repetition over purely attitudinal or identity-based messaging in the early stages.

## Notes

The claim is based on cross-sectional data, which the authors note cannot definitively prove the proposed causal ordering.

## Statement

Higher organizational identification with a nonprofit is positively associated with higher donation intentions toward that nonprofit.

## Implications for BEBA

This establishes organizational identification as a direct antecedent of donation intention, making it a critical construct in the BEBA architecture.

## Notes

This relationship is widely supported and considered a consensus finding in the reviewed literature.

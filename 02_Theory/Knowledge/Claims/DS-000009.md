## Statement

Perceived descriptive social norms have a positive but modest statistical association with charitable donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This establishes a realistic effect size for social norms, positioning them as a contributing but non-determinative factor in the BEBA architecture.

## Notes

The meta-analytic effect size for overall social norms (descriptive and injunctive) on charitable giving is r = .18.

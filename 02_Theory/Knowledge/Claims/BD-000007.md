## Statement

The relationship between perceived nonprofit brand differentiation and donation intention is mediated by donor-brand self-concept congruence.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim specifies a key symbolic pathway (identity) through which brand architecture influences behavior, a central tenet of BEBA.

## Notes

This is a core mediation hypothesis for the symbolic function of a brand.

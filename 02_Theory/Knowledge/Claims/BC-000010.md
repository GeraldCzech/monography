## Statement

Donor brand commitment toward an NGO is composed of attitudinal and emotional dimensions in the tested measurement model.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports modelling commitment as a multidimensional psychological bond rather than as repeated behaviour alone.

## Notes

Attitudinal commitment includes relationship satisfaction, while emotional commitment includes feeling part of the NGO.

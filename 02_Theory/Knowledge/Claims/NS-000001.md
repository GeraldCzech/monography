## Statement

The long-term sustainability of a nonprofit organization is a function of its leadership, adaptability, and production capacity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a candidate theoretical model for NPO sustainability, a key outcome variable that BEBA aims to explain.

## Notes

This is a theoretical proposition from York (2015), cited by Hommerová & Severová (2018). Its empirical status is not specified in the summary.

## Statement

Brand Orientation positively influences internal brand equity within business-to-business firms.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the core BEBA mechanism where an internal, brand-led architecture builds valuable internal brand assets and employee alignment.

## Notes

While specific to B2B, this finding is presented as part of the general consensus on BO's positive effects.

## Statement

Individuals identifying as White exhibit significantly higher top-of-mind awareness of nonprofit organizations compared to individuals from racial and ethnic minority groups.

## Implications for BEBA

This highlights that brand equity, starting with awareness, is not equitably distributed, posing challenges for NPOs serving diverse communities.

## Statement

The value of cultural and social capital in philanthropy is not fixed but is contingent on the specific structure and dominant logic of the field in which it is deployed.

## Implications for BEBA

This implies that the value of a nonprofit's brand assets is not absolute but is determined by the rules of its specific competitive field.

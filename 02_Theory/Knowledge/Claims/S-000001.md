## Statement

Perceived inauthenticity or excessive publicity can reduce the warm glow effect of prosocial behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies skepticism as a potential inhibitor of brand-enabled behavioural reinforcement.

## Notes

Conditions for the cooling effect are not yet fully settled in the reviewed literature.

## Statement

An individual's habitus is not static and can be modified, challenging purely deterministic models of social reproduction.

## Implications for BEBA

This implies that brand-enabled behavior architectures can potentially influence or adapt to a changing habitus, not just cater to a static one.

## Notes

This claim stems from critiques of "strict reproductionist readings" of Bourdieu's theory.

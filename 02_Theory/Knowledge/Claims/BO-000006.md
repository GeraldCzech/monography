## Statement

Brand Orientation is a strategic orientation where organizational processes are guided by the imperative to create, develop, and protect brand identity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim defines the strategic mindset that serves as the necessary cultural and organizational precursor for implementing a BEBA.

## Notes

This is the foundational definition of the concept, widely accepted in the literature.

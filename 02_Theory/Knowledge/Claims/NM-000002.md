## Statement

Stakeholder resistance to funding marketing as an operational expense is a significant impediment to nonprofit marketing capacity.

## Implications for BEBA

This highlights the need for BEBA to justify brand investment as a mission-critical capacity, not merely an overhead cost.

## Notes

The source paper notes this as a point of consensus in prior literature (e.g., Helmig et al., 2004; Bennett & Savani, 2004) which their findings reinforce.

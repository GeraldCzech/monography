## Statement

Longitudinal evidence on whether integrated brand equity and TPB models predict sustained giving behaviour is not sufficiently covered in the reviewed literature.

## Implications for BEBA

This limits the evidential basis for claiming that brand-enabled behavioural architecture predicts durable donor behaviour over time.

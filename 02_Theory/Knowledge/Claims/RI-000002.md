## Statement

The strength of a pro-social role-identity is positively predicted by an individual's past behavior in that domain.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides the causal link for why a BEBA must focus on enabling behavior to build a stable, identity-based motivation.

## Notes

This claim describes the core feedback loop where behavior shapes identity.

## Statement

An individual's habitus, shaped by their social trajectory, conditions their dispositions toward giving, influencing which causes and donation practices are perceived as legitimate and appropriate.

## Implications for BEBA

This links the BEBA concept of a 'Behaviour Architecture' to the sociological construct of 'habitus', suggesting architectures resonate with socially conditioned dispositions.

## Notes

Direct empirical evidence for this claim within the specific context of charitable giving is lacking.

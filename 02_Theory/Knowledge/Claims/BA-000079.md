## Statement

An increase in a nonprofit's brand awareness (cognitive accessibility) causes a positive increase in an individual's perception of the descriptive social norm for supporting that nonprofit.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the fluency-as-prevalence heuristic as a core social-cognitive mechanism within the BEBA model.

## Notes

The underlying psychological mechanism (availability/recognition heuristic) is well-established, but its direct empirical test linking NPO brand awareness to social norms is a research gap.

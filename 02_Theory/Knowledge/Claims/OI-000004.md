## Statement

Organizational Identification (a group-level construct) and Donor Identity Salience (an individual-level construct) are empirically distinct.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the use of a dual-identity architecture within BEBA, separating the social-relational component from the individual-role component.

## Notes

Establishing the discriminant validity of these two constructs was a key prerequisite of the study's model.

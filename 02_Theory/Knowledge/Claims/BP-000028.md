## Statement

The perceived brand personality profiles of nonprofit organisations differ significantly from those of for-profit organisations, with NPOs scoring substantially higher on traits related to social competence and trustworthiness.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim reinforces the core rationale for BEBA by demonstrating that NPO brand personality profiles are empirically distinct from for-profit profiles.

## Notes

The comparison in the study was with a limited set of prominent for-profit brands (e.g., Coke, BMW), not a representative sample of the for-profit sector.

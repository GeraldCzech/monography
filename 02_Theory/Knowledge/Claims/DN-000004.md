## Statement

Donor-nonprofit identification positively influences donor identity salience in nonprofit contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA can model identification with a specific nonprofit as a mechanism that strengthens the donor role within the supporter’s self-concept.

## Notes

This finding contradicted the authors’ initial hypothesis about the dominant causal direction.

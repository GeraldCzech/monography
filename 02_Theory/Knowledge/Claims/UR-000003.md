## Statement

Perceptions of nonprofit reliability and ethicality reduce donor uncertainty by increasing confidence that the organization is responsible, trustworthy, moral, and reputable.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim directly supports BEBA's explanation of how nonprofit brands become behaviourally effective under uncertainty.

## Notes

The reviewed material frames this as a mechanism, but the summary does not report a direct empirical test of uncertainty reduction as a mediator.

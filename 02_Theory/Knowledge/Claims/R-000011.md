## Statement

The effect of donation observability on giving is moderated by cultural norms.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA cannot be a universalist theory; it must account for cultural context moderating the power of social and reputational mechanisms.

## Notes

In contexts where public displays of charity are discouraged, observability can decrease giving.

## Statement

Brand familiarity increases a nonprofit's likelihood of being considered for donation by increasing salience and perceived credibility.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the BEBA view of brands as cognitive heuristics that help donors navigate crowded charitable markets.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis presents familiarity as a mental shortcut rather than as a universally sufficient driver of donation intention.

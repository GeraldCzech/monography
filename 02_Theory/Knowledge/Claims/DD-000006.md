## Statement

The influence of nonprofit brand salience on new donor brand choice intention is stronger under low donor decision involvement than under high donor decision involvement.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA by identifying low involvement as a condition under which heuristic brand mechanisms are especially behaviourally effective.

## Notes

This claim concerns the relative strength of the salience pathway.

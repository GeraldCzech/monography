## Statement

Donor satisfaction has a positive causal effect on organisational identification in nonprofit service contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim shows how positive brand experiences (leading to satisfaction) can be converted into deeper, more resilient stakeholder relationships (identification).

## Notes

The model only explained 15% of the variance in organisational identification, suggesting satisfaction is an antecedent but other important antecedents exist.

## Statement

Higher-order brand associations are stronger predictors of sustained donor support than lower-order factors like brand awareness.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that BEBA's architecture should prioritize and differentiate between lower-order and higher-order brand associations.

## Notes

The summary indicates that the precise hierarchy and relative importance of brand equity drivers remains an active area of research.

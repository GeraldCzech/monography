## Statement

The positive effect of Subjective Norm on donation intention is moderated by cultural context.

## Implications for BEBA

This highlights that brand strategies aiming to leverage social norms must be culturally adapted.

## Notes

The effect is often stronger in collectivist cultures and weaker or non-significant in individualist ones.

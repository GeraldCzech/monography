## Statement

Integrating nonprofit brand equity constructs into the Theory of Planned Behavior increases explanatory power for stakeholder behavioral intentions.

## Implications for BEBA

This is a direct candidate proposition for testing whether BEBA generalises integrated brand-behaviour models to nonprofit contexts.

## Notes

This claim is extrapolated from for-profit evidence and is not directly validated in the reviewed nonprofit literature.

## Statement

The effects of Perceived Behavioural Control and Moral Norm on charitable donation intention are more consistently significant across contexts than the effect of Subjective Norm.

## Implications for BEBA

A brand's ability to enhance self-efficacy and reduce perceived barriers (influencing PBC) may be more critical than shaping social norms.

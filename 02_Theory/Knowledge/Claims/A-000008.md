## Statement

The configurations of brand conditions that lead to high consumer-based brand equity are not the logical inverse of the configurations that lead to its absence.

## Implications for BEBA

This qualifies BEBA models by indicating that successful and unsuccessful behavioural brand architectures may require separate explanation.

## Notes

The claim depends on configurational analysis using fs/QCA rather than symmetric linear modelling.

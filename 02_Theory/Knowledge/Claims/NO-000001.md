## Statement

Treating foundations, charities, and NGOs as a single NPO category obscures theoretically relevant differences in nonprofit brand equity formation.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should distinguish nonprofit organization types when modelling how brands become behaviourally effective.

## Notes

This is a conceptual claim advanced by the review rather than a direct empirical finding.

## Statement

There is no consensus in the nonprofit marketing literature on a standardized set of performance metrics.

## Implications for BEBA

This justifies the development of BEBA's "behavioural effectiveness" construct as a contribution to solving this measurement problem.

## Notes

The review identifies this as a significant gap, making cross-organizational comparisons difficult.

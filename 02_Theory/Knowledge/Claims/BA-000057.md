## Statement

Brand awareness measures are most interchangeable for medium market-share brands and least interchangeable for small and large market-share brands.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim challenges any BEBA measurement approach that treats awareness indicators as universally interchangeable across organisations.

## Notes

The reviewed material states that Romaniuk, Wight, and Faulkner challenge the practical interchangeability implication associated with Laurent et al. after mathematical transformation.

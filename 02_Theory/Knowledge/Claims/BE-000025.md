## Statement

The brand equity creation process consists of three causally related sub-systems: Brand Building, Brand Understanding, and Brand Relationship.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a candidate architectural structure for linking organisational brand inputs, stakeholder cognition, and relationship-based behavioural outcomes.

## Notes

The three-block structure was tested in the context of consumers’ favorite brands in urban centers in the United Kingdom.

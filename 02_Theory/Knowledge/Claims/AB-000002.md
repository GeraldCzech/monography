## Statement

Advocacy nonprofit brands tend to rely on proactive and cerebral communication strategies that tightly link the brand to a specific cause.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that advocacy brands may enable sustained identity-based behaviours differently from crisis-oriented relief brands.

## Notes

The claim is bounded to the advocacy organizations examined in the qualitative study.

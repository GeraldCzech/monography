## Statement

Approximately 20% of endowments were donated under full crowd-out conditions in Crumpler and Grossman’s experimental design.

## Evidence

Crumpler & Grossman (2008)

## Implications for BEBA

Warm glow may explain a non-trivial portion of charitable giving behaviour that does not depend on perceived organisational impact.

Specific empirical magnitude reported in the SKU.

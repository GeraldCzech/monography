## Statement

The distinction between brand personality and brand image remains conceptually ambiguous in the brand equity literature.

## Implications for BEBA

This warns BEBA to maintain clear construct boundaries between brand image and brand personality.

## Notes

The reviewed material states that some researchers treat the terms interchangeably whereas others argue for conceptual and empirical independence.

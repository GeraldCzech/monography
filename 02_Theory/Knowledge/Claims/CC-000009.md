## Statement

Monetary donation is a practice for converting economic capital into symbolic capital.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must model how brand interactions facilitate the conversion of economic capital into symbolic capital for the supporter.

## Notes

This mechanism is described as particularly evident in elite and upper-middle-class philanthropy.

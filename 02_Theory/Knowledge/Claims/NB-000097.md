## Statement

From the perspective of the general public, nonprofit brand equity is a multidimensional construct comprising Brand Awareness, Brand Trust, and Brand Commitment.

## Implications for BEBA

Provides a validated three-dimensional perceptual structure (Awareness, Trust, Commitment) that BEBA can link to behavioural outcomes.

## Notes

The validity of this dimensional structure is explicitly limited to the 'general public' stakeholder group in this study.

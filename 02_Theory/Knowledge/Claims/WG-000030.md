## Statement

In cause-related marketing contexts, small donation sizes generate warm glow more effectively than large highly publicized donation sizes.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim cautions BEBA against assuming that larger visible prosocial commitments always strengthen donor or consumer response.

## Notes

The reviewed material frames skepticism and cooling effects as an active area of investigation.

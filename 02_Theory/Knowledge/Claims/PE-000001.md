## Statement

Perceived operational effectiveness has a significant positive effect on both giving intention and giving behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies perceived effectiveness as a critical non-brand construct that must be integrated into the BEBA model alongside brand equity.

## Notes

Unlike brand equity, the effect of perceived effectiveness was robust across both intention and behavior.

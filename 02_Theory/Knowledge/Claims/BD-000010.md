## Statement

The direct causal effect of perceived nonprofit brand uniqueness on subsequent observed donation behaviour is not currently supported by direct experimental or longitudinal evidence.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim prioritises research to test a core, yet unproven, pathway from brand strategy to donor behaviour.

## Notes

The claim concerns a lack of evidence for a link to *observed behaviour*, not necessarily to self-reported intentions. This identifies a critical research gap.

## Statement

Public perception of nonprofits as 'Marketers' is linked to their use of corporate marketing language and competitive framing.

## Implications for BEBA

It identifies specific brand communication strategies as potential triggers for the counterproductive 'Marketer' perception.

## Notes

This claim is an inference from qualitative data about public reactions; it is not based on a controlled experiment showing a causal link.

## Statement

Brand trust mediates the relationship between other brand perceptions, such as brand anthropomorphism, and supportive intentions.

## Implications for BEBA

This positions brand trust as a key mediating hub within the BEBA architecture, linking antecedent perceptions to behavioral outcomes.

## Notes

This implies that building other brand assets may be ineffective if they do not first contribute to building trust.

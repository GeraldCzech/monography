## Statement

The lack of discriminant validity among the dimensions of Aaker's (1991) CBBE model is more severe for service and internet brands than for tangible goods brands.

## Implications for BEBA

This strongly suggests that a model developed for goods is unsuitable for highly intangible nonprofit brands.

## Notes

The degree of intangibility appears to be a key boundary condition for the structural validity of the Aaker model.

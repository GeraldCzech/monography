## Statement

Satisfaction, trust, and commitment have stronger effects on donation intentions than on actual donation behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim challenges brand equity models that treat positive relational evaluations as sufficient proxies for behavioural effectiveness.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports that effects on intentions are 3 to 8 times larger than effects on behavior.

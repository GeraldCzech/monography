## Statement

A stakeholder's perception of an NPO's 'Social Competence and Trust' is a significant positive predictor of their willingness to donate to that organisation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides direct empirical evidence for the central mechanism of the BEBA model: brand perception enabling stakeholder behaviour.

## Notes

The evidence links brand personality to donation intention, not observed donation behaviour.

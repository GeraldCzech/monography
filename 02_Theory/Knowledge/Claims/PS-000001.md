## Statement

Pro-social behaviours such as donating are better explained as socially embedded practices than as outcomes of isolated, rational-choice calculations.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the core sociological justification for the BEBA framework's departure from purely cognitive or economic models of behaviour.

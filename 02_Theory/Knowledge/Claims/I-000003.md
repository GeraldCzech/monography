## Statement

Household income moderates new donor brand choice pathways such that high-income donors rely more strongly on brand attitude and low-income donors rely more strongly on brand salience.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA by suggesting that donor resources or capacity shape whether evaluative or heuristic brand mechanisms dominate.

## Notes

Income is treated as a moderator analogous to decision involvement in the reviewed study.

## Statement

Cultural dimensions, socio-economic factors, or religiosity may explain the lack of scalar invariance in non-profit brand image measurement across countries.

## Implications for BEBA

This highlights the need for BEBA to incorporate cultural-level moderators that influence not just behavioural relationships but also the measurement of perceptual constructs.

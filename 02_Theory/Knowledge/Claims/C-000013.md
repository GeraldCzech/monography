## Statement

Anticipated warm glow is a plausible mediator between exposure to nonprofit brand cues and donation intention.

## Evidence

The synthesis proposes “anticipated warm glow” or “experienced warm glow” as candidate constructs within the BEBA measurement model and links warm glow to donation motivation and fundraising appeal effects.

Supported indirectly by the synthesis of: Andreoni, 1990; Crumpler & Grossman, 2008; List et al., 2021; Grossman & Levy, 2024.

## Implications for BEBA

Warm glow may be operationalized as a mediator connecting brand-enabled experiences, identity reinforcement, emotional reward, and donation behaviour.

## Statement

Warm-glow preferences can produce charitable allocation patterns that are inefficient from an impact-maximisation perspective.

## Evidence

Null (2011)

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should distinguish affectively satisfying giving from impact-oriented giving when explaining donation behaviour under uncertainty.

## Notes

Relevant to the dissertation’s question of behavioural effectiveness under uncertainty.

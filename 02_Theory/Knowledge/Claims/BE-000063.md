## Statement

There is no consistent evidence in the reviewed literature for a direct, causal effect of nonprofit brand equity on stakeholder perceptions of quality.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim creates the theoretical space for BEBA to propose a more nuanced role for the brand than as a simple antecedent signal of quality.

## Notes

The literature is characterized by conceptual ambiguity and a lack of direct modeling of this relationship.

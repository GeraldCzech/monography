## Statement

In decentralized international nonprofits, the brand provides a shared identity that supports internal cohesion across staff, volunteers, country sections, and teams.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim extends BEBA from external donor behaviour to internal coordination and identity-guided behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed material describes the brand as “organizational glue” for decentralized nonprofits.

## Statement

The total effect of past donation on future donation is mediated by two distinct pathways: (a) cognitive automaticity and (b) relational commitment to the nonprofit.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim represents the central mediating mechanism of the BEBA model, integrating relational and habitual pathways.

## Notes

This is a key theoretical synthesis for BEBA, but the review notes a lack of integrated longitudinal studies that test both pathways concurrently.

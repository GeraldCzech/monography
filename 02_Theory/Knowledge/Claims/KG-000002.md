## Statement

Representing a developing social science theory as a knowledge graph provides a more precise, communicable, and extensible operationalization of that theory than a prose-based description or a single path diagram.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies the use of a knowledge graph as the central medium for representing and managing the BEBA theory.

## Notes

The claim makes a specific comparison against two common alternatives: prose and path diagrams.

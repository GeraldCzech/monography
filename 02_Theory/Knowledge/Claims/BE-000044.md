## Statement

Aggregate-level analysis of brand equity metrics conceals significant differences between consumer loyalty segments.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA measurement should prioritise disaggregated analysis across supporter segments rather than relying on aggregate brand-equity indicators.

## Notes

This claim concerns research and measurement practice rather than a direct psychological mechanism.

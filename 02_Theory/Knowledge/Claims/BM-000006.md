## Statement

The psychological structure of brand perception is context-dependent, requiring distinct measurement models for different domains such as commercial, non-profit, and B2B.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the direct rationale for BEBA's specific focus on adapting and validating constructs for the non-profit sector.

## Notes

This justifies the rejection of a "one-size-fits-all" approach to brand equity measurement.

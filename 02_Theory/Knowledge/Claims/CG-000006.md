## Statement

Charitable giving is driven by a mix of motives, including altruism, egoism (warm glow), values, trust, and social context.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA's premise that a brand architecture must appeal to multiple, non-altruistic motivations to be effective.

## Notes

The reviewed literature presents this as a strong consensus, dismissing purely altruistic models as inadequate.

## Statement

Charitable giving is better explained by an impure altruism model that combines concern for recipient welfare with warm glow utility than by a pure altruism model.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s premise that nonprofit brands shape behaviour through motivational architectures rather than only through beliefs about impact.

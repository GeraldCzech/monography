## Statement

Most charitable donors are motivated by a combination of pure altruism and warm glow rather than by pure altruism alone.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim positions nonprofit brand effectiveness as dependent on both perceived beneficiary impact and donor-centred psychological rewards.

## Statement

The dominant paradigm for brand image measurement between 1991-2016 involved a two-stage process combining qualitative association elicitation with quantitative association evaluation.

## Implications for BEBA

This dominant research paradigm provides an external validation for the staged structure of the BEBA Knowledge Pipeline.

## Notes

This hybrid approach is noted as a common and accepted practice in the reviewed literature.

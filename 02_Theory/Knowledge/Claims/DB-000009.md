## Statement

Attitudinal donor loyalty has a weak or non-significant statistical relationship with actual donation behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This strongly supports BEBA's core premise that bridging the intention-action gap is a critical and non-trivial function of a behavioural architecture.

## Notes

The model explained a very low variance in donation behavior (R² = .04 and .05), highlighting a significant loyalty-behavior gap.

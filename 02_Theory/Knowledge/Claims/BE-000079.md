## Statement

Bayesian evidence synthesis provides a more rigorous, transparent, and epistemologically sound method for accumulating knowledge from fragmented literature than NHST-based meta-analysis or narrative review.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies the use of Bayesian methods for the 'Evidence' stage of the BEBA knowledge pipeline.

## Notes

This claim underpins the project's epistemological choice to treat scientific knowledge as provisional belief updated by evidence.

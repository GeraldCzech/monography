## Statement

Charitable giving can be motivated by the fulfillment of a moral duty, independent of a calculation of its consequences.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies a deontological motivation that brands might appeal to through justice- or duty-based framing.

## Notes

Contrasts with consequentialist frameworks like effective altruism.

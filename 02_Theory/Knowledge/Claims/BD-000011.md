## Statement

Perceived brand differentiation has a positive and statistically significant direct effect on an individual's actual donation behaviour, controlling for brand awareness and brand image.

## Implications for BEBA

Testing this claim is a central goal for BEBA, as it addresses the critical intention-behaviour gap and aims to establish a direct link from brand strategy to behaviour.

## Notes

This represents a major documented research gap; the link is widely assumed in practitioner literature but lacks robust, direct empirical evidence for actual behaviour.

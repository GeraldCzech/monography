## Statement

Descriptive norms (operating via social proof) and injunctive norms (operating via social approval) are conceptually and empirically distinct constructs.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides the justification for modeling descriptive and injunctive norms as separate architectural components with distinct causal pathways.

## Statement

Low-familiarity consumers update post-consumption expectations more strongly through service experiences than high-familiarity consumers.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the distinction between a novice learning loop and an expert reinforcement loop in BEBA.

## Notes

The evidence is correlational rather than causal.

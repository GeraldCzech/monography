## Statement

The success of Cause-Related Marketing campaigns is moderated by the perceived fit between the sponsoring brand and the social cause.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a specific, testable moderator variable (fit) that should be included in any BEBA model involving brand partnerships.

## Notes

This is a well-established boundary condition in the CRM literature.

## Statement

Brand audits can identify gaps between intended brand meaning and stakeholder perceptions.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports using audits as diagnostic tools for detecting misalignment between nonprofit brand architecture and stakeholder behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed material presents brand audits as a managerial tool rather than as an empirically tested intervention.

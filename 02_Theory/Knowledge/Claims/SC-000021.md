## Statement

Alignment between a nonprofit's brand identity and a stakeholder's self-concept positively predicts their giving intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies self-concept alignment as a key pathway through which brand associations enable behaviour.

## Notes

The act of giving is framed as a form of identity expression.

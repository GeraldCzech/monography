## Statement

An individual's top-of-mind awareness of nonprofit organizations is positively correlated with their level of education and income.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies key demographic segments where the foundational brand-enabled behaviour of awareness is weaker, requiring targeted interventions.

## Notes

The finding is based on data from a single US county (San Diego).

## Statement

The relationship between brand awareness and brand equity is moderated by the valence of brand associations.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim necessitates that the BEBA model accounts for the quality and valence of brand signals, not just their intensity or reach.

## Notes

For organizations with negative public information, higher awareness may be associated with lower, not higher, brand equity.

## Statement

Low brand recognition is a primary marketing challenge for small nonprofits, limiting their stakeholder engagement.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim directly validates BEBA's focus on brand equity as a central component of an effective behavioural architecture for NPOs.

## Notes

This finding is particularly relevant for the "smaller, local non-profits" that were the focus of the study.

## Statement

In co-created nonprofit brand equity models, brand awareness has a greater relative influence on brand equity for internal stakeholders than for external stakeholders.

## Implications for BEBA

This qualifies how the BEBA operates, suggesting internal actors' brand perceptions are more strongly anchored in direct experience and awareness.

## Notes

The "internal stakeholders" in this context were military recruits undergoing mandatory training.

## Statement

For nonprofit brands, Brand Trust is a distinct and more powerful explanatory dimension for stakeholder-brand relationships than generalized constructs such as 'brand image' or 'brand personality'.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the elevation of Brand Trust to a primary, independent construct within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

The authors argue that trust should not be subsumed under broader concepts, but treated as a core dimension in its own right.

## Statement

The relative influence of core brand equity drivers on donor behaviour changes systematically over multi-year periods.

## Implications for BEBA

This mandates that the BEBA model must be adaptive, allowing the weighting of its components to change over time.

## Notes

This claim is based on an inference from adjacent commercial-sector research and is not yet supported by direct nonprofit longitudinal data.

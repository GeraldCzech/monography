## Statement

Donors use the personification of a nonprofit into a brand personality as a cognitive heuristic to simplify evaluation under uncertainty.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim specifies the cognitive mechanism by which the brand architecture operates for individual donors, directly addressing BEBA's core problem.

## Notes

This reflects the paper's core theoretical mechanism rather than a direct empirical measurement of cognitive load.

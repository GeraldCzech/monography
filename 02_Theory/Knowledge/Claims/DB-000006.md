## Statement

A model using brand familiarity, brand associations, and brand commitment explains 56.7 percent of the variance in donor-based brand equity for the tested childhood-related NGO sample.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies a testable comparator model for BEBA’s explanation of nonprofit brand-enabled behaviour.

## Notes

The model was tested with PLS-SEM on a convenience sample of 131 Spanish respondents.

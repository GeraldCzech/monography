## Statement

Classifying nonprofit organisations by their primary economic activity at the establishment level produces more valid cross-national comparisons than classifying by legal status or at the enterprise level.

## Implications for BEBA

This establishes the correct unit of analysis and classification basis for segmenting the sector in BEBA's organisational-level models.

## Notes

This approach is chosen to increase the internal homogeneity of categories and align with national income accounting practices.

## Statement

Cultural dimensions such as individualism and collectivism moderate the relative importance of brand equity blocks and their translation into behavioral outcomes.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should treat the behavioural effectiveness of nonprofit brands as culturally contingent.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis identifies culture as a boundary condition.

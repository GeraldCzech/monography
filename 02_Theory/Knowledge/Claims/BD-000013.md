## Statement

Perceived brand differentiation of a nonprofit has a positive effect on stakeholders' intention to donate and their commitment to the organization.

## Implications for BEBA

Validates that brand differentiation influences precursors to behaviour, supporting an important pathway within the architecture.

## Notes

The evidence is noted as being stronger for intentional outcomes than for actual behaviour, highlighting the intention-behaviour gap.

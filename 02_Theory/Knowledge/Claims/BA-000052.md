## Statement

Top-of-mind awareness, unaided awareness, and aided awareness represent a hierarchy of brand-memory retrieval difficulty.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim implies that BEBA should model mental availability as differentiated retrieval potential rather than as a single undifferentiated awareness score.

## Notes

Top-of-mind awareness is described as the most difficult retrieval task, unaided awareness as intermediate, and aided awareness as the least difficult.

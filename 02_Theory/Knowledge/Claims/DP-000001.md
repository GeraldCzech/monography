## Statement

The profile of a "blood only" donor (younger, higher risk-propensity, unmarried) is significantly different from the profile of a "money only" donor (older, female, higher income, lower risk-propensity), indicating they respond to different life circumstances and dispositions.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides evidence that different behavioural pathways in the architecture require distinct, tailored brand-enabled activation strategies.

## Notes

The explanatory power of the model is low (pseudo-R²=.06), meaning these profiles represent statistical tendencies, not deterministic rules.

## Statement

Fundraising appeals that emphasize the donor’s emotional benefit generate higher participation or donation amounts than appeals focused solely on recipient benefit.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim implies that nonprofit brand communications can increase behavioural effectiveness by explicitly activating warm glow mechanisms.

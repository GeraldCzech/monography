## Statement

The validity of the proposed nonprofit branding effectiveness model is bounded by the exclusion of government stakeholders from the empirical study.

## Implications for BEBA

This qualifies BEBA by indicating that stakeholder pathways involving government actors require additional evidence.

## Notes

The study included donors, volunteers, beneficiaries, and media but excluded the government perspective.

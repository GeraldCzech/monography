## Statement

Sector-wide interventions aimed at increasing public confidence in nonprofits are likely ineffective if they do not first establish a baseline of public awareness.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a strategic directive for nonprofit brand management, prioritizing awareness-building over subsequent trust-building campaigns.

## Notes

This is a key conclusion derived from the study's finding that awareness is the most significant predictor of confidence.

## Statement

Existing nonprofit brand valuation methods do not adequately capture non-financial behaviours such as volunteering, advocacy, or mission impact.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim challenges financially narrow conceptions of nonprofit brand equity and supports a behaviour-based valuation logic.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports concerns about methodological flaws and incomplete behavioural coverage in brand valuation.

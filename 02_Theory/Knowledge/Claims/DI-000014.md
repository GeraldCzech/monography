## Statement

Attitude, subjective norm, and perceived behavioural control are significant positive predictors of charitable donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This establishes the core psychological antecedents of intention that a nonprofit brand must influence.

## Notes

The claim's robustness is supported by a meta-analysis covering multiple donation types (money, blood, organ).

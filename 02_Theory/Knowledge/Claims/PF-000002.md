## Statement

Framing a price reduction for giving as a "matching grant" is more effective at increasing donations than framing it as a "rebate".

## Implications for BEBA

This shows that a brand's communication strategy (framing) is a critical component of its behavioural architecture.

## Notes

This is presented as a reliably replicated finding.

## Statement

Warm glow can influence donation intention by contributing to a favourable affective evaluation of the giving act.

## Evidence

Andreoni (1990); List et al. (2021); Grossman & Levy (2024)

## Implications for BEBA

Warm glow may be modelled as an antecedent to affective attitude or donation intention in TPB/IBM-compatible behavioural layers.

## Notes

This claim links warm glow to behavioural intention but should be distinguished from brand attitude.

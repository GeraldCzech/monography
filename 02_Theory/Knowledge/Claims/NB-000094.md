## Statement

The perceived Integrity of a nonprofit brand is positively correlated with an individual's likelihood of contributing.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides evidence that a specific brand personality dimension functions as a lever for influencing donor behavior intentions.

## Notes

The study measured self-reported likelihood to contribute (money, time, or goods), not actual donation behavior.

## Statement

Brand equity research developed from financial conceptualisation in the 1980s to foundational customer-based and managerial model-building in the 1990s and broad contextual diversification after 2000.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim positions BEBA as a response to the historical expansion and fragmentation of brand equity theory.

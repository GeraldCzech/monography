## Statement

A middle-range theory-building stage is a necessary intermediary step to bridge quantitative statistical evidence and formal computational ontology.

## Implications for BEBA

This justifies the explicit theory-building phase in the BEBA project, preventing a direct and potentially flawed leap from data to ontology.

## Notes

This stage provides the conceptual abstraction and bounding required for successful formalisation into a machine-readable format.

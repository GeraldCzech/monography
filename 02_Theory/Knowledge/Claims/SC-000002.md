## Statement

An individual donor's self-concept has a direct positive effect on their giving intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim identifies self-concept as a psychological mechanism linking nonprofit brands to donation intention.

## Notes

Evidence is based on cross-sectional survey data from China and concerns giving intention rather than observed giving behaviour.

## Statement

The intention to perform an institutionalized pro-social behavior is positively predicted by past behavior in the same domain.

## Implications for BEBA

This emphasizes the critical role of facilitating initial and repeated behaviors to build a foundation for future intentions.

## Notes

This finding supports the 'habit loop' concept as a core driver of sustained pro-social action.

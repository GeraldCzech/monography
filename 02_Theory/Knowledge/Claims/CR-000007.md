## Statement

The 2008 financial crisis was associated with an increase in market concentration within the Austrian social services sub-sector.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides evidence that systemic crises can reshape the competitive environment, potentially altering the brand strategies required for survival.

## Notes

The evidence for this effect is specific to the social services sub-sector and has not been demonstrated as a sector-wide phenomenon.

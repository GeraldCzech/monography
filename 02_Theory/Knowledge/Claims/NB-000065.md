## Statement

Nonprofit branding effectiveness cannot be adequately measured by brand image metrics alone.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s rejection of perception-only brand equity models for nonprofit contexts.

## Notes

The claim reflects the paper’s critique of outside-in measurement approaches.

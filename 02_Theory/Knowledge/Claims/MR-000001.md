## Statement

Middle-range theory provides the appropriate level of abstraction for building a testable and practically relevant model of nonprofit brand-enabled behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies the 'Theory' stage of the BEBA pipeline, scoping the project to bounded, applicable models rather than a grand theory.

## Notes

This directs BEBA's theoretical ambition towards creating a useful, bounded conceptual model rather than a universal law.

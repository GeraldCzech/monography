## Statement

Nonprofit organisations engage with a more diverse set of stakeholders than typical for-profit firms.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s need to model multiple stakeholder behaviour pathways rather than a single customer pathway.

## Notes

Stakeholders named in the reviewed material include donors, volunteers, beneficiaries, media, and government.

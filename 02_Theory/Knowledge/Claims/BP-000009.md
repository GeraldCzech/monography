## Statement

Brand personality can operate as a relational heuristic when stakeholders perceive a brand as a relationship partner.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports BEBA’s relational architecture by linking brand personality to stakeholder support behaviour through perceived relationship quality.

## Notes

The reviewed material presents this as a theoretical mechanism rather than a directly isolated empirical effect.

## Statement

A Brand-Enabled Behaviour Architecture influences stakeholder behaviour through two distinct pathways: a pre-reflexive, habitus-driven pathway for routine behaviours, and a deliberative, intention-driven pathway for novel behaviours.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim integrates BEBA with competing psychological models by proposing a dual-process framework, specifying conditions for different behavioural pathways.

## Notes

This directly addresses the paradigm conflict between practice-theory and rational-choice models mentioned in the source summary.

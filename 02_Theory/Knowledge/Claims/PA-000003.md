## Statement

The critique of philanthropy as a threat to democracy is primarily applicable to large-scale, agenda-setting donors and not to mass-market, small-dollar giving.

## Implications for BEBA

This defines a boundary condition for the political critique of BEBA, suggesting it is most relevant for high-value stakeholders.

## Notes

This specifies the scale at which the "power" critique is most potent.

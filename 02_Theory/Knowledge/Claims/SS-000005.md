## Statement

The presence of cues providing social information about descriptive norms can causally alter actual donation behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates including social norms as a key situational factor influencing donation behaviour.

## Notes

This effect can backfire, reducing donations if the communicated norm is lower than what some donors would have otherwise given.

## Statement

The relationship between donation intention and actual donation behaviour is significantly weaker than the relationship between cognitive antecedents and donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

Explaining and bridging this gap is a core function of the "architecture" component of the BEBA framework.

## Notes

This gap is a primary justification for moving beyond purely psychological models of intention to include contextual and architectural factors.

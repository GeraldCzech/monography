## Statement

Brand Awareness and Brand Associations are empirically distinct and separable dimensions of consumer-based brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This distinction is critical for modeling how a nonprofit stakeholder moves from simple awareness to meaningful engagement based on brand meaning.

## Notes

This finding explicitly challenges prior studies (e.g., Yoo & Donthu, 2001) that reported these two dimensions collapsed into a single factor.

## Statement

Adding moral norms to the Theory of Planned Behavior increases explained variance in charitable donation intention from approximately 44% to approximately 52%.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports extending BEBA beyond social pressure toward internalised moral obligation as a brand-relevant behavioural mechanism.

## Notes

The variance estimate is reported in the reviewed synthesis as coming from a meta-analysis.

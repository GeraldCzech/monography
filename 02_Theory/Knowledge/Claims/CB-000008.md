## Statement

Customer-based brand equity can be modelled as a cognitive process in which brand awareness and brand image increase purchase propensity.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides a cognitive pathway for modelling how nonprofit brand knowledge becomes behaviourally enabling.

## Notes

The reviewed material frames this as a continuum of brand knowledge from awareness to image to propensity.

## Statement

A nonprofit brand's behavioural effectiveness increases with its perceived alignment with the habitus of a target donor segment.

## Implications for BEBA

It suggests that BEBA's "Behaviour Architecture" must account for pre-cognitive, socially ingrained dispositions (habitus).

## Notes

While a core theoretical implication, the review notes a lack of direct empirical research testing this for individual donation decisions.

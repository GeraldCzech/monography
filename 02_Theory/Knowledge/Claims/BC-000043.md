## Statement

The choice of a specific nonprofit brand is guided by a 'taste' that reflects the supporter's habitus and class position.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must model brands as signals of taste and milieu, not just as carriers of information about organisational effectiveness.

## Notes

This connects the abstract concept of habitus to the concrete act of brand selection.

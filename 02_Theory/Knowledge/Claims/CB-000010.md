## Statement

The factor loadings of a multidimensional consumer-based brand equity scale are equivalent between UK and Spanish consumer samples.

## Implications for BEBA

This indicates that cross-national nonprofit brand equity comparisons require metric invariance before latent scores can be meaningfully compared.

## Notes

The finding applies to the tested consumer product categories and countries only.

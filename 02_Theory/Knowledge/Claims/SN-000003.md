## Statement

Subjective norms predict charitable donation intention in some charitable giving contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This indicates that normative pressure should be included in BEBA but treated as context-sensitive rather than universally strong.

## Notes

The reviewed literature reports significant effects in some studies and non-significant effects for money donation in another.

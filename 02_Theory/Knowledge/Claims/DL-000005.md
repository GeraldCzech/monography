## Statement

Both organizational identification and donor identity salience exert direct, positive effects on donor loyalty.

## Implications for BEBA

This confirms that identity-building is a valid parallel strategy to satisfaction-building for fostering positive behavioural intentions.

## Notes

These identity-based paths operate in parallel to the satisfaction-loyalty path.

## Statement

Donor decision involvement positively moderates the relationship between nonprofit brand attitude and new donor brand choice intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA by showing that evaluative brand mechanisms become more influential when donors are highly involved in the decision.

## Notes

The relationship is stronger under high decision involvement.

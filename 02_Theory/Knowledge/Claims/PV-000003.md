## Statement

Perceived value mediates the relationship between perceived quality and stakeholder satisfaction in nonprofit contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This specifies the sequential pathway by which quality perceptions are translated into satisfaction, providing a core mechanism for BEBA.

## Notes

The strongest evidence comes from nonprofit sport clubs where members are also service consumers.

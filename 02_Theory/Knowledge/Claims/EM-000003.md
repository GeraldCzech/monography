## Statement

Bayesian evidence synthesis provides a more coherent epistemological foundation for cumulative social science than NHST-based meta-analysis.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies the choice of Bayesian meta-analysis as the core evidence aggregation engine for the BEBA project.

## Notes

The coherence stems from modeling scientific reasoning as belief revision rather than the binary hypothesis rejection common to NHST.

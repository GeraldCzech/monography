## Statement

Donor identity salience functions as a direct predictor of donor loyalty, not as a moderator of the relationship between organizational identification and loyalty.

## Implications for BEBA

This specifies how identity salience should be integrated into the BEBA model, favouring a direct-effect pathway over a more complex moderation model.

## Notes

The study explicitly tested a rival model with a moderation effect and found it to have a poorer fit.

## Statement

The predictive advantage of Brand Attachment over Brand Attitude Strength increases for behaviours requiring greater personal resource expenditure.

## Implications for BEBA

Behavioural difficulty should be modelled as a boundary condition for when nonprofit brand attachment matters most.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis identifies time, money, and social capital as relevant resource types.

## Statement

The dimensions of nonprofit brand equity are formed sequentially, with Brand Awareness acting as a necessary precondition for the development of Brand Trust and Brand Commitment.

## Implications for BEBA

Suggests a specific process model (Awareness -> Trust -> Commitment -> Behaviour) for BEBA to test and extend.

## Notes

The study's cross-sectional design proposes this sequence as a "relationship cycle" but cannot definitively prove the temporal order.

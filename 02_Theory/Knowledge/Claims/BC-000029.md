## Statement

A supporter's perceived sense of community with a charity is positively associated with their level of identification with that charity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim shows how a social construct (community) can shape an individual's identity, providing a social mechanism for brand internalization.

## Notes

This is based on Proposition 5 (P5) from the Hassay & Peloza (2009) conceptual model.

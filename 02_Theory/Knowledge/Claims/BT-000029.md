## Statement

Donor trust in a nonprofit brand is a positive predictor of attitudinal commitment to the organization.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports modeling commitment as a key psychological state that is enabled by trust, forming a critical relational pathway.

## Notes

This claim connects the cognitive state of trust to the more stable, relational state of commitment.

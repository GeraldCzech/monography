## Statement

The positive effect of brand anthropomorphism on donation intention is mediated by brand trust.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the view that brand signals designed to increase relatability operate by first building trust.

## Notes

Like TR-05, this demonstrates a specific, rather than universal, mediation pathway for trust.

## Statement

The influence of antecedent factors on long-term volunteer performance is fully mediated by volunteer role identity and behavioral intentions.

## Implications for BEBA

This positions identity and intention as the core mediating hub in the architecture, which external factors (like brand) must influence.

## Notes

The claim of "full mediation" is a strong one that may be subject to statistical debate, but it reflects the finding cited in the source text.

## Statement

The generalisability of warm-glow mechanisms beyond Western donor contexts is not yet established.

## Evidence

Amonhaemanon et al. (2024); Fraser (2018, 2022)

## Implications for BEBA

Austrian charitable giving should not be assumed to follow warm-glow patterns observed mainly in Western or Anglo-American samples without contextual validation.

## Notes

The evidence base is described as geographically concentrated.

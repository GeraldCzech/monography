## Statement

Brand credibility does not significantly moderate the positive relationship between brand association and brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This further refines the scope of Brand Credibility's influence, indicating it may not amplify all perceptual inputs equally.

## Notes

This is another important null moderation finding that helps define the boundaries of credibility's effect.

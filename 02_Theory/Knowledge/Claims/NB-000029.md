## Statement

Nonprofit brand trust is a more sector-specific dimension of nonprofit brand equity than general brand image or brand personality.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s emphasis on trust as a central mechanism through which nonprofit brands reduce uncertainty.

## Notes

The claim is grounded in the argument that nonprofit stakeholders face information asymmetry and depend on beliefs about ethical conduct, appropriate use of funds, and mission fidelity.

## Statement

Brands with higher consumer-based brand equity generate greater consumer preference than functionally similar brands with lower consumer-based brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the BEBA proposition that brand equity functions as an architecture shaping behavioural precursors.

## Notes

Preference was measured through conjoint analysis in the reviewed study.

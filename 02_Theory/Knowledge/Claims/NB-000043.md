## Statement

Nonprofit brand awareness has a positive effect on an individual donor's self-concept.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that awareness may contribute not only to salience but also to donor self-concept activation.

## Notes

Evidence is based on cross-sectional survey data from China.

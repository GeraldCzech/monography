## Statement

Stakeholder groups differ in their ability to assess a nonprofit's brand orientation and performance, with donors exhibiting lower awareness than parents.

## Implications for BEBA

This mandates that a BEBA model must account for information asymmetry and perception gaps across different stakeholder segments.

## Notes

This finding is specific to the comparison of donors and parents in a single charter school context and is based on the frequency of "unable to answer" responses.

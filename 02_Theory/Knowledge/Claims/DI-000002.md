## Statement

An individual's intention to donate is positively predicted by attitude toward donating, perceived norms, and perceived behavioural control.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the behavioural scaffold through which nonprofit brands can influence donation intention.

## Notes

The claim concerns intention rather than actual donation behaviour.

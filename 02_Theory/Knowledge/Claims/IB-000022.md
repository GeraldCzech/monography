## Statement

The effect of an NPO's brand orientation behaviour on its organizational performance is fully mediated by the serial relationship of staff emotional brand attachment and then staff service involvement.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim defines a specific, empirically-supported causal pathway for BEBA's internal architecture, linking organisational strategy to performance via psychological states.

## Notes

The supporting evidence is cross-sectional, so causality is inferred from the structural model rather than proven longitudinally.

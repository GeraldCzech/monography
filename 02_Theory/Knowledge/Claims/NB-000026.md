## Statement

Brand-related antecedents explain variance in nonprofit resource provider support intentions after controlling for individual altruistic disposition.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA's premise that nonprofit brands can enable behaviour beyond general prosocial motivation.

## Notes

Based on an exploratory single-organisation telephone survey using support intentions rather than observed behaviour.

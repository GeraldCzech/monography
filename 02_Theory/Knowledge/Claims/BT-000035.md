## Statement

The perceived trustworthiness of a nonprofit brand positively influences donation intention by increasing positive Attitude and enhancing Perceived Behavioural Control.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim represents a core theoretical proposition for how BEBA connects brand management to donor psychology.

## Notes

This is a proposed theoretical mechanism for BEBA, derived from the literature review rather than being a direct, consensus empirical finding. The summary notes that brand factors are not yet systematically integrated into TPB models.

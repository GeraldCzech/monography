## Statement

Donor-nonprofit organizational identification is a causal antecedent to the salience of a donor's identity.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides an evidence-based mechanism for how a social, group-level identification translates into an individual's self-concept.

## Notes

The study found this direction to be dominant, contrary to the authors' initial hypothesis.

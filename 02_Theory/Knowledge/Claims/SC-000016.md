## Statement

The ICNPO framework can be reliably applied across diverse national contexts to produce comparable data on the composition of the nonprofit sector.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim underpins the feasibility of using the ICNPO as a stable, cross-cultural foundation for BEBA's comparative research design.

## Notes

In the context of the 1992 paper, this is a central proposition and hypothesis, not a conclusion backed by extensive empirical evidence at the time.

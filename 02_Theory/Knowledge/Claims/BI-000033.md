## Statement

Brand image represents a more complex cognitive structure than brand awareness because it consists of associations linked to the brand in consumer memory.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should distinguish simple recognition from richer associative structures when modelling brand-enabled behaviour.

## Notes

The claim concerns the conceptual structure of brand knowledge rather than a behavioural outcome.

## Statement

Individuals who engage in multiple forms of donation (e.g., money and time) possess a socio-demographic and psychographic profile that is systematically different from individuals who engage in only a single form of donation.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must explicitly model the 'multiple-behaviour' supporter as a distinct and important actor type within the architecture.

## Notes

The identified profiles are based on correlates, not direct motivational measures, which were absent in the dataset.

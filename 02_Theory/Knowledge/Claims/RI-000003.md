## Statement

The strength of a pro-social role-identity is positively predicted by perceived social expectations.

## Implications for BEBA

Brand communications can actively shape identity by framing desired behaviors as socially expected and valued.

## Notes

This aligns with core tenets of symbolic interactionism and the Theory of Reasoned Action.

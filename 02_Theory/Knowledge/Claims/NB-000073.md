## Statement

A higher level of Nonprofit Brand Orientation is positively associated with higher self-reported overall organisational performance.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides direct empirical evidence for BEBA's core thesis that a brand-centric architecture enhances organisational effectiveness.

## Notes

The study uses a cross-sectional design, establishing a strong correlation but not definitive causality. Performance was self-reported by managers.

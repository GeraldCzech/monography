## Statement

High perceived fit between a nonprofit and a for-profit sponsor strengthens the nonprofit's brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This supports the core BEBA proposition that coherent architectural elements (like a well-aligned partner) enhance brand effectiveness.

## Notes

Finding is based on experimental studies using well-known, positively-regarded brands across identity, meaning, response, and relationship dimensions.

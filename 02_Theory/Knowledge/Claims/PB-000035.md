## Statement

Framing a giving subsidy as a "match" is more effective at increasing donations than framing it as a "rebate".

## Implications for BEBA

This shows that a brand's framing of an action can be a core component of the behavioural architecture itself.

## Notes

This is presented as a robustly supported psychological framing effect.

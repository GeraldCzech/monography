## Statement

A behavior-specific volunteer role identity is a positive and significant predictor of an individual's intention to volunteer.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must model how brand influences the formation and activation of this specific, predictive role identity.

## Notes

The predictive power is strongest when the identity is specific to the behavior (e.g., "blood donor") rather than general (e.g., "helpful person").

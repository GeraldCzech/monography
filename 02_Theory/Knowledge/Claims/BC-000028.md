## Statement

A supporter's level of behavioral involvement in a charity's activities is positively associated with their perceived sense of community.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim is crucial as it formalizes the 'behaviour first' principle, showing how action (involvement) builds a social-psychological outcome (sense of community).

## Notes

This is based on Proposition 4 (P4) from the Hassay & Peloza (2009) conceptual model and is the first step in the experiential learning pathway.

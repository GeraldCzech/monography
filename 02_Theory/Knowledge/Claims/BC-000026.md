## Statement

A supporter's level of identification with a charity is positively and directly associated with their level of commitment to that charity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim posits a direct path from identity to commitment, a critical mechanism for long-term supporter retention within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

This is based on Proposition 2 (P2) from the Hassay & Peloza (2009) conceptual model.

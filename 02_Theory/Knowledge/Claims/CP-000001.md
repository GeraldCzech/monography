## Statement

Coping planning is a more significant mediator of the intention-behavior link than action planning in behavioral domains requiring persistence against obstacles.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that a BEBA should focus on helping users anticipate and plan for barriers, not just schedule actions.

## Notes

The reviewed evidence points to mediation, suggesting coping plans work by translating intention into more effective self-regulation.

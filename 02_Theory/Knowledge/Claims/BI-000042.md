## Statement

The influence of specific brand image dimensions on donation intentions is contingent on the specific nonprofit brand's market position and public profile.

## Implications for BEBA

This finding validates the BEBA principle that a single, universal model of brand-driven behaviour is insufficient and context matters.

## Notes

The study demonstrated this by comparing a high-income, established brand (Barnardo’s) with a lower-income, event-driven brand (BBC Children in Need).

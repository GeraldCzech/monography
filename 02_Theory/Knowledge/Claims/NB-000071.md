## Statement

Nonprofit Brand Orientation (NBO) is a higher-order construct that can be reliably and validly measured as three distinct, first-order dimensions: Interaction, Orchestration, and Affect.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a psychometrically sound basis for measuring the internal organisational architecture component of BEBA.

## Notes

The three dimensions were deductively derived from Keller's (2000) brand report card and validated on Australian NPOs, resulting in a 12-item scale.

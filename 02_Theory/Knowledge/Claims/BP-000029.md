## Statement

The alignment between a nonprofit's brand personality and a stakeholder's self-concept mediates the positive effect of brand personality on donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies self-concept enhancement as a specific psychological mechanism within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

This suggests the brand can act as a vehicle for stakeholder self-expression and identity signaling.

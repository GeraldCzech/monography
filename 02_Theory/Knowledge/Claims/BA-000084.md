## Statement

Increased stakeholder awareness of a nonprofit brand enhances the perceived descriptive social norm of supporting that nonprofit.

## Implications for BEBA

Provides support for a social-cognitive pathway where brand perceptions influence social norm perceptions, a key architectural link.

## Notes

The mechanism is theorized to be the availability heuristic, but the review notes a lack of direct empirical tests in nonprofit contexts.

## Statement

Donor trust in a nonprofit brand is a positive predictor of donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This directly validates trust as a behaviour-enabling mechanism for the specific outcome of intended financial support.

## Notes

The claim is specific to donation *intention*, which may not perfectly translate to actual donation *behavior*.

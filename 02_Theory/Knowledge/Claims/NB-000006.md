## Statement

The effects of negative or ambivalent brand perceptions on integrated brand-equity and behavioural models are not sufficiently covered in the reviewed literature.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should treat brand skepticism, distrust, and brand hate as underdeveloped but potentially important constraints on brand-enabled behaviour.

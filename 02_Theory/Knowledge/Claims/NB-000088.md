## Statement

The perceived brand personality of a nonprofit organization is a four-dimensional construct consisting of Integrity, Nurturance, Sophistication, and Ruggedness.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a structured, measurable component for the nonprofit brand's behaviour architecture.

## Notes

The study notes that this structure is distinct from the for-profit model (Aaker, 1997) and was validated in a US context.

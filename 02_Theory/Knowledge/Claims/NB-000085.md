## Statement

A nonprofit's brand orientation is strongly and positively correlated with stakeholder perceptions of its organizational effectiveness.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides direct empirical evidence for BEBA's core premise that a brand architecture is linked to perceived performance.

## Notes

The finding is from a single case study and relies on perceptual data for performance, not objective metrics.

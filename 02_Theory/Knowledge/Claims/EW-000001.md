## Statement

The ETO workflow is a valid and rigorous methodology for moving from heterogeneous empirical inputs to a formal, reusable knowledge structure, by synthesizing principles from systematic review, qualitative analysis, and ontology engineering.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim validates the specific multi-stage knowledge pipeline used for theory development in BEBA.

## Notes

The validity of this claim rests on the successful synthesis of principles from three distinct methodological fields.

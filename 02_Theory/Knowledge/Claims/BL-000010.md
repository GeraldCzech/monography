## Statement

Brand trust is a positive antecedent of brand loyalty.

## Implications for BEBA

This validates the core BEBA proposition that trust is a key mechanism for translating brand perceptions into sustained pro-social behaviour.

## Notes

The study considers both attitudinal and behavioural aspects of loyalty.

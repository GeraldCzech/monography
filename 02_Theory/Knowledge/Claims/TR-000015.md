## Statement

The stable context of a trustor accounts for a portion of variance in their trust evaluations that is approximately equal in magnitude to the variance from the specific NPO evaluation.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must model the stable, pre-existing context of individuals as a primary component, not as a secondary control variable.

## Notes

The study found variance was split almost equally between the individual's stable context (50.91%) and their specific NPO evaluation (49.09%).

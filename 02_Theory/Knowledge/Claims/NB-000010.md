## Statement

Nonprofit brand equity has a direct positive influence on stakeholder behavioral intention independent of attitude.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that BEBA should test whether nonprofit brands can activate behaviour through pathways beyond explicit evaluative attitudes.

## Notes

The direct pathway is inconsistent in the reviewed literature and has not been directly established for nonprofit contexts.

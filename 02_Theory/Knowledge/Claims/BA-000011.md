## Statement

In the tested NGO donor model, brand associations are empirically represented by perceived reputation, perceived differentiation, and donor identification with the NGO’s values.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that BEBA should treat nonprofit brand associations as value-laden and identity-relevant evaluative structures.

## Notes

The empirical grouping differed from the authors’ initial theoretical structure because identification items loaded with brand associations.

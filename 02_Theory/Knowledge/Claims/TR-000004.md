## Statement

Trust in a charitable organization or donation platform increases the predictive validity of extended Theory of Planned Behavior models for charitable donation.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim positions trust as a key bridge between nonprofit brand equity and individual donation behaviour.

## Notes

The reviewed material emphasises trust especially in online and less familiar donation contexts.

## Statement

The strength of a pro-social role-identity is positively predicted by parental modeling of the behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that brand storytelling featuring relatable role models can be an effective strategy for identity formation.

## Notes

The paper treats parental modeling as a well-established influence on pro-social behavior.

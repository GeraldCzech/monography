## Statement

Senior nonprofit marketers in the UK do not perceive limited marketing budgets as the primary obstacle to successful nonprofit brand building.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies resource-based explanations of brand effectiveness by suggesting that internal architecture may matter more than financial marketing inputs.

## Notes

The claim concerns perceived obstacles among interviewed senior marketers, not measured budget effects on brand performance.

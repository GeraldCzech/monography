## Statement

The Evidence-Theory-Ontology (ETO) pipeline provides a transparent, replicable, and traceable methodology for converting fragmented scientific literature into a computable knowledge graph.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes the ETO pipeline as the core research process for developing the BEBA model.

## Notes

The success of this methodology is contingent on boundary conditions like the availability of quantitative evidence and conceptual clarity.

## Statement

Brand familiarity alone is insufficient to reliably increase donation intention without accompanying trust or positive brand associations.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies the role of familiarity by treating it as a salience or heuristic condition rather than a sufficient behavioural driver.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports nuanced findings, with some studies suggesting a credibility-enhancing effect and others finding null or negligible direct effects.

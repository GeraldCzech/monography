## Statement

Nonprofit brands reduce donor decision complexity by functioning as heuristics under conditions of information asymmetry and intangible outcomes.

## Implications for BEBA

This directly supports BEBA's core claim that brands structure behaviour under uncertainty.

## Notes

The document presents this as a theoretical mechanism rather than as a direct empirical test in the Austrian study.

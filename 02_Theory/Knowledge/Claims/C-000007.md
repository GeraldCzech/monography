## Statement

Donations made from earned income produce stronger warm glow than equivalent donations made from windfall income.

## Evidence

The synthesis states that earned income, compared with windfall gains, strengthens warm glow and donation outcomes.

Supported in the synthesis by: Luccasen & Grossman, 2017.

## Implications for BEBA

The context of the ask matters. Workplace giving, sponsored challenges, charity runs, or effort-linked campaigns may amplify warm glow because the donation is connected to earned or effortful resources.

## Notes

The synthesis notes a need for additional field evidence on this moderator.

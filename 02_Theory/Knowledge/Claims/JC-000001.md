## Statement

Gradual belief updating via Jeffrey Conditionalization is a more adequate formal model for integrating uncertain empirical results into a developing theory than strict Bayesian conditionalization.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides the formal mechanism for integrating evidence from multiple, uncertain empirical models within BEBA.

## Notes

This specifies the type of Bayesian updating used, accommodating the reality of uncertain evidence in social science.

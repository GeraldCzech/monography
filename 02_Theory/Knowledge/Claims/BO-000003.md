## Statement

The adoption of a brand orientation mindset is a precondition for the long-term safeguarding of organisational brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s view that brand-enabled behaviour begins with an internal strategic orientation rather than with external communication alone.

## Notes

The claim is derived from a conceptual model illustrated with service-organisation case studies rather than from formal quantitative testing.

## Statement

In philanthropic fields characterized by high uncertainty, nonprofits with higher symbolic capital attract resources more effectively than those competing on programmatic outcomes.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim directly links symbolic capital (brand) to resource attraction under uncertainty, a core question for BEBA.

## Notes

This is a key theoretical claim for BEBA derived from the framework, but the review suggests it is currently more of a conjecture than an empirically tested fact.

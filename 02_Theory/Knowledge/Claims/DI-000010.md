## Statement

Behavior-specific identity salience is a stronger predictor of corresponding prosocial behavior than general prosocial identity salience.

## Implications for BEBA

This informs the operationalization of identity constructs within BEBA, favoring specific over general measures for predicting behavior.

## Notes

For example, 'blood donor' identity is more predictive of blood donation than a general 'helpful person' identity.

## Statement

An organization's internal brand orientation is a distinct and measurable construct that acts as an antecedent to market performance.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports modeling a nonprofit's brand orientation as a driver of its brand equity within the BEBA framework.

## Notes

This connects an NPO's internal strategic posture to its external stakeholder-related outcomes.

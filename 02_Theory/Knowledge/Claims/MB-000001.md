## Statement

Internal resistance to nonprofit brand management arises when branding and marketing are perceived as commercial techniques that divert resources from mission-related programs.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim qualifies BEBA by identifying internal legitimacy constraints on the use of brand architecture in nonprofit organizations.

## Notes

The reviewed material describes this as a recurring tension inside nonprofit organizations.

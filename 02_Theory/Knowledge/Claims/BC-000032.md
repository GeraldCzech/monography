## Statement

Supporter commitment can be developed via an experiential-learning pathway initiated by behavioral involvement in a charity's activities.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim formalizes the 'behaviour before brands' principle, providing a testable model for how participation creates brand-aligned attitudes and commitment.

## Notes

This describes the `Involvement -> Perceived Sense of Community -> Identification` mechanism from Hassay & Peloza (2009).

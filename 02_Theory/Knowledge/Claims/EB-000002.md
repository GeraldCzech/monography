## Statement

Nonprofit organizations are perceived as well suited to experiential and emotional branding because their missions and values can generate stakeholder emotional resonance.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim extends BEBA by linking nonprofit mission and values to stakeholder experience as a route to behavioural brand effectiveness.

## Notes

The reviewed material presents this as an argument and practitioner perception rather than as a directly tested causal effect.

## Statement

Strong existing brand associations make radical brand repositioning less effective than incremental repositioning.

## Implications for BEBA

This implies that nonprofit brand change may need to work with existing stakeholder associations rather than attempt abrupt replacement.

## Notes

The reviewed material identifies repositioning difficulty but does not provide validated mechanisms.

## Statement

Measurements of brand strength and brand value can provide feedback for adjusting brand description and marketing mix decisions.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports modelling nonprofit brand management as a feedback system linking stakeholder states, behaviour, and organisational learning.

## Notes

The claim is based on Wood’s proposed Brand Management Loop.

## Statement

Nonprofit brand personality has a positive effect on an individual donor's self-concept.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the idea that brand personality can operate as an identity-enabling component of nonprofit brand architecture.

## Notes

Evidence is based on cross-sectional survey data from China.

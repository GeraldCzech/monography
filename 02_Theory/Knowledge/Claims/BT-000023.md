## Statement

Brand trust is a partial mediator of the relationship between overall satisfaction and brand loyalty.

## Implications for BEBA

This implies BEBA should model both direct behavioural reinforcement from positive experiences and the more complex, trust-mediated pathway to sustained support.

## Notes

This finding challenges a strict interpretation of commitment-trust theory that would posit full mediation, as a direct path from satisfaction to loyalty remains significant.

## Statement

The act of volunteering initiates a behavioral feedback loop where role enactment strengthens volunteer role identity, which in turn increases the likelihood of future volunteering behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides empirical grounding for the 'architecture' part of BEBA, which is designed to deliberately foster this positive feedback loop.

## Notes

This claim describes a dynamic, longitudinal process that underpins behavioral loyalty.

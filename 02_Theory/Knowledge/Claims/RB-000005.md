## Statement

For recurring pro-social behaviours, the primary behavioural driver shifts from conscious intention to an incorporated, pre-reflexive routine over time.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA requires separate architectural pathways for behaviour acquisition (intention-driven) and behaviour retention (habit-driven).

## Notes

This is particularly relevant for understanding sustained engagement like regular giving or volunteering.

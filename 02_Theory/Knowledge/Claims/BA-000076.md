## Statement

The impact of a nonprofit's sociopolitical advocacy on its brand equity is contingent on stakeholders' perception of the action's authenticity.

## Implications for BEBA

This indicates BEBA must incorporate contextual moderators and stakeholder perception filters to model the effects of brand actions.

## Notes

The moderating perception distinguishes between positive outcomes (viewed as 'brand bravery') and negative outcomes (viewed as hypocrisy).

## Statement

A brand can be perceived as authentic without being the pioneer or first entrant in its category.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim allows BEBA to distinguish category leadership through exemplarity from historical first-mover status.

## Notes

The paper distinguishes perceived quintessential exemplarity from pioneer status.

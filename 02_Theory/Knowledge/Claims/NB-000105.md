## Statement

The relative importance and structure of brand equity dimensions are contingent on cultural context.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim challenges BEBA to define the cultural boundary conditions of its proposed architecture and test for measurement invariance across national contexts.

## Notes

While research is nascent, initial replication studies suggest that cultural adaptation of models is required.

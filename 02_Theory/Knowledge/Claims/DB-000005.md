## Statement

In the NGO context, brand commitment has a significant positive effect on donor-based brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports positioning commitment as a behavioural architecture component linking brand relationships to supportive donor intentions.

## Notes

Evidence is based on a PLS-SEM study using a Spanish convenience sample focused on childhood-related NGOs.

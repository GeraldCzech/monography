## Statement

A high level of perceived trustworthiness is a characteristic of a successful NPO.

## Implications for BEBA

This directly supports a core BEBA thesis that brand-related perceptions like trust are central to NPO effectiveness.

## Notes

Claim is from Novotný & Lukeš (2008), cited by Hommerová & Severová (2018).

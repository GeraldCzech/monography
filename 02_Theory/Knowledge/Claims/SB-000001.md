## Statement

The relative importance of nonprofit brand dimensions differs between monetary donation and volunteering.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports behaviour-specific modelling within BEBA rather than assuming one generic brand-to-support pathway.

## Notes

The reviewed material indicates that brand image may be more influential for monetary donations, whereas brand typicality may be more relevant for volunteering.

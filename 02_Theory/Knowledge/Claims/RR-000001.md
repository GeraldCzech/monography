## Statement

Nonprofit organizations can substitute large-scale paid communication with internal culture, mission strength, employee passion, public relations, and low-cost communication practices in brand-building efforts.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the BEBA idea that brand-building capacity may be embedded in organisational practices rather than dependent only on financial resources.

## Notes

The reviewed material indicates this mechanism but does not provide a formal typology or effectiveness test of low-cost branding practices.

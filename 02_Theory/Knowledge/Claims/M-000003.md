## Statement

The lack of consistent operationalization of Bourdieusian concepts, particularly cultural and social capital, is a primary barrier to accumulating robust, generalizable empirical evidence on their role in charitable giving.

## Implications for BEBA

Any attempt to integrate Bourdieusian concepts into BEBA empirically requires creating and validating novel, rigorous operationalizations.

## Notes

This is a meta-claim about the state of research rather than a direct claim about the phenomenon of philanthropy.

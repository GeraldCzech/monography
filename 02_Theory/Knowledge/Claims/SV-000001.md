## Statement

Marketing functions extend beyond purely commercial goals to include the promotion of social value and the resolution of societal problems.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes the legitimacy of applying marketing and brand theory to nonprofit and social missions, a foundational premise for BEBA.

## Notes

Derived from the consensus that marketing's role is not limited to commerce.

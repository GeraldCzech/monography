## Statement

The effect of sponsor-nonprofit fit on nonprofit brand equity is mediated by cognitive consistency.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a specific psychological mechanism for how brand architecture coherence influences individual perception and evaluation.

## Notes

Low-fit partnerships violate expectations, creating dissonance that leads to negative attributions regarding the nonprofit's motives.

## Statement

The relative importance of a nonprofit's symbolic capital (brand) in driving stakeholder support, as compared to its reported programmatic outcomes, increases under conditions of high field-level uncertainty.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim directly supports BEBA's core research question by positioning the brand (as symbolic capital) as a primary mechanism for enabling behaviour under uncertainty.

## Notes

This provides a clear, testable hypothesis for model competition against performance-based models.

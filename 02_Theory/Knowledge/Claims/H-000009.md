## Statement

Strict social reproduction models of habitus are insufficient to fully explain philanthropic behaviour, as the acquisition of cultural capital can also facilitate social mobility and challenge established field structures.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests BEBA's model should not treat donor dispositions as static, as they can change through the acquisition of capital.

## Notes

The evidence for this claim comes from adjacent fields like education and social mobility, not directly from philanthropy research.

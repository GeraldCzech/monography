## Statement

There is a significant bias in philanthropy research towards incorrectly reporting non-significant findings as significant.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA must treat many published "significant" findings with skepticism and prioritize evidence from pre-registered studies.

## Notes

This points towards a strong publication bias for positive results in the field.

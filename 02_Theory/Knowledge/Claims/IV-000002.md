## Statement

The claim that donating to a single, identifiable victim is more effective than donating to statistical victims is not supported by recent field experiments and registered reports.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should not rely on the identifiable victim effect as a core mechanism and should question theories built upon it.

## Notes

This is a highly-cited finding that does not hold up well under replication.

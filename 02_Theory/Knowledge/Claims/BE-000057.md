## Statement

The positive effect of Brand Identity on Brand Resonance is fully mediated by the intermediate stages of Brand Meaning and Brand Responses in the nonprofit health-promotion context.

## Implications for BEBA

This highlights the necessity of intermediate cognitive and affective stages; brand awareness alone is insufficient to build the resonance that drives behaviour.

## Notes

The study found a negative direct path from Identity to Resonance (β = -0.143) alongside strong positive indirect paths, suggesting a statistical suppression effect where the mediators are essential.

## Statement

A supporter's level of identification with a charity is positively associated with their level of behavioral involvement in that charity's activities.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes a direct link between an internal cognitive state (Identification) and a desired action (Involvement), supporting a key cognitive pathway in BEBA.

## Notes

This is based on Proposition 1 (P1) from the Hassay & Peloza (2009) conceptual model.

## Statement

The brand image of a nonprofit organization is a multidimensional construct composed of Usefulness, Efficiency, Affect, and Dynamism.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a structural blueprint for the cognitive and affective components of a nonprofit's BEBA.

## Notes

The scale was developed and validated in a French context using large, well-known humanitarian aid charities.

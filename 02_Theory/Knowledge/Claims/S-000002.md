## Statement

Customer satisfaction partially mediates the relationship between brand equity and behavioral outcomes such as repurchase or willingness to pay.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should distinguish between immediate brand-enabled intention and sustained behaviour mediated by stakeholder satisfaction.

## Notes

The reviewed synthesis identifies satisfaction as a commonly observed partial mediator.

## Statement

The five-factor brand personality structure developed for commercial brands is not directly applicable to the nonprofit sector.

## Implications for BEBA

This reinforces the BEBA principle that behaviour architectures are context-specific and cannot be naively transferred between sectors.

## Notes

The study found that Aaker's dimensions of Sincerity, Excitement, and Competence did not emerge as distinct factors for nonprofits.

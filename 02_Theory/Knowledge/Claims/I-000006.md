## Statement

The strength of an intention moderates its likelihood of being translated into behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

While BEBA focuses on post-intentional factors, it must still account for the initial strength of brand-generated intent as a boundary condition.

## Notes

Stronger intentions are more likely to be enacted, but the relationship is not deterministic.

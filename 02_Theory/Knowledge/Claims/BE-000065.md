## Statement

A Bayesian evidence aggregation framework provides a more robust and scientifically sound basis for cumulative theory building in complex social science domains than a methodology based on discrete null-hypothesis significance tests.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim justifies BEBA's cumulative, probabilistic approach to knowledge integration over traditional NHST.

## Notes

This claim establishes the epistemological superiority of the chosen approach for the BEBA research context.

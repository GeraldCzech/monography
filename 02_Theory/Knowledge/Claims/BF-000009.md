## Statement

Brand familiarity can have non-significant or negative effects on nonprofit brand equity when familiarity is associated with negative knowledge of the organization.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should treat familiarity as an uncertainty amplifier as well as an uncertainty reducer depending on valence.

## Notes

The negative interpretation is attributed to Romero and Abril’s synthesis of inconsistent findings.

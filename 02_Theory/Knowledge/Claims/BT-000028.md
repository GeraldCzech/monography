## Statement

An increase in a donor's perception of a nonprofit's financial and operational transparency is positively associated with their trust in the organization.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies transparency as a functional component of the brand architecture designed to reduce donor uncertainty.

## Notes

This relies on donor perceptions of transparency, which may or may not correlate with objective transparency metrics.

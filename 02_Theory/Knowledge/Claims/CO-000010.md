## Statement

Perceived deviation of a nonprofit brand from the 'Good Samaritan' normative ideal is associated with decreased public commitment.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim directly connects the brand architecture's alignment with public ideals to behavioural enablement or disablement.

## Notes

The study identifies strong negative sentiments like disillusionment and animosity, which are precursors to decreased commitment, rather than measuring commitment directly.

## Statement

Impure altruism models charitable giving as motivated by both concern for charitable output and private utility from one’s own act of giving.

## Evidence

Andreoni (1989, 1990); Konow (2006, 2010)

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should not model charitable giving as purely brand-mediated or purely altruistic; donor motivation may include private affective reward.

## Notes

Parent theoretical framework for warm glow giving.

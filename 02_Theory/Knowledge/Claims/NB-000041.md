## Statement

Nonprofit brand awareness has a direct positive effect on an individual's giving intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the BEBA view that cognitive availability can make nonprofit brands behaviourally effective.

## Notes

Evidence is based on cross-sectional survey data from China and concerns giving intention rather than observed giving behaviour.

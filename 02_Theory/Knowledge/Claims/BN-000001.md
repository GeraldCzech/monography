## Statement

Brand name significantly influences consumer preference as a distinct attribute in multi-attribute choice contexts.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports the idea that the brand name can carry behavioural meaning and operate as a decision heuristic.

## Notes

The effect was especially salient in the low-risk product category examined in the study.

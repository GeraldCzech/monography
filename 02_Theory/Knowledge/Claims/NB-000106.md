## Statement

Higher levels of stakeholder-based brand equity are positively associated with supportive behavioral intentions.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim establishes the fundamental link between brand perceptions and behavioral precursors that BEBA seeks to explain.

## Notes

The reviewed literature primarily measures behavioral intentions rather than actual, observed behaviors.

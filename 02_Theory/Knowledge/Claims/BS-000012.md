## Statement

For nonprofit brands in international aid, prominence contributes more strongly to salience for larger market-leading organizations, whereas distinctiveness contributes relatively more strongly to salience for smaller organizations.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim extends BEBA by distinguishing different salience-building routes for nonprofit brands depending on market position.

## Notes

The claim is bounded to the charity set and category context examined in the study.

## Statement

Consistency of a firm’s marketing mix over time is a stronger driver of brand signal credibility than brand investments or signal clarity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim prioritises consistency over spending or visibility as a credibility-building mechanism for nonprofit brands.

## Notes

The reviewed material reports this as an empirical finding.

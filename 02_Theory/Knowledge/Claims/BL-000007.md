## Statement

Brand loyalty is a behavioural consequence of consumer-based brand equity rather than a constituent component of consumer-based brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim cautions BEBA against defining supportive repeat behaviour as part of nonprofit brand equity rather than as an outcome of it.

## Notes

The reviewed material frames this as a disagreement with dominant cognitive psychology models.

## Statement

An increase in the salience of a specific "donor" identity is positively associated with an individual's intention to perform the corresponding donation behavior.

## Implications for BEBA

The claim establishes identity salience as a direct antecedent of behavioral intention, making it a key construct for the BEBA model.

## Notes

The effect is noted to be stronger for specific, role-based identities than for general prosocial identities.

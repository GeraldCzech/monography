## Statement

Brand Resonance with a nonprofit health-promotion brand positively predicts stakeholders' behavioural intentions to adopt the promoted behaviour.

## Implications for BEBA

This provides direct evidence for the terminal link in the BEBA model, where the brand architecture culminates in a measurable effect on behavioural intentions.

## Notes

The study reported a standardized path coefficient of β = 0.315 (p < .001); the outcome was self-reported behavioural intention, not observed behaviour.

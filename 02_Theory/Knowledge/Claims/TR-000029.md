## Statement

Trust in a nonprofit organization positively influences donation intention by reducing perceived uncertainty.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim validates modeling the brand as an architecture for generating trust to overcome the credence good problem.

## Notes

The function of trust is primarily as a heuristic for managing the credence good nature of nonprofit impact.

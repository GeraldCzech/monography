## Statement

Nonprofit brand image has a direct positive effect on an individual's giving intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim indicates that nonprofit brand image can function as a direct behavioural cue in donor decision-making.

## Notes

Evidence is based on cross-sectional survey data from China and concerns giving intention rather than observed giving behaviour.

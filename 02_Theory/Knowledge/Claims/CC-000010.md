## Statement

Empirical studies frequently lack consistent and valid operationalizations of key Bourdieusian concepts like cultural and social capital.

## Implications for BEBA

This is a critical warning that BEBA must develop canonical definitions and measures for these concepts to be empirically tractable.

## Notes

The summary flags this as a major hindrance to cumulative empirical progress in the field.

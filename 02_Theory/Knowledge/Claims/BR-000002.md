## Statement

The perceived respectability of a nonprofit brand is positively associated with resource provider support intentions.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim suggests that respect may function as a relational quality enabling support behaviour under uncertainty.

## Notes

Based on one exploratory empirical study of a single nonprofit organisation.

## Statement

Social-image motivation can influence the amount donated, conditional on the decision to give.

## Evidence

Grossman and Levy (2024) distinguish self-image and social-image mechanisms and associate social image with donation amount. Harbaugh (1998) is also cited in relation to social prestige and public giving.

## Implications for BEBA

BEBA should distinguish between mechanisms that trigger initial participation and mechanisms that amplify behavioural intensity. Public recognition, community visibility, or donor-status cues may increase donation magnitude when ethically and contextually appropriate.

## Statement

Social-level brand engagement involves interpreting the brand in an interpersonal, communal, or socio-cultural context.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s treatment of nonprofit brands as potential architectures for collective action, not only individual preference.

## Notes

The statement derives from Schmitt’s definition of the social engagement level.

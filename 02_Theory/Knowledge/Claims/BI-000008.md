## Statement

Brand equity can exert a direct positive effect on behavioral intention beyond its indirect effect through attitude.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that BEBA should allow both mediated and direct pathways from brand equity to behavioural intention.

## Notes

The reviewed material notes variability in whether direct paths are modelled or statistically significant.

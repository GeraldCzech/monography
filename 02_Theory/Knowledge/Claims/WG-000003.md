## Statement

Incomplete crowd-out of private donations by government or third-party provision provides empirical support for the existence of warm-glow motivation.

## Evidence

Andreoni (1990); Wilhelm et al. (2014); Konow (2010)

## Implications for BEBA

Donor behaviour cannot be fully explained by outcome-oriented altruism; BEBA must allow for act-based utility.

## Notes

Incomplete crowd-out is one of the strongest empirical anchors for the construct.

## Statement

In new donor acquisition for international aid charities, nonprofit brand salience has a direct positive effect on brand choice intention independent of brand attitude.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA by showing that nonprofit brands can enable behaviour through heuristic memory accessibility, not only through evaluation.

## Notes

The evidence is based on scenario-based choice intention.

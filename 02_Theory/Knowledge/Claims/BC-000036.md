## Statement

Brand credibility does not significantly moderate the positive relationship between brand awareness and brand equity.

## Implications for BEBA

This specifies the limits of Brand Credibility's moderating role, suggesting it interacts more with evaluative than with basic recognition components.

## Notes

This is a null finding for the moderation effect, which is important for accurate model specification.

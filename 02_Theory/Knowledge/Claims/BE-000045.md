## Statement

Brand equity is used in the literature to denote at least three distinct constructs: brand value, brand strength, and brand description.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s need for strict construct separation and canonical definitions.

## Notes

The claim concerns conceptual usage rather than empirical effects.

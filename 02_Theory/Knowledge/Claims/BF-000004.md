## Statement

Familiarity with a nonprofit brand is negatively associated with resource provider support intentions in Faircloth's exploratory study.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim challenges simple awareness-based accounts by suggesting that familiarity can reduce support when associated perceptions are unfavourable.

## Notes

The author interprets the result as potentially reflecting negative perceptions of the specific organisation studied.

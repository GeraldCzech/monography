## Statement

Brand equity models developed for consumer goods markets may not apply directly to B2B markets because factors such as price and delivery can outweigh brand considerations.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim reinforces BEBA's assumption that behavioural effectiveness depends on context-specific decision conditions.

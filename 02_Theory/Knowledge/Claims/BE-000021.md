## Statement

The connection between digital signals such as search volume or social media sentiment and theory-driven brand equity dimensions remains insufficiently validated.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim warns BEBA not to treat digital indicators as valid behavioural-equity measures without theoretical and empirical validation.

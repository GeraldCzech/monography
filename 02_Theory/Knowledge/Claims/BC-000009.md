## Statement

Resource providers are more inclined to support a nonprofit when the nonprofit brand personality matches their self-concept.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides a candidate psychological mechanism through which brand meaning can enable support behaviour.

## Notes

Presented as a proposed mechanism in the reviewed material rather than as a directly tested effect in Faircloth's study.

## Statement

In the nonprofit sector, stakeholder perceptions of brand respect are positively associated with intentions to provide resource support.

## Implications for BEBA

This suggests that respect is a behaviourally relevant brand perception within nonprofit brand-enabled behaviour architecture.

## Notes

Evidence is based on one exploratory empirical study.

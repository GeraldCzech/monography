## Statement

The Theory of Planned Behavior's core constructs (attitude, subjective norm, perceived behavioral control) are strong predictors of charitable donation intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This establishes the dominant intention-formation model that BEBA must engage with and extend.

## Notes

The meta-analysis by White et al. (2023) confirms strong associations across multiple donation contexts.

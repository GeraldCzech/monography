## Statement

An increase in a donor's perception of a nonprofit brand's integrity leads to a positive increase in their trust in that brand.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim validates that perceived integrity is a key signal within the brand architecture for building trust.

## Notes

The claim concerns perceived integrity, which is a subjective assessment by the donor, not necessarily objective organizational conduct.

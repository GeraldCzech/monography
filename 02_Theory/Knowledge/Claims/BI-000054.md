## Statement

A nonprofit brand image characterized by high social standing and perceived popularity is positively associated with the perceived descriptive social norm of support.

## Implications for BEBA

Strengthens the link between the brand image construct and the social norms component of the BEBA architecture.

## Notes

A methodological challenge exists in distinguishing a causal effect from semantic overlap between the constructs, requiring precise operationalization in BEBA.

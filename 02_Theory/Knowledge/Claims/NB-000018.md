## Statement

Attitude toward the nonprofit organization is not consistently significant as a predictor of charitable donation intention when other behavioural predictors are included.

## Implications for BEBA

This challenges simple brand-liking explanations and supports BEBA’s focus on brands enabling behavioural conditions rather than directly causing donations.

## Notes

The reviewed literature reports non-significant findings in some studies and direct modelling of brand image in another.

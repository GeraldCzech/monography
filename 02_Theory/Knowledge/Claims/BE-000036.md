## Statement

Consumer engagement with brands can be differentiated into object-centered, self-centered, and social levels of psychological engagement.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim provides BEBA with a layered psychological structure for explaining different depths of stakeholder engagement with nonprofit brands.

## Notes

The claim reflects Schmitt’s integrative theoretical framework and requires empirical validation of the distinctiveness of the three levels.

## Statement

Consumer-based brand equity, as measured by the Multidimensional Brand Equity (MBE) scale, is a significant positive correlate of both brand attitude and purchase intention.

## Implications for BEBA

This empirically validates the link between brand equity and behavioural intentions, a central pathway in the BEBA framework.

## Notes

This finding is part of the scale's nomological validation, establishing its predictive power for key marketing outcomes.

## Statement

Accounting and marketing use the term added value to refer to different phenomena.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim warns BEBA to distinguish stakeholder-perceived benefits from organisational value outcomes.

## Notes

The reviewed material identifies this as a central terminology conflict.

## Statement

Short-term financial metrics are insufficient for managing brand equity because they do not directly capture customer-based brand assets.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim supports BEBA’s emphasis on measuring behaviour-enabling brand assets rather than relying only on financial or fundraising outcomes.

## Notes

The claim is theoretical and managerial in orientation.

## Statement

Perception of a nonprofit brand as a "Marketer" archetype is negatively associated with public trust in that brand.

## Implications for BEBA

This identifies a specific brand perception ('Marketer') as a significant barrier to trust within the BEBA framework, representing an architectural failure state.

## Notes

The study suggests this form of distrust is profound, stemming from a perceived betrayal of the "true spirit" of charity.

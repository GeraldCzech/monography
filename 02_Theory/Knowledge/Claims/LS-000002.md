## Statement

Senior marketing personnel in UK nonprofit organizations identify leadership support as one of the most critical internal factors for successful nonprofit branding.

## Implications for BEBA

This claim positions leadership support as a core organizational precondition for brand-enabled behavioural effectiveness.

## Notes

The claim concerns practitioner perceptions in an exploratory qualitative sample, not a statistically validated causal effect.

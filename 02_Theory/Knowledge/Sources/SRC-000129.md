  greater emphasis on factors internal to the organisation, paying greater attention
  to the role of employees in the brand building process. This paper explores the
  implications of corporate branding for the management of internal brand resources.
  We describe a model for managing brands through narrowing the gap between a brand’s
  identity and its reputation and, building on this, identify three key factors that
